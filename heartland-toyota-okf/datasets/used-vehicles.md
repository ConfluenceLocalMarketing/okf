# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Heartland Toyota

Heartland Toyota maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (199+ vehicles listed on CARFAX)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Toyota, Chevrolet, Ford, Jeep, Ram, Subaru, Tesla, Volkswagen, Volvo, Hyundai, Kia, Lexus, Lincoln, Mazda, Porsche, and many more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $12,000 (bargain inventory) to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, hybrids, and EVs
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover

## Quality Assurance

- **Toyota Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed 160-point inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance
- **Vehicle History Reports** - Available on request
- **Safety inspections** on all pre-owned vehicles

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Toyota Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous 160-point inspection and include:
- Toyota factory-backed limited warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified Toyota technicians
- Complimentary CARFAX Vehicle History Report
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- KBB Instant Cash Offer trade-in tool
- Car payment calculator
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- SmartPath online purchasing
- Click-N-Drive home delivery
- Vehicle hold policy

## Special Programs

- **Bargain Inventory** - Vehicles under $12,000
- **Economy Vehicle Program** - Safety-checked, value-priced pre-owned options for first-time buyers and budget-conscious customers

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal via KBB Instant Cash Offer
- Competitive trade-in values
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
