# Frequently Asked Questions - Heartland Toyota

## Inventory

**Q: What new Toyota models are available at Heartland Toyota?**
A: The full Toyota lineup including Camry, Corolla, RAV4, RAV4 Hybrid, RAV4 Plug-In Hybrid, Tacoma, Tundra, Highlander, Grand Highlander, 4Runner, Sienna, Prius, bZ4X, Corolla Cross, Crown Signia, Sequoia, Land Cruiser, GR Supra, and C-HR.

**Q: What brands are available in the used vehicle inventory?**
A: The pre-owned inventory spans Toyota, Chevrolet, Ford, Jeep, Ram, Subaru, Tesla, Volkswagen, Volvo, Hyundai, Kia, Lexus, Lincoln, Mazda, Porsche, and many more.

**Q: Does Heartland Toyota offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, Toyota Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are available with factory-backed 160-point inspections, warranties, and 24/7 roadside assistance.

**Q: Do you have vehicles under $12,000?**
A: Yes, Heartland Toyota offers a Bargain Inventory of vehicles under $12,000 and an Economy Vehicle Program with safety-checked, value-priced options.

## Financing

**Q: What financing options are available?**
A: Traditional loans, leases, and special financing programs. The dealership offers SmartPath online purchasing with transparent pricing.

**Q: Does the dealership work with all credit profiles?**
A: Yes, the finance team works with multiple lending partners to accommodate various credit situations and budgets.

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes, online credit application and pre-approval are available through the dealership website.

## Service

**Q: Can the service center work on non-Toyota vehicles?**
A: Yes, the service center can perform maintenance and repairs on various makes and models.

**Q: Does the dealership offer Toyota Express Maintenance?**
A: Yes, Toyota Express Maintenance provides quick in-and-out service (oil changes, tire rotations) without an appointment.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through the dealership website.

**Q: Does the service center use genuine Toyota parts?**
A: Yes, the service center uses genuine Toyota OEM parts for all repairs and maintenance.
