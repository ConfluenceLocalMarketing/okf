# New Toyota Models - Heartland Toyota

Heartland Toyota carries a full lineup of new Toyota vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Toyota RAV4
Compact SUV, Toyota's best-selling model in the US. Available in gas, Hybrid, and Plug-In Hybrid variants. Multiple trims including LE, XLE, XLE Premium, Adventure, TRD Off-Road, Limited, and Woodland Edition. Seats 5 passengers. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Toyota RAV4 Hybrid / RAV4 Plug-In Hybrid
Hybrid and plug-in hybrid variants of the RAV4 offering exceptional fuel economy. The RAV4 Prime (Plug-In) offers an estimated 42 miles of EV-only range.

### Toyota Highlander / Highlander Hybrid
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in L, LE, XLE, Limited, and Platinum trims. Hybrid variant available for enhanced fuel efficiency.

### Toyota Grand Highlander / Grand Highlander Hybrid
Large 3-row SUV with more interior space than Highlander. Available in gas and hybrid variants with multiple trims.

### Toyota 4Runner / 4Runner i-FORCE MAX
Mid-size off-road SUV with legendary capability. Available in SR5, TRD Sport, TRD Off-Road, TRD Pro, Limited, and Platinum trims. New 2026 generation with available i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain.

### Toyota Corolla Cross / Corolla Cross Hybrid
Subcompact crossover SUV based on the Corolla platform. Available in L, LE, XLE, and SE trims. Hybrid variant available.

### Toyota Crown Signia
Premium crossover wagon replacing the Toyota Crown. Available in XLE and Limited trims. Standard hybrid powertrain.

### Toyota bZ4X
All-electric compact SUV. Available in XLE and Limited trims. Available in FWD and AWD. Estimated range up to 252 miles.

### Toyota Land Cruiser
Full-size off-road luxury SUV. New 2027 generation available with hybrid powertrain.

### Toyota Sequoia
Full-size hybrid SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in SR5, Limited, Platinum, TRD Pro, and Capstone trims. Standard i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain. Maximum towing capacity up to 9,520 lbs.

### Toyota C-HR
Subcompact crossover with distinctive styling. Available in multiple trims.

## Cars

### Toyota Camry / Camry Hybrid
Mid-size sedan, America's best-selling car. Available in LE, SE, XLE, XSE, and TRD trims. Standard hybrid powertrain on 2025+ models. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Toyota Corolla / Corolla Hybrid
Compact sedan known for reliability and value. Available in L, LE, SE, XSE, and Nightshade trims. Hybrid variant available.

### Toyota Prius / Prius Plug-In Hybrid
Iconic hybrid hatchback with striking new design. Available in LE, XLE, and Limited trims. Plug-In Hybrid (Prime) offers extended EV-only range.

### Toyota GR Supra
High-performance sports coupe. Available in 2.0 and 3.0 trims, including the track-focused GR Supra A91-MT Edition with manual transmission.

## Trucks

### Toyota Tacoma / Tacoma i-FORCE MAX
Mid-size pickup truck, America's best-selling mid-size pickup. Available in SR, SR5, TRD Sport, TRD Off-Road, Limited, TRD Pro, and Trailhunter trims. New generation with available i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain. Maximum towing capacity up to 6,500 lbs.

### Toyota Tundra / Tundra i-FORCE MAX
Full-size pickup truck with powerful twin-turbo V6. Available in SR, SR5, Limited, Platinum, 1794 Edition, TRD Pro, and Capstone trims. Standard i-FORCE (twin-turbo V6) or available i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain. Maximum towing capacity up to 12,000 lbs.

## Vans

### Toyota Sienna
Hybrid minivan seating up to 8 passengers. Available in LE, XLE, XSE, Limited, Platinum, and Woodland Edition trims. Standard hybrid powertrain with available AWD.

Promotions vary by model and include low APR financing, lease specials, and rebates. Current offers include military and college grad rebates.
