## Warranty

All new Toyota models include:
- **3-Year / 36,000-Mile Basic Limited Warranty**
- **5-Year / 60,000-Mile Powertrain Limited Warranty**
- **5-Year / Unlimited-Mile Corrosion Perforation Limited Warranty**
- **3-Year / 36,000-Mile 24/7 Roadside Assistance**
- **2-Year / 25,000-Mile Scheduled Maintenance (ToyotaCare)**

## Toyota Safety Sense

Standard or available across most Toyota models:
- Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection
- Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist
- Automatic High Beams
- Dynamic Radar Cruise Control
- Lane Tracing Assist
- Road Sign Assist
- Blind Spot Monitor (available)
- Rear Cross-Traffic Alert (available)

## ToyotaCare

Complimentary scheduled maintenance for 2 years / 25,000 miles, plus 24/7 roadside assistance for 3 years / 36,000 miles on all new Toyotas.
