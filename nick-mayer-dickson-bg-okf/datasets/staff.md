# Dealership Staff

Specific staff names are not available at this time. The dealership operates through the following departments:

## Sales Department
Available during business hours to assist with new Buick and GMC sales, pre-owned vehicles, test drives, and vehicle delivery.

## Finance Department
Handles financing, leasing, extended warranties, and aftermarket products. Online credit applications are available on the dealership website.

## Service Department
Staffed by factory-trained technicians performing maintenance, diagnostics, and repairs. See [service-center.md](service-center.md) for full details.

## Parts Department
Stocks genuine OEM Buick and GMC parts, accessories, and maintenance items.

## Management
General management oversees all dealership operations including sales, service, parts, and customer relations.

Contact the dealership at 615-639-0282 to reach any department.
