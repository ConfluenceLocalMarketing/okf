# Financing & Leasing

The Leif Johnson Ford of Buda Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for new and pre-owned vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home via the [Finance Application](https://www.leifjohnsonfordofbuda.com/finance-application/)
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Get an instant online valuation through [Black Book Trade-In](https://www.leifjohnsonfordofbuda.com/black-book-trade-in/) or the [Black Book Credit Estimator](https://www.leifjohnsonfordofbuda.com/black-book-credit-estimator/).

## Payment Calculators

Estimate monthly payments using the [Payment Calculators](https://www.leifjohnsonfordofbuda.com/payment-calculators/) before visiting the dealership.
