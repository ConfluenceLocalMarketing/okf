## Ford Blue Advantage Program

The dealership participates in the [Ford Blue Advantage](https://www.leifjohnsonfordofbuda.com/fordblueadvantage/) certified pre-owned program, which provides:

- **Gold Certified**: Up to 7-year/100,000-mile limited warranty, 172-point inspection, 24/7 roadside assistance, rental car coverage
- **Blue Certified**: Up to 3-month/4,000-mile limited warranty, 139-point inspection, 24/7 roadside assistance
