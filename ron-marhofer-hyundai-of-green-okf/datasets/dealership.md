# Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Green - Dealership Profile

## About

Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Green is a full-service Hyundai dealership located at 3360 S. Arlington Rd, Akron, OH 44312. The dealership serves customers from Uniontown, Toledo, North Canton, Cleveland, and everywhere in between with new Hyundai inventory, quality used cars, certified service, genuine parts, and competitive financing.

The dealership is Ohio's number one volume Hyundai dealer and offers free home delivery up to 150 miles.
