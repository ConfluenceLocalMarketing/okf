# Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Green - Used Vehicles

Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Green offers a wide selection of quality pre-owned vehicles, including used Hyundai models and vehicles from other reputable automakers. All used vehicles are carefully inspected and available with competitive financing options.

## Inventory Overview

- Large selection of pre-owned cars, SUVs, and trucks
- Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) vehicles available
- Multi-point inspected vehicles
- CARFAX-certified history reports available
- Competitive pricing and financing options

## Benefits of Buying Used

- Lower price point compared to new vehicles
- Certified Pre-Owned Hyundai vehicles include remaining factory warranty
- Wide variety of makes, models, and years
- Online inventory with photos and vehicle history
- Test drives available by appointment
- Trade-in accepted
