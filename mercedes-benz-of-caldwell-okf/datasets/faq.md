# Mercedes-Benz of Caldwell FAQ

## Vehicles

**Q: What Mercedes-Benz models do you carry?**
A: Full lineup including C-Class, E-Class, S-Class, GLC, GLE, GLS, G-Class, EQ electric vehicles, and AMG performance models. See [new-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/mercedes-benz-of-caldwell-okf/datasets/new-vehicles.md).

**Q: Do you have electric vehicles?**
A: Yes, the full EQ lineup including EQS, EQE, EQB, EQS SUV, and EQE SUV.

**Q: Do you offer AMG models?**
A: Yes, we carry Mercedes-AMG performance variants.

**Q: What is offered in the certified pre-owned program?**
A: Factory-backed CPO with inspections, extended warranty, and roadside assistance. See [used-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/mercedes-benz-of-caldwell-okf/datasets/used-vehicles.md).

## Financing

**Q: What financing rates do you offer?**
A: Competitive low-rate financing is available, for example 1.4% for 24 months on select new models. See [financing.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/mercedes-benz-of-caldwell-okf/datasets/financing.md).

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes, online pre-approval is available on our website.

**Q: Do you offer leases?**
A: Yes, we offer tailored lease options on all new Mercedes-Benz models.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes, use our online trade-in valuation tool.

## Service

**Q: What services does your center provide?**
A: Factory-trained service, genuine OEM parts, and a collision center. See [service-center.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/mercedes-benz-of-caldwell-okf/datasets/service-center.md).

**Q: Do you offer pickup and drop-off service?**
A: Yes, no-contact pickup and drop-off options are available.

**Q: What amenities are available for waiting customers?**
A: Complimentary WiFi, lounge seating, snacks, and TVs.

**Q: Do you have a collision center?**
A: Yes, we have a dedicated Mercedes-Benz collision center.
