# Mercedes-Benz of Caldwell Finance Center

The finance center at Mercedes-Benz of Caldwell provides tailored purchase and lease options for luxury vehicles, along with competitive low-rate financing.

## Financing Options

### New Vehicle Financing
- Low APR promotions (e.g., 1.4% for 24 months on select models)
- Competitive rates through Mercedes-Benz Financial Services
- Flexible terms

### Pre-Owned Financing
- Rates on certified pre-owned and pre-owned luxury vehicles
- CPO special financing rates
- Extended warranty options

### Online Pre-Approval
- Quick online application
- Transparent terms
- No impact on credit score for initial inquiry

## Leasing

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
