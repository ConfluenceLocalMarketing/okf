# Kruse Motors Auto Group

Kruse Motors is a multi-brand automotive dealership located in Marshall, MN, serving as a certified dealer for Ford, Lincoln, Buick, and GMC. The dealership provides new and used vehicle sales, factory-authorized service, genuine OEM parts, and automotive financing. Kruse Motors has been voted the best place to buy a new or used vehicle year-after-year and also voted best for service including the quick lube and car wash center. The dealership serves drivers throughout Marshall, Worthington, Redwood Falls, Brookings, Granite Falls, and surrounding Southwest Minnesota communities.
