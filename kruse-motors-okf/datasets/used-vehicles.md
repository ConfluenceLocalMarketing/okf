# Used Vehicle Inventory - Kruse Motors

Kruse Motors maintains a large inventory of pre-owned and certified pre-owned vehicles across multiple brands and body styles. Each vehicle is inspected and reconditioned before sale.

## Pre-Owned Inventory

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| Used Cars & Sedans | Multi-make selection including sedans, hatchbacks, and coupes |
| Used Trucks | Full-size and midsize pickups including Ford F-150, GMC Sierra, Ram 1500 |
| Used SUVs | Compact, midsize, and full-size SUVs including Ford Explorer, GMC Acadia, Buick Enclave |
| Affordable Vehicles | Inventory available under $15,000 and under $25,000 |

## Certified Pre-Owned

Factory-certified pre-owned vehicles that meet manufacturer quality standards, backed by comprehensive warranties. CPO vehicles are carefully inspected and include extended warranty coverage.

## Trade-In & Sell Programs

| Program | Description |
|---|---|
| KBB Instant Cash Offer | Get a fair-market offer from Kelley Blue Book with no obligation |
| Value Your Trade | Online trade-in estimate to apply toward next purchase |
| We Buy Cars | Direct vehicle purchase program with no car-buying obligation |

## Vehicle Protection

Kruse Motors offers vehicle protection plans including gap protection, powertrain plus coverage, complete choice protection, vehicle service contracts, and pre-owned vehicle coverage.

## Quality Process

Each used vehicle is meticulously inspected and reconditioned to high standards. The dealership is selective when choosing inventory and stands behind the condition of every pre-owned vehicle sold.
