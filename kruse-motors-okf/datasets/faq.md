# Frequently Asked Questions - Kruse Motors

## General

**What makes Kruse Motors different from other dealerships?**
Kruse Motors is a family-owned dealership serving Marshall and Southwest Minnesota since 1974, offering Ford, Lincoln, Buick, and GMC vehicles under one roof. The dealership focuses on transparent pricing, no-pressure sales, and long-term customer relationships.

**Where is Kruse Motors located?**
1651-61 East College Drive, Marshall, MN 56258.

**What are the showroom hours?**
Monday–Thursday 8:00 AM–7:00 PM, Friday 8:00 AM–6:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM–4:00 PM.

**What brands does Kruse Motors sell?**
New: Ford, Lincoln, Buick, and GMC. Used: All makes and models.

## Inventory

**How can I browse inventory?**
Visit the inventory page online or use the vehicle search tool on the website. Inventory can be filtered by new, used, certified pre-owned, brand, body style, and price range.

**Can I custom-order a vehicle?**
Yes, through the Kruse Your Way program. You can custom-order a Ford, Lincoln, Buick, or GMC vehicle with your chosen color, features, and accessories.

**Do you offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
Yes, Kruse Motors offers a selection of certified pre-owned vehicles that meet manufacturer quality standards and include comprehensive warranties.

## Financing

**Can I get pre-qualified without affecting my credit score?**
Yes. The pre-qualification process uses a soft credit check that does not require your Social Security Number or date of birth and has no impact on your credit score.

**Do you work with buyers who have challenged credit?**
Yes. The finance team works with a wide network of lenders and handles various credit situations.

**Is there a student discount program?**
Yes, the Ford Drives U program offers savings for students on new or used vehicles.

## Service

**What type of service does Kruse Motors offer?**
Routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations), advanced diagnostics, brake service, transmission repairs, electrical system support, and more. Service is available for Ford, Lincoln, Buick, and GMC vehicles at dedicated service centers.

**Do I need to buy my vehicle at Kruse to have it serviced there?**
No. The service department works on any vehicle, and customers who did not purchase at Kruse have reported excellent service experiences.

**Does Kruse offer free oil changes?**
Yes, Kruse Motors offers a Free Oil for Life program on vehicles purchased at the dealership.

## Trade-In & Selling

**How do I get a trade-in value?**
Use the Kelley Blue Book Trade-In Advisor Tool online for an instant, accurate estimate, or visit the dealership in person.

**Can I sell my car to Kruse without buying one?**
Yes. The We Buy Cars program allows you to sell your vehicle directly with no obligation to purchase.

**Does Kruse match KBB values?**
Kruse partners with Kelley Blue Book to provide transparent, fair-market offers through the KBB Instant Cash Offer program.
