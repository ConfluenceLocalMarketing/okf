# Service Center - Nick Mayer Ford

The Nick Mayer Ford service center in Mayfield Heights, OH is a Ford-certified facility staffed by ASE certified technicians. The center provides routine maintenance, diagnostic services, major repairs, and genuine Ford OEM parts.

## ASE Certified Technicians

All service technicians are ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified, ensuring professional-quality service on all Ford models. Ongoing training keeps the team current with the latest Ford technology, including hybrid and electric vehicle systems.

## Services Offered

### Routine Maintenance
- Oil and filter changes
- Tire rotation and balancing
- Brake inspections and service
- Fluid checks and replacements
- Battery testing and replacement
- Multi-point vehicle inspections

### Diagnostic Services
- Check engine light diagnostics
- Computer system analysis
- Electrical system troubleshooting
- Performance issue diagnosis

### Major Repairs
- Engine and transmission service
- HVAC system repair
- Exhaust system service
- Steering and suspension work
- Ford certified collision repair and body work

## Genuine Ford OEM Parts

The parts department stocks genuine Ford OEM parts including:

- Engine components
- Brake pads, rotors, and calipers
- Filters (oil, air, cabin, fuel)
- Belts and hoses
- Batteries
- Accessories and customizations
