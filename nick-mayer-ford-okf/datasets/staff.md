# Staff Directory - Nick Mayer Ford

Nick Mayer Ford in Mayfield Heights, OH employs a team of automotive professionals across sales, service, parts, and management departments. The service center is staffed by ASE certified technicians.

## Departments

### Management
The dealership management team oversees daily operations, sales performance, and customer satisfaction across all departments.

### Sales Department
The sales team guides customers through vehicle selection, test drives, and the purchasing process for both new and pre-owned vehicles.

### Finance & Insurance (F&I)
The finance team assists with loan and lease options, including second-chance financing and bankruptcy assistance. They work with multiple lenders to secure competitive rates.

### Service Department
The service department employs ASE certified technicians trained to service all Ford models. Services range from routine maintenance to complex diagnostics and major repairs.

### Parts Department
The parts team stocks genuine Ford OEM parts and accessories, available for purchase by customers and wholesale accounts.
