- Competitive pricing across all segments

## Ford Blue Advantage CPO Program

Ford Blue Advantage provides certified pre-owned coverage:

- **Gold Certified**: Vehicles up to 6 years old with under 80,000 miles. Includes 172-point inspection, comprehensive warranty, roadside assistance, and rental car coverage.
- **Blue Certified**: Vehicles up to 10 years old with under 120,000 miles. Includes 139-point inspection, powertrain warranty, and roadside assistance.
