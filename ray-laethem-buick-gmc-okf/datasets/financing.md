# Financing & Leasing

The Ray Laethem Buick GMC Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for new and pre-owned vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home via the online credit application
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new Buick or GMC vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Special Programs

- **Buick GMC Military Offer**: Special pricing and incentives for active military, veterans, and military families
- **First Responder Discount**: Exclusive savings for first responders including police, fire, and EMS personnel
- **Lease and Purchase Offers**: Current manufacturer incentives and dealership specials

Get an instant online valuation through the dealership's trade-in appraisal tool.
