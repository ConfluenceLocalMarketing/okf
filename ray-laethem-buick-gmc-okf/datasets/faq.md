# Frequently Asked Questions

## Vehicles & Inventory

**Q: What Buick models are available at Ray Laethem Buick GMC?**
A: The dealership carries the full Buick lineup including Encore GX, Envision, Envista, Enclave, and Encore.

**Q: What GMC models are available?**
A: The dealership carries the full GMC lineup including Sierra 1500, Sierra 2500 HD, Sierra EV, Canyon, Terrain, Acadia, Yukon, Yukon XL, and HUMMER EV.

**Q: Do you have used vehicles from other manufacturers?**
A: Yes. The pre-owned inventory spans multiple makes and models, not just Buick and GMC.

**Q: Can I see vehicle history reports for used vehicles?**
A: Yes. CarFax or similar vehicle history reports are available on pre-owned inventory.

## Financing

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes. Complete the online credit application from the dealership website.

**Q: What credit situations do you work with?**
A: The finance team partners with multiple lenders to accommodate various credit profiles.

**Q: Do you offer both buying and leasing options?**
A: Yes. The finance center provides both purchase financing and lease programs on new Buick and GMC vehicles.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes. Use the online trade-in appraisal tool for instant valuation.

## Service

**Q: Does the service department use OEM or aftermarket parts?**
A: The service department uses only genuine GM OEM parts for all repairs and maintenance.

**Q: Do you offer a mobile service program?**
A: Yes. The Test Drive To Go program includes vehicle pickup and delivery for service appointments.

**Q: Can I schedule service online?**
A: Yes. Use the online service appointment form on the website.

**Q: What is the Test Drive To Go program?**
A: A convenient service where the dealership brings a vehicle to your home or office for a test drive, and also offers pickup and delivery for service.

## Dealership

**Q: What are your sales hours?**
A: Monday through Saturday 9am–7pm, closed Sunday. Hours may vary by location.

**Q: Where is the dealership located?**
A: The main showroom is at 17677 Mack Ave, Detroit, MI 48224, with a service center at 22711 Van Dyke Ave, Warren, MI.

**Q: Is Ray Laethem Buick GMC family-owned?**
A: Yes. Founded in 1940, the dealership has been family-owned and operated for three generations.

**Q: What special programs do you offer?**
A: The dealership offers Buick GMC Military Offer, First Responder Discount, and various lease and purchase specials.
