# Ray Laethem Buick GMC

## Overview

Ray Laethem Buick GMC is a family-owned and operated dealership serving the greater Detroit metropolitan area. Founded in 1940, the dealership has been under family ownership for three generations, offering new and pre-owned Buick and GMC vehicles, factory-certified maintenance and repair, genuine OEM parts, and collision repair services.
