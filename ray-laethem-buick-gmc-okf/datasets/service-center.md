# Service Center

The Ray Laethem Buick GMC Service Department provides factory-certified maintenance and repair services for Buick and GMC vehicles.

## Services Offered

- Routine maintenance: oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, preventative maintenance
- Advanced diagnostics and repair: brake service, engine diagnostics, electrical service, transmission repairs
- GM-specific parts and accessories - genuine OEM components
- Tire sales and service
- Test Drive To Go - vehicle pickup and delivery for service appointments
- Collision repair center
- GM accessories installation
