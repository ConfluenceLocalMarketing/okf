# Services - Montclare Auto Repair

Montclare Auto Repair provides comprehensive automotive services for all makes and models. The shop is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and alignment machines.

Complete brake system care including inspection, pad replacement, rotor service, caliper repair, and brake fluid exchange.

## Suspension Services

Shock and strut replacement, steering system repair, and precision wheel alignment.

## General Maintenance

Oil changes, tune-ups, fluid services, and preventative maintenance programs.

Diagnostics, repair, and performance optimization for all vehicle types.

Climate control system diagnostics, A/C recharge, compressor replacement, and heater core service.

## Electrical Services

Battery testing and replacement, alternator repair, starter service, and electrical system diagnostics.

## Emissions Repair

Emissions testing, diagnostics, and repair to ensure compliance with Illinois environmental standards.

Tire sales, mounting, balancing, rotation, and pressure maintenance.

Vehicle towing and recovery services.
