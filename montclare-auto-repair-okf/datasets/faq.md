# Frequently Asked Questions - Montclare Auto Repair

## What services does Montclare Auto Repair offer?

We offer brake services, suspension repair, general maintenance, engine services, A/C and heating, electrical services, emissions repair, tire services, and towing. See [services.md](services.md) for full details.

## Are you ASE Certified?

Yes. Our technicians are ASE Certified. We are also a NAPA AutoCare Center, RepairPal Certified, and a CARFAX Top-Rated Service Center.

## Do you offer towing?

Yes. We provide vehicle towing and recovery services.

## Do you have free drop-off?

Yes, we offer free drop-off service for your convenience.

## What amenities do you offer?

We offer complimentary WiFi and refreshments, a customer shuttle, online appointment scheduling, and discounts for seniors, AAA members, and military personnel.

## How do I schedule an appointment?

Call us at (773) 237-2672 or schedule online through our website.

## What are your hours?

Monday through Friday 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, Saturday 8:00 am to 3:00 pm. Closed Sunday.

## Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. We provide a 24-month / 24,000-mile NAPA Peace of Mind warranty honored at 14,000+ locations nationwide.

## Is your shop RepairPal Certified?

Yes. Montclare Auto Repair is RepairPal Certified, meaning we meet fair pricing standards and quality service benchmarks.

## Do you perform emissions testing and repair?

Yes. We offer emissions diagnostics and repair to help your vehicle meet Illinois compliance requirements.

## Do you offer any discounts?

Yes. We offer senior discounts, AAA discounts, and military discounts. Ask about current promotions when you call.

## Do you have Spanish-speaking staff?

Yes. Spanish is spoken at our shop.
