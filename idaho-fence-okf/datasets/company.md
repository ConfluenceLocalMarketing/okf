# Idaho Fence Company

Idaho Fence Company is a family-owned and operated fence contractor based in Post Falls, Idaho, serving residential, commercial, and retail customers throughout Idaho since 1977.

## Services

### Residential Fencing
- **Privacy Fencing** — Wood and vinyl privacy fences for backyards and properties
- **Security Fencing** — Chain link and steel fencing for property security
- **Aesthetic Fencing** — Ornamental iron and decorative wood fencing for curb appeal

### Commercial Fencing
- **Security Fencing** — High-security chain link and steel fencing for business properties
- **Asset Protection** — Perimeter fencing for equipment yards, storage facilities, and industrial sites

### Retail Fencing
- **DIY Materials** — Fencing materials and supplies for do-it-yourself customers
- **Installation Services** — Professional installation available for retail material purchases

### Farm Fencing
- **Agricultural Fencing** — Livestock containment and pasture division fencing
- **Field Fencing** — Durable fencing for large acreage and agricultural operations

## Materials

- **Chain Link** — Galvanized and vinyl-coated chain link fencing in multiple heights
- **Wood** — Cedar, pine, and treated lumber privacy fencing
- **Vinyl** — Low-maintenance vinyl fencing in多种 styles and colors
- **Steel** — Heavy-gauge steel fencing for maximum security
- **Ornamental Iron** — Decorative wrought-iron style fencing
- **Farm Fencing** — Field fence, barbed wire, and high-tensile fencing
