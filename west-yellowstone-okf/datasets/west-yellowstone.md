# West Yellowstone

## Overview

West Yellowstone is a small mountain town in **Gallatin County, Montana**, situated immediately adjacent to the **west entrance of Yellowstone National Park**. Founded in the early 1900s as a railroad gateway community, it has grown into the park's busiest entry point, hosting over half of all Yellowstone visitors annually.

- **Population:** ~1,300
- **Elevation:** 6,666 ft
- **County:** Gallatin County, Montana
- **Season:** Year-round (peak summer); winter access via snowcoach and snowmobile
- **Airport:** Yellowstone Airport (WYS)

## Lodging

West Yellowstone offers over 2,000 hotel rooms across a range of property types:

- **Hotels & Motels** — Kelly Inn, Holiday Inn, WorldMark West Yellowstone
- **Cabins & Lodges** — Explorer Cabins at Yellowstone, Three Bear Lodge
- **RV Parks & Campgrounds** — Grizzly RV Park, Yellowstone Grizzly RV Park

## Attractions

- **Yellowstone National Park** — Old Faithful, Grand Prismatic Spring, Hayden Valley, Madison River, Firehole River
- **Grizzly & Wolf Discovery Center** — Wildlife conservation and education center
- **Museum of the Yellowstone** — Housed in the historic Union Pacific Depot
- **IMAX Theatre** — Large-format adventure films
- **Earthquake Lake** — Site of the 1959 Hebgen Lake earthquake
- **Hebgen Lake** — Fishing, boating, and camping
- **Henry's Lake State Park** — Trout fishing and camping
- **Harriman State Park** — Wildlife refuge and hiking trails

## Activities

See [seasonal-activities.md](../tables/seasonal-activities.md) for a comprehensive breakdown by season.

### Summer
- Wildlife viewing (bison, bears, elk, wolves, eagles)
- Geyser gazing and thermal feature hikes
- Fly fishing on the Madison, Firehole, and Gibbon Rivers
- Hiking and backpacking in Yellowstone backcountry
- Biking and mountain biking
- Horseback riding and trail rides
- Photography and scenic driving

### Winter
- Snowmobiling on groomed trails
- Cross-country skiing and snowshoeing
- Snowcoach tours into Yellowstone
- Wildlife viewing in winter habitats
