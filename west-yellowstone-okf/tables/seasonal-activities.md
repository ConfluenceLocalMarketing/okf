# West Yellowstone Seasonal Activities

| Activity | Summer | Winter | Equipment Needed | Skill Level | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Wildlife viewing | Yes | Yes | Binoculars, camera | All | Bison, bears, elk, wolves, eagles |
| Geyser gazing | Yes | Limited (snowcoach) | None | All | Old Faithful, Grand Prismatic |
| Fly fishing | Yes | Ice fishing | Rod, waders, flies | Intermediate | Madison, Firehole, Gibbon Rivers |
| Hiking | Yes | Snowshoeing | Boots, poles | Beginner to Advanced | Yellowstone backcountry trails |
| Biking / mountain biking | Yes | Fat biking | Bicycle, helmet | All | Park roads and area trails |
| Horseback riding | Yes | No | Guided | All | Trail rides from local outfitters |
| Photography | Yes | Yes | Camera | All | Scenic, wildlife, night sky |
| Snowmobiling | No | Yes | Snowmobile, gear | Intermediate | Groomed trails, park tours |
| Cross-country skiing | No | Yes | Skis, poles, boots | Beginner to Advanced | Groomed and backcountry trails |
| Snowcoach tours | No | Yes | None (guided) | All | Heated coaches into Yellowstone |
| Scenic driving | Yes | Limited | Vehicle | All | Park roads, Beartooth Highway |
