# New Mazda Models - Jake Sweeney Mazda Tri-County

Jake Sweeney Mazda Tri-County carries a full lineup of new Mazda vehicles. Mazda is known for refined styling, engaging performance (Jinba Ittai), and premium interiors. Below is a summary of available models.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Mazda CX-30
Subcompact SUV, starting MSRP $26,375. Available in 2.5 S, Select, Preferred, Premium, and Turbo trims. 2.5L 4-cylinder engine standard, turbocharged option available. All-wheel drive available. 20 in stock.

### Mazda CX-5
Compact SUV, starting MSRP $29,990. Multiple trims including 2.5 S, Select, Preferred, Premium, Premium Plus, and Carbon Edition. 2.5L 4-cylinder or 2.5L Turbo engine options. All-wheel drive. 89 in stock.

### Mazda CX-50
Mid-size SUV, starting MSRP $29,900. Available in 2.5 S, Select, Preferred, Premium, Premium Plus, and Meridian Edition trims. All-wheel drive standard. 42 in stock.

### Mazda CX-50 Hybrid
Hybrid mid-size SUV, starting MSRP $34,750. Toyota-sourced hybrid powertrain. Excellent fuel economy. All-wheel drive. 17 in stock.

### Mazda CX-70
Mid-size SUV (two-row), starting MSRP $46,780. Available in 3.3 Turbo, Turbo S, and Turbo S Premium trims. Inline-6 cylinder engine. All-wheel drive. 7 in stock.

### Mazda CX-70 Plug-In Hybrid
PHEV mid-size SUV, starting MSRP $44,450. 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with electric motor. All-electric range for daily commuting. All-wheel drive. 5 in stock.

### Mazda CX-90
Full-size SUV (three-row), starting MSRP $39,300. Available in 3.3 Turbo, Turbo S, Turbo S Premium, and Turbo S Premium Plus trims. Inline-6 cylinder engine. All-wheel drive standard. 33 in stock.

### Mazda CX-90 Plug-In Hybrid
PHEV full-size SUV, starting MSRP $50,695. 2.5L 4-cylinder with electric motor. All-electric range for daily commuting. Three-row seating. All-wheel drive. 5 in stock.

### Mazda MX-5 Miata

### Mazda MX-5 Miata RF
Retractable fastback convertible, starting MSRP $38,450. Power-retractable hard top. Club and Grand Touring trims. 2.0L 4-cylinder. Rear-wheel drive. 4 in stock.
