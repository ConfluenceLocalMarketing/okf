# Redhawk Gastropub

Redhawk Gastropub is a one-of-a-kind restaurant located on the Snake River Canyon rim in Twin Falls, Idaho. The establishment offers a hand-curated menu of burgers, steaks, seafood, and salads, alongside 28 beers on tap, craft cocktails, and an award-winning wine selection. Voted a Best Restaurant by Diners' Choice and 2024 NextDoor Neighborhood Fave.
