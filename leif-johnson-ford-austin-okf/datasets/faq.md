# Frequently Asked Questions

## Vehicles & Inventory

**Q: What Ford models are available at Leif Johnson Ford of Austin?**
A: The dealership carries the full Ford lineup including F-150, F-150 Lightning, Maverick, Ranger, Super Duty, Bronco, Bronco Sport, Explorer, Expedition, Escape, Mustang, Mustang Mach-E, E-Transit, and Transit vans.

**Q: Do you have used vehicles from other manufacturers?**
A: Yes. The pre-owned inventory spans multiple makes and models including Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Honda, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and more.

**Q: Can I see window stickers for used vehicles online?**
A: Yes. Transparent original window stickers are displayed on digital inventory pages.

**Q: Can I custom-order a new Ford from the factory?**
A: Yes. The dealership facilitates custom factory ordering directly with Ford to secure your preferred options.

## Financing

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes. Complete the online [finance application](https://www.leifjohnsonford.com/finance-application/) from home.

**Q: What credit situations do you work with?**
A: The finance team works with multiple lending partners to accommodate various credit profiles, including limited credit history.

**Q: Do you offer both buying and leasing options?**
A: Yes. The finance center provides both purchase financing and lease programs.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes. Use the [Black Book Trade-In](https://www.leifjohnsonford.com/black-book-trade-in/) tool for instant online valuation.

## Service

**Q: Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?**
A: The service department uses only OEM parts for all repairs.

**Q: Do you offer mobile service?**
A: Yes. Mobile Service brings repairs to your home or office.

**Q: Can I schedule service online?**
A: Yes. Use the online [service appointment](https://www.leifjohnsonford.com/service-appointment/) form.

**Q: Do you offer Ford Pickup & Delivery?**
A: Yes. The dealership provides vehicle pickup and delivery service for service appointments.
