# Carpet Interiors Floor & Home

Carpet Interiors is a family-owned flooring and home improvement retailer in Orland Park, Illinois, serving the Chicagoland area for over 45 years. As a member of the Carpet One Floor & Home cooperative, they offer a wide selection of flooring products from leading brands.

## Products

- **Carpet** — Broad selection of styles, colors, and fibers with professional installation
- **Luxury Vinyl** — LVT (Luxury Vinyl Tile) and LVP (Luxury Vinyl Plank)
- **Hardwood** — Solid and engineered hardwood flooring
- **Tile** — Ceramic, porcelain, and stone tile
- **Laminate** — Durable laminate flooring options
- **Area Rugs** — Ready-made and custom area rugs
- **Window Treatments** — Custom blinds and shades

## Services

- Free in-home and in-office estimates
- Professional installation by experienced crews
- Healthier Living Installation System
- Free measure service
- Shop from Home consultations

## Brands

Carries major flooring brands through the Carpet One Floor & Home network.
