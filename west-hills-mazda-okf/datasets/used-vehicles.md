# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - West Hills Mazda

West Hills Mazda maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (hundreds in stock)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Mazda, Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Subaru, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from budget-friendly to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** SUVs, crossovers, sedans, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover
- **Bargain inventory** available for budget-conscious buyers

## Quality Assurance

- **Detailed inspections** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **Mazda Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance
- **Vehicle history reports** - Available on request
- **Low-mileage options** across multiple makes

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Mazda Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- Mazda factory-backed warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified Mazda technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- Value Your Trade tool (KBB)
- Car payment calculator
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Vehicle hold policy

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal available through KBB instant offer
- Competitive trade-in values
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
