# New Mazda Models - West Hills Mazda

West Hills Mazda carries a full lineup of new Mazda vehicles with over 150 new Mazdas in stock. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Mazda CX-30
Subcompact crossover SUV with upscale interior and engaging driving dynamics. Available trims include 2.5 S, Select Sport, Preferred, Premium, Carbon Edition, Turbo, and Turbo Premium Plus. Powered by a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine (191 hp) or 2.5L Turbo (250 hp) with 6-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and AWD. Seats 5 passengers.

### Mazda CX-5
Compact crossover SUV, Mazda's best-selling model. Available trims include 2.5 S, Select, Preferred, Premium, Premium Plus, Carbon Edition, Turbo, Turbo Signature. Powered by a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine (187 hp) or 2.5L Turbo (256 hp). Available in FWD and AWD. Seats 5 passengers.

### Mazda CX-50
Compact crossover SUV with rugged off-road-inspired styling. Available in 2.5 S and 2.5 Turbo variants across multiple trims. Powered by a 2.5L 4-cylinder or 2.5L Turbo engine. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Mazda CX-70
Mid-size 2-row crossover SUV with bold proportions. Available with Plug-In Hybrid powertrain combining a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with electric motor. Premium Package trim with luxurious interior.

### Mazda CX-90
Mid-size 3-row crossover SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available with e-Skyactiv G 3.3L Turbo Inline-6 or Plug-In Hybrid powertrain. Multiple trims including Select, Preferred, Premium, Premium Plus, and Turbo S variants. Available in RWD and AWD.

### MX-30
All-electric crossover SUV with distinctive freestyle doors. Compact and city-friendly with a focus on sustainable materials.

## Cars

### Mazda3
Compact sedan and hatchback available in 2.5 S, Select Sport, Preferred, Premium, Carbon Edition, Turbo, and Turbo Premium Plus trims. Powered by 2.5L 4-cylinder (191 hp) or 2.5L Turbo (250 hp). Available in FWD and AWD with 6-speed automatic transmission. Hatchback offers versatile cargo space.

### MX-5 Miata
Iconic 2-seat roadster available in soft-top (Sport, Club, Grand Touring) and RF (Retractable Fastback) configurations. Powered by 2.0L 4-cylinder engine (181 hp) with 6-speed manual or automatic transmission. RWD. Celebrated for its lightweight design and exceptional handling.

## Hybrid & Electric Options

### Mazda CX-70 Plug-In Hybrid
Mid-size plug-in hybrid crossover combining efficiency with performance. Premium Package trim with advanced technology.

### Mazda CX-90 Plug-In Hybrid
3-row plug-in hybrid SUV with up to 26 miles EV-only range. Combines a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with electric motor for 323 hp combined output.

### MX-30 EV
All-electric crossover with a compact footprint and distinctive design.
