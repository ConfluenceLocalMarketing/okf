# Service Center - West Hills Mazda

The West Hills Mazda Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 1000 Oyster Bay Ave S, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Collision Repair** - Body work and paint services
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Mazda's recommended maintenance schedule
