# Sutherlands Home Improvement

Sutherlands is a privately-owned, family-run home improvement retailer founded in 1917 by Robert R. Sutherland. With 109 years of history, the company provides lumber, building materials, hardware, tools, appliances, paint, lawn and garden supplies, and building packages from locations across the central United States.

## History

Founded in 1917 by Robert R. Sutherland, Sutherlands has grown from a single lumber yard into a regional home improvement chain serving the central United States. The company maintains its family-owned heritage while providing modern e-commerce capabilities including online ordering, in-store pickup, and home delivery.
