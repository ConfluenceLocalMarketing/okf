# New Chevrolet Models - Jake Sweeney Chevrolet

Jake Sweeney Chevrolet carries a full lineup of new Chevrolet vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## Pickup Trucks

### Chevrolet Silverado 1500
Full-size pickup truck offered in multiple configurations. Available trims include Work Truck (WT), Custom, Custom Trail Boss, LT, RST, LT Trail Boss, Z71, LTZ, and High Country. Engine options range from the 2.7L Turbo to the 6.2L V8. Towing capacity up to 13,300 lbs.

### Chevrolet Silverado HD (2500HD / 3500HD)
Heavy-duty pickup trucks designed for maximum towing and payload. Available in multiple trims with Duramax diesel and gas V8 engine options.

### Chevrolet Silverado EV
All-electric full-size pickup truck available in Work Truck (WT) and RST trims. Estimated range up to 400 miles. Part of Chevrolet's Ultium EV platform.

### Chevrolet Colorado
Mid-size pickup truck available in WT, LT, Z71, Trail Boss, and ZR2 trims. Features the 2.7L Turbo engine. Off-road capable Trail Boss and ZR2 variants available.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Chevrolet Trax
Subcompact SUV, value-oriented, available in LS, LT, Activ, and RS trims. Turbocharged engine standard. One of the most affordable new Chevrolets.

### Chevrolet Trailblazer
Small SUV between Trax and Equinox in size. Available in LS, LT, Activ, and RS trims. All-wheel drive available.

### Chevrolet Equinox
Compact SUV, popular family crossover. Multiple trims including LS, LT, and RS. Available with all-wheel drive.

### Chevrolet Blazer / Blazer EV
Mid-size SUV with bold styling. Available in 2LT, 3LT, and RS trims. The **Blazer EV** is the all-electric variant offering zero-emission driving.

### Chevrolet Traverse
Mid-size 8-passenger SUV with three-row seating. Available in LS, LT, RS, and Premier trims. V6 engine standard.

### Chevrolet Tahoe
Full-size SUV based on the Silverado platform. Available in LS, LT, Z71, RST, Premier, and High Country trims. Offers Duramax diesel option.

### Chevrolet Suburban
Full-size extended SUV, the longest Chevrolet SUV. Same trim lineup as Tahoe with additional cargo space behind the third row.

## Cars

### Chevrolet Malibu
Mid-size sedan, comfortable and fuel-efficient. Available in LS, RS, and Premier trims. Standard 1.5L Turbo engine.

### Chevrolet Camaro
Muscle car / sports coupe. Available in LT1, 2LT, 3LT, and SS trims. V6 and V8 engine options.

### Chevrolet Corvette
Mid-engine sports car in Stingray, E-Ray, and high-performance Z06 variants. Available as coupe or convertible.

### Chevrolet Cruze
Compact sedan offering fuel-efficient transportation.

### Chevrolet Sonic
Subcompact hatchback/sedan, value-oriented city car.

### Chevrolet Spark
Minicar / city car, Chevrolet's most affordable model.

### Chevrolet Impala
Full-size sedan with spacious interior and comfortable ride.

## Electric Vehicles

### Chevrolet Bolt EV / Bolt SUV
All-electric compact hatchback and SUV variants offering affordable EV ownership with competitive range (~259 miles).

### Chevrolet Equinox EV
All-electric compact SUV. Part of Chevrolet's Ultium EV platform.

### Chevrolet Blazer EV
All-electric mid-size SUV available in 2LT, RS, and SS trims. Estimated range ~293 miles.

### Chevrolet Silverado EV
All-electric full-size pickup truck with estimated range up to 400 miles (WT model).

### BrightDrop 400 / 600
All-electric commercial vans designed for last-mile delivery and fleet operations. Part of Chevrolet's commercial EV lineup.

Promotions vary by model and include low APR financing, lease specials, and rebates. Contact the dealership for current offers.
