# Service Center - Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Cuyahoga Falls

The Ron Marhofer Hyundai of Cuyahoga Falls Service Department provides factory-authorized maintenance and repair services for all Hyundai models.

## Services Offered

- Routine maintenance: oil changes, tire rotations, multi-point inspections
- Factory-scheduled maintenance per Hyundai recommendations
- Hyundai Complimentary Maintenance (3 years/36,000 miles)
- Advanced diagnostics and repair: brake service, engine diagnostics, electrical service, transmission repairs
- Free brake inspections covering pads, rotors, fluid, and road test
- Tire replacement and alignment
- Battery service and testing
- Genuine Hyundai parts and accessories
- Hyundai Blue Link connected services support
