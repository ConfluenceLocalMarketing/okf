## Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned Program

The dealership participates in the Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned program, which provides:

- Comprehensive warranty coverage
- 173-point inspection and reconditioning
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Trip interruption coverage
- Rental car reimbursement
- 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain limited warranty (CPO)
