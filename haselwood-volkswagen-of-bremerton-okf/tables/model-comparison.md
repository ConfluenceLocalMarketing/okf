## Warranty

All new Volkswagen models include:
- **4-Year / 50,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty**
- **5-Year / 60,000-Mile Powertrain Limited Warranty**
- **7-Year / 100,000-Mile Anti-Corrosion Perforation Limited Warranty**
- **3-Year / 36,000-Mile Roadside Assistance**
- **2-Year / 24,000-Mile Scheduled Carefree Maintenance**

Plus:
- **Lifetime Powertrain Warranty** (West Hills Advantage - dealership-specific)
- **Volkswagen Safe & Secure** - Connected safety and security services

## IQ.DRIVE Safety Suite

Available or standard across most Volkswagen models:
- Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)
- Travel Assist (semi-automated driving)
- Lane Keeping System (Lane Assist)
- Emergency Assist
- Front Assist with Autonomous Emergency Braking
- Pedestrian Monitoring
- Blind Spot Monitor
- Rear Traffic Alert
- Park Distance Control

## Volkswagen Carefree Scheduled Maintenance

Prepaid maintenance programs available for 2, 3, or 4 years covering scheduled maintenance including oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, and more.
