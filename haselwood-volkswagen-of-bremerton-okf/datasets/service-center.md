# Service Center - Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton

The Haselwood Volkswagen Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 5008 Auto Center Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Tire Services** - Tire replacement, balancing, rotation, and wear evaluations
- **Brake Services** - Brake pad replacement, rotor resurfacing, fluid flush
- **Battery Services** - Battery testing, health check, replacement
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Volkswagen's recommended maintenance schedule
