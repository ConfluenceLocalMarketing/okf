# New Volkswagen Models - Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton

Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton carries a full lineup of new Volkswagen vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Volkswagen Atlas
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 7 passengers. Available trims include S, SE, SE with Technology, SEL, SEL R-Line, and Peak Edition. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (269 hp) with 8-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and 4Motion AWD. Towing capacity up to 5,000 lbs. Standard VW Digital Cockpit and IQ.DRIVE safety suite.

### Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport
Mid-size 2-row crossover SUV with sporty fastback roofline. Available in S, SE, SE with Technology, SEL, and SEL R-Line trims. Shares the Atlas powertrain with 2.0L turbo engine and 8-speed automatic. Available in FWD and 4Motion AWD. Spacious cargo area with 40.3 cu ft behind rear seats.

### Volkswagen Tiguan
Compact 2-row SUV, Volkswagen's best-selling model in the US. Available in S, SE, SE R-Line, SEL, and SEL R-Line trims. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (184 hp) with 8-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and 4Motion AWD. Seats 5 passengers with generous cargo space.

### Volkswagen Taos
Subcompact SUV, Volkswagen's most affordable SUV. Available in S, SE, and SEL trims. Powered by a 1.5L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (158 hp) with 8-speed automatic (FWD) or 7-speed DSG (AWD). Available in FWD and 4Motion AWD. Excellent fuel economy and value-oriented pricing.

### Volkswagen ID.4
All-electric compact SUV available in multiple configurations. Up to 304 miles estimated range. Available in RWD and 4Motion AWD. Up to 335 hp in AWD configurations. Features a 12.9-inch touchscreen display, wireless smartphone integration, and available 2-year Electrify America charging plan.

## Cars

### Volkswagen Jetta
Compact sedan, refined and fuel-efficient. Available in S, Sport, SE, and SEL trims. Powered by a 1.5L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (158 hp) with 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic transmission. FWD. Exceptional highway fuel economy. IQ.DRIVE standard on most trims.

### Volkswagen Jetta GLI
Performance-tuned compact sedan. Available in 40th Anniversary Edition and Autobahn trims. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (228 hp) with 6-speed manual or 7-speed DSG dual-clutch automatic. VAQ limited-slip differential, sport-tuned suspension, and performance brakes.

### Volkswagen Arteon
Full-size fastback sedan with sleek design and premium features. Available in SE, SEL R-Line, and SEL Premium R-Line trims. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (300 hp) with 7-speed DSG and 4Motion AWD. Premium interior with R-Line sport seats, panoramic sunroof, and Harman Kardon audio.

## Hatchbacks

### Volkswagen Golf GTI
Iconic sport compact hatchback, the original hot hatch. Available in S, SE, and Autobahn trims. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (241 hp) with 6-speed manual or 7-speed DSG. Front-wheel drive with VAQ limited-slip differential. Sport suspension, plaid cloth seats, and signature GTI styling.

### Volkswagen Golf R
High-performance all-wheel-drive hatchback. Available in a single fully-loaded trim. Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (315 hp) with 6-speed manual or 7-speed DSG. 4Motion AWD with torque vectoring. Drift mode, performance exhaust, and premium interior.

## Electric Vehicles

### Volkswagen ID.4
All-electric compact SUV with up to 304 miles range. Multiple trims including Standard, Pro, Pro S, and Pro S Plus. RWD standard, available 4Motion AWD. DC fast charging capability up to 175 kW. Includes 2-year Electrify America charging plan.

### Volkswagen ID. Buzz
All-electric retro-styled van, inspired by the classic Microbus. Available in rear-wheel drive with a large 91 kWh battery. Seating for up to 7 passengers in a versatile interior. 12.9-inch touchscreen display, available powered sliding doors, and iconic design paying homage to the original Bus.
