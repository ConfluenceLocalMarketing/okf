# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton

Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Volkswagen, Chevrolet, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Nissan, GMC, Jeep, Ram, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $12,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, hatchbacks, and coupes
- **Bargain Inventory** - Quality vehicles under $12,000

## Quality Assurance

- **Multi-point inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **Volkswagen Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed inspections, extended warranties, and 24/7 roadside assistance
- **Vehicle Protection Plans** - Extended coverage options available
- **CARFAX History Reports** - Available for most vehicles
- **CARFAX One-Owner Vehicles** - Meticulously maintained single-owner vehicles

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Volkswagen Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- Volkswagen factory-backed extended warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified VW technicians
- Vehicle history report
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- Value Your Trade tool (KBB Instant Cash Offer)
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Click-N-Drive online purchasing
- Home delivery options
- At-home test drives
