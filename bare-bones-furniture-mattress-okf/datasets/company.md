# Bare Bones Furniture & Mattress

## Overview

Bare Bones Furniture & Mattress is a locally owned and family-operated furniture retailer based in Glens Falls, New York. Now run by the second generation of the Quirion family, the business focuses on selling quality brand-name home furniture and mattresses at competitive prices with a strong emphasis on in-stock availability and immediate delivery or pickup. The company operates a large free-standing showroom and an off-site warehouse.
