# Volvo Parts Webstore — FAQ

## Ordering

### How do I find the right part for my Volvo?
You can search by year, model, and category, or enter your Vehicle Identification Number for exact-fit parts. Detailed parts diagrams are available for visual navigation.

### Do I need a VIN to order parts?
A VIN is recommended for engine, transmission, brake, and electrical components to ensure correct fitment, but not all categories require it.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Standard online payment methods including credit cards are accepted through the SimplePart e-commerce platform.

## Shipping

### Do you ship nationwide?
Yes, Volvo Parts Webstore ships nationwide across the United States.

### How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary by destination and part availability. Most orders ship within 1–2 business days from Oak Park, IL.

### What are the shipping costs?
Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the order weight, dimensions, and destination.

## Returns

### What is the return policy?
Parts must be returned in new, unused condition in original packaging. Return requests must be initiated within 30 days of delivery.

### Are there any non-returnable items?
Electrical components, special-order parts, and clearance items may be final sale. Contact customer service for specific items.

### Who pays return shipping?
Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the wrong part was shipped due to an error on our end.

## Fitment

### How can I verify a part fits my Volvo?
Use the VIN lookup tool on the website, consult the parts diagrams, or contact customer support with your VIN and the part number in question.

### Are all parts genuine OEM Volvo?
Yes, every part sold is a genuine OEM Volvo part guaranteed to meet factory specifications.

## Support

### How do I contact customer support?
Call (708) 786-6574 or email sales@volvopartswebstore.com. Customer support is available during business hours.

### Does the webstore offer discounts?
Yes, pricing is below MSRP and seasonal promotions and coupon codes are available.
