# Varsity Lincoln Website

Varsity Lincoln operates its online presence at https://www.varsitylincoln.com/. The website provides full dealership capabilities including inventory browsing, service scheduling, credit applications, and trade-in valuation.

## Site Features

- New Lincoln vehicle inventory with photos, specs, and pricing
- Pre-owned inventory search with Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned listings
- Service center appointment scheduling
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Payment calculator
- Value Your Trade tool
- Vehicle protection plans
- Special offers and incentives
- Lincoln model research and comparison
- Lincoln Pickup & Delivery scheduling
- Lincoln Mobile Service requests
- Complimentary delivery within 300 miles details
