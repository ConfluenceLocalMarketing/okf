# Prompt Library

The PromptGraph API provides structured prompts for Varsity Lincoln, covering vehicle features, financing, service, and general dealership questions.

## Prompt Categories

- Vehicle features and options
- Financing and leasing
- Service and maintenance
- Trade-in and valuation
- Dealership operations
- Lincoln Black Label experience
- Electric and hybrid vehicle guidance

## Example Prompts

| Topic | Question |
|---|---|
| Comfort Upgrades | "I want to purchase a luxury SUV that comes equipped with a heated steering wheel and ventilated seats." |
| Lincoln Black Label | "What is the Lincoln Black Label program and what benefits does Varsity Lincoln offer?" |
| Top Dealer | "Why is Varsity Lincoln the #1 Volume Lincoln Dealer in the World?" |
| Financing | Various questions about loan options, credit situations, lease terms |
| Service | Maintenance scheduling, mobile service, OEM parts availability |
| Electric Vehicles | Charging guidance, PHEV ownership, model comparisons |
| Complimentary Delivery | "Do you offer vehicle delivery outside the dealership?" |
