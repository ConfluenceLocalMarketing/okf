# Frequently Asked Questions

## Vehicles & Inventory

**Q: What Lincoln models are available at Varsity Lincoln?**
A: The dealership carries the full Lincoln lineup including Corsair, Nautilus, Aviator, Navigator, and Navigator L.

**Q: Do you have used vehicles from other manufacturers?**
A: Yes. The pre-owned inventory spans multiple luxury makes and models, not just Lincoln.

**Q: Are Lincoln Black Label vehicles available?**
A: Yes. Varsity Lincoln is one of only a few Lincoln Black Label dealers nationwide, offering exclusive interior themes and premium ownership benefits.

**Q: What is the Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned program?**
A: It includes a 200-point inspection, comprehensive limited warranty, 24/7 roadside assistance, and complimentary Lincoln Pickup & Delivery for covered repairs.

## Financing

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes. Complete the online credit application on the Varsity Lincoln website from home.

**Q: What credit situations do you work with?**
A: The finance team works with multiple lending partners to accommodate various credit profiles.

**Q: Do you offer both buying and leasing options?**
A: Yes. The finance center provides both purchase financing and Lincoln luxury lease programs.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes. Use the Value Your Trade tool on the Varsity Lincoln website for instant online valuation.

## Service

**Q: Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?**
A: The service department uses only genuine OEM Lincoln parts for all repairs.

**Q: Do you offer mobile service?**
A: Yes. Lincoln Mobile Service brings factory-trained technicians to your home or office for select maintenance.

**Q: Do you offer Lincoln Pickup & Delivery?**
A: Yes. Complimentary vehicle pick-up and drop-off for service appointments.

**Q: Do you provide loaner vehicles during service?**
A: Yes. Complimentary loaner vehicles are available during service appointments.

**Q: Can I schedule service online?**
A: Yes. Use the online service scheduling tool on the Varsity Lincoln website.

## Dealership

**Q: What are your sales hours?**
A: Monday–Saturday 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Sunday Closed.

**Q: Where is the dealership located?**
A: 49251 Grand River, Novi, MI 48374.

**Q: Is Varsity Lincoln family-owned?**
A: Yes. Varsity Lincoln is part of the Varsity Automotive Group, family-owned and operated for over 30 years.

**Q: Is Varsity Lincoln really the #1 Lincoln dealer in the world?**
A: Yes. Varsity Lincoln has been the #1 Volume Lincoln Dealer in the World for 22 consecutive years, since 1997.

**Q: Do you offer complimentary vehicle delivery?**
A: Yes. Complimentary delivery within 300 miles on qualifying vehicles.
