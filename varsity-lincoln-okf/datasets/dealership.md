# Varsity Lincoln

Varsity Lincoln is a Lincoln luxury automotive dealership located at 49251 Grand River, Novi, MI 48374, serving Novi, Ann Arbor, Wixom, Farmington Hills, and the Metro Detroit area. The dealership is part of Varsity Automotive Group, family-owned and operated for over 30 years. Varsity Lincoln has been the #1 Volume Lincoln Dealer in the World for 22 consecutive years (since 1997).
