# Pre-Owned Vehicles

Varsity Lincoln maintains a diverse pre-owned luxury inventory spanning Lincoln and other premium makes, with a range of model years and price points.

## Inventory Categories

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| All Used Vehicles | Full pre-owned inventory across all makes and models |
| Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned | Lincoln-certified vehicles with factory-backed warranty |
| Used Luxury SUVs | Pre-owned luxury SUVs from Lincoln and other premium brands |
| Pre-Owned Specials | Discounted pre-owned vehicles with special pricing |

## Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned Program

The dealership offers the Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned program, which provides:

- 200-point inspection by factory-trained technicians
- Comprehensive limited warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Complimentary Lincoln Pickup & Delivery for covered repairs
- Vehicle history report
- Rental car coverage

## Trade-In

The dealership offers trade-in evaluation through:
- Online trade-in valuation tool on the Varsity Lincoln website
- In-person appraisal at the dealership
- Value Your Trade tool

## Complimentary Delivery

Complimentary vehicle delivery within 300 miles on qualifying vehicles.
