# Financing & Leasing

The Varsity Lincoln Finance Center offers purchase financing and luxury lease programs for new and pre-owned Lincoln vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home via the online credit application on the Varsity Lincoln website
- **Pre-Approval**: Get pre-approved before visiting the dealership
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets

## Leasing

Lincoln luxury leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Get an instant online valuation through the Value Your Trade tool on the Varsity Lincoln website, or bring your vehicle in for an in-person appraisal.

## Payment Calculators

Estimate monthly payments using the payment calculator on the Varsity Lincoln website before visiting the dealership.
