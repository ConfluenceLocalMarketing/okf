# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - West Hills Ford

West Hills Ford maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (hundreds in stock)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Ford, Chevrolet, GMC, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Nissan, Jeep, Ram, Dodge, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Subaru, Volkswagen, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover

## Quality Assurance

- **Certified Pre-Owned Ford Models** - Factory-backed inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance
- **Multi-Point Inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **Vehicle Service Contracts** - Available for most vehicles to prevent unexpected expenses
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Ford Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- Ford factory-backed warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified Ford technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- KBB Instant Cash Offer - Trade-in valuation tool
- Car payment calculator
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Click-N-Drive online purchasing
- Home delivery
- Vehicle hold policy

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal via KBB Instant Cash Offer
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
- Competitive trade-in values
