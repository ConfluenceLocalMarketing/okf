# Service Center - West Hills Ford

The West Hills Ford Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 1100 Oyster Bay Ave South, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Ford Warranty & Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Ford's recommended maintenance schedule
- **EV & Hybrid Service** - Specialized service for Mustang Mach-E, F-150 Lightning, and hybrid models
