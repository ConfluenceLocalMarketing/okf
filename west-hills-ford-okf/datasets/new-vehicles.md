# New Ford Models - West Hills Ford

West Hills Ford carries a full lineup of new Ford vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Ford Bronco
Iconic off-road SUV with removable doors and roof. Available trims include Big Bend, Black Diamond, Outer Banks, Badlands, Wildtrak, Everglades, Heritage, Heritage Limited, and Raptor. Powered by 2.3L EcoBoost (300 hp) or 2.7L EcoBoost V6 (330 hp). Available in 2-door and 4-door configurations. Features available 7-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission. Trail Toolbox includes Trail Control, Trail Turn Assist, and Trail One-Pedal Drive.

### Ford Bronco Sport
Compact adventure SUV designed for outdoor lifestyles. Available in Big Bend, Heritage, Outer Banks, Badlands, and Free Wheeling trims. Powered by 1.5L EcoBoost (181 hp) or 2.0L EcoBoost (250 hp) in Badlands trim. Standard 8-speed automatic transmission with available twin-clutch rear drive unit for enhanced off-road traction.

### Ford Escape
Compact crossover SUV with available hybrid and plug-in hybrid powertrains. Available in S, SE, SEL, ST-Line, Platinum, and Plug-In Hybrid trims. Features the 1.5L EcoBoost (180 hp) or 2.0L EcoBoost (250 hp). Hybrid models deliver excellent fuel economy with up to 2,000 lbs towing capacity.

### Ford Explorer
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 7 passengers. Available in XLT, ST-Line, Limited, ST, Platinum, and King Ranch trims. Powered by 2.3L EcoBoost (300 hp) or 3.0L EcoBoost V6 (400 hp in ST). Available in RWD and 4WD. Towing capacity up to 5,600 lbs. Ford Co-Pilot360 standard.

### Ford Expedition / Expedition Max
Full-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Powered by 3.5L EcoBoost V6 (375 hp) with 10-speed automatic transmission. Available in XL, XLT, Limited, King Ranch, and Platinum trims. Maximum towing capacity up to 9,300 lbs. Expedition Max offers additional cargo space behind the third row.

### Ford Edge
Mid-size 2-row crossover SUV. Available in SE, SEL, ST-Line, Titanium, and ST trims. Powered by 2.0L EcoBoost (250 hp) or 2.7L EcoBoost V6 (335 hp in ST). Available in FWD and AWD. Ford Co-Pilot360 standard.

### Ford Mustang Mach-E
All-electric crossover SUV available in Select, Premium, California Route 1, and GT trims. Up to 312 miles estimated range. Available in RWD and eAWD. Up to 480 hp in GT trim. BlueCruise hands-free highway driving available. DC fast charging capability.

## Trucks

### Ford F-150
Full-size pickup truck, America's best-selling vehicle for over 40 years. Available in XL, XLT, Lariat, King Ranch, Platinum, Tremor, Raptor, and Lightning (all-electric) trims. Powertrain options include 2.7L EcoBoost V6, 3.3L V6, 5.0L V8, 3.5L EcoBoost V6, 3.5L PowerBoost Full Hybrid V6, and all-electric (Lightning). Maximum towing capacity up to 14,000 lbs. Available Features: Pro Power Onboard generator, 12-inch touchscreen, BlueCruise hands-free driving.

### Ford F-250 / Super Duty
Heavy-duty pickup truck for maximum capability. Available in XL, XLT, Lariat, King Ranch, Platinum, and Limited trims. Powered by 6.8L V8 or 7.3L V8 gasoline engines, plus 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel. Maximum towing capacity up to 40,000 lbs (fifth wheel/gooseneck). Advanced toaling technology including Pro Trailer Hitch Assist.

### Ford Maverick
Compact hybrid pickup truck, starting under $25,000. Available in XL, XLT, Lariat, and Tremor trims. Standard 2.5L hybrid powertrain (191 hp) with CVT, achieving EPA-estimated 42 mpg city. Available 2.0L EcoBoost (250 hp) with 8-speed automatic. 4.5 ft bed with FLEXBED storage system. Standard towing capacity of 1,500 lbs (up to 4,000 lbs with EcoBoost and 4K Tow Package).

### Ford Ranger
Mid-size pickup truck redesigned for 2024. Available in XL, XLT, Lariat, and Raptor trims. Powered by 2.3L EcoBoost (270 hp) or 3.0L EcoBoost V6 (405 hp in Raptor). 10-speed automatic transmission. Maximum towing capacity up to 7,500 lbs. Available with Pro Trailer Backup Assist and Trail Control.

## Cars

### Ford Mustang
Iconic sports car, seventh generation. Available in EcoBoost, GT, Dark Horse, and GTD trims. Powertrains: 2.3L EcoBoost (315 hp), 5.0L Coyote V8 (480 hp in GT, 500 hp in Dark Horse). Available 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic. Performance features include MagneRide dampers, Electronic Limited-Slip Differential, and Track Apps.

## Electric & Hybrid

### Ford F-150 Lightning
All-electric version of America's best-selling truck. Available in Pro, XLT, Lariat, and Platinum trims. Dual motor AWD with up to 580 hp and 775 lb-ft torque. EPA-estimated range up to 320 miles. Mega Power Frunk with 400 liters of lockable storage. Pro Power Onboard up to 9.6 kW. DC fast charging from 15% to 80% in approximately 44 minutes.

### Ford Mustang Mach-E
See SUV section above.

### Ford Escape Hybrid / Plug-In Hybrid
Compact SUV with hybrid or plug-in hybrid powertrain options. Hybrid delivers estimated 41 mpg city. Plug-In Hybrid offers up to 37 miles of EV-only range.

### Ford Maverick Hybrid
Standard hybrid powertrain on base model. EPA-estimated 42 mpg city. See Trucks section above.

### Ford E-Transit
All-electric cargo van for commercial use. Available in 3 roof heights, 3 body lengths, and 2 wheelbases. Targeted range of 126 miles. DC fast charging capable.
