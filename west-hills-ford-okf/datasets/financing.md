# West Hills Ford - Finance Center

## Finance Center

The Finance Center at West Hills Ford offers flexible financing and leasing solutions for new and pre-owned Ford vehicles. The finance team works with Ford Credit and multiple other lenders to secure competitive rates for customers across all credit profiles.

## Financing Options

- **New Vehicle Loans** - Competitive APR financing on all new Ford models through Ford Credit
- **Used Vehicle Loans** - Rate financing on pre-owned inventory with flexible terms
- **Click-N-Drive Online Purchasing** - Complete vehicle purchase from home with integrated financing
- **Special Finance Programs** - Military appreciation, first-time buyer assistance, and college graduate programs
- **Online Credit Application** - Secure pre-approval from home
- **Credit-Charged Programs** - Options for less-than-perfect credit through multiple lenders

## Leasing

Ford Credit lease programs offer lower monthly payments and the ability to drive a new Ford every few years. Lease options include Ford Lease Protection and mileage flexibility.

## Buying vs Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Return at lease end |
| Mileage Limits | None | Annual mileage cap applies |
| Warranty Coverage | After factory warranty | Typically covered by warranty |
| End of Term | No further obligations | Option to buy or return |
| Equity Building | Yes | No |

## Trade-In

Get a value for your trade-in through the KBB Instant Cash Offer online tool or in-person evaluation. The dealership accepts all makes and models regardless of condition.

## Payment Calculators

Online payment calculator tools allow customers to estimate monthly payments based on vehicle price, down payment, trade-in value, APR, and loan term.
