# Frequently Asked Questions - West Hills Ford

**Q: What brands are available in the used vehicle inventory?**
A: The pre-owned inventory spans multiple makes including Ford, Chevrolet, GMC, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Nissan, Jeep, Ram, Dodge, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Subaru, and Volkswagen.

**Q: Does West Hills Ford offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, Ford Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are available with factory-backed inspections, warranties, and 24/7 roadside assistance.

## Financing

**Q: What financing options are available?**
A: Traditional loans, leases, and special financing programs. The dealership works with multiple lenders to find the best terms.

**Q: Does the dealership work with all credit profiles?**
A: Yes, the finance team works with a network of lenders to accommodate various credit situations including bad credit or no credit.

**Q: Can I complete a purchase online?**
A: Yes, the dealership offers Click-N-Drive online purchasing allowing customers to complete their vehicle purchase from home.

## Service

**Q: Do you offer mobile service or maintenance pick-up?**
A: Yes, West Hills Ford offers maintenance pick-up and free local shuttle service while your vehicle is being serviced.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through the dealership website.

**Q: Can the service center work on non-Ford vehicles?**
A: Yes, the service center can perform maintenance and repairs on various makes and models.
