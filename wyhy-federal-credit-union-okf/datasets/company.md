# WyHy Federal Credit Union

## Overview

WyHy Federal Credit Union is a not-for-profit, member-owned financial cooperative headquartered in Cheyenne, Wyoming. Founded in December 1953, the credit union serves approximately 28,378 members across Wyoming with a full range of personal and business financial products. WyHy is federally insured by the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) and participates in the CO-OP Shared Branch and ATM network.

WyHy Digital is the credit union's online banking platform, providing account aggregation for non-WyHy accounts, free credit score access, card controls, budgeting tools, and savings goal tracking.
