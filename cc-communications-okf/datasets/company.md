# CC Communications

CC Communications is a locally-owned telecommunications company based in Fallon, Nevada, serving Churchill County since 1889. The company provides high-speed fiber internet, telephone services, security systems, PC repair, and business solutions. CC Communications is expanding its fiber network to Elko, Spring Creek, and Virginia City.
