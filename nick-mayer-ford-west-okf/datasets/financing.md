# Financing & Leasing

The Nick Mayer Ford West Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start from home
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: Competitive rates
- **Second-Chance Financing**: Options for less-than-perfect credit
- **Bankruptcy Assistance**: Programs available
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions

## Leasing

Lower monthly payments, drive a new Ford every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing Table

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own at end of term | Return at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited |
| Sales Tax | Upfront or in loan | Included in payment |

## Trade-In

Online appraisal and in-person valuation.
