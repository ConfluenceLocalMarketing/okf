# Nick Mayer Ford West

## Overview

Nick Mayer Ford West is a full-service Ford dealership located in Avon Lake, Ohio, serving the greater Cleveland metropolitan area including Lorain, Westlake, North Ridgeville, and Elyria. The dealership offers new and pre-owned Ford vehicles, financing options including second-chance financing, a full-service Ford-certified service center with ASE certified technicians, and an OEM parts department.
