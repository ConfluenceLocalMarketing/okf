# New Vehicle Models - West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM

West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM carries a full lineup of new vehicles across four brands: Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM. Below is a summary of available 2026 models.

## Chrysler

### Chrysler Pacifica
Mid-size minivan seating up to 8 passengers. Available in multiple trims. Features Stow 'n Go seating and Chrysler's innovative family-focused technology. Powered by a 3.6L V6 engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD.

### Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid
Plug-in hybrid electric minivan. Combines Pacifica's versatility with electric-only driving range. Available in Select and Pinnacle trims.

### Chrysler Voyager
Value-oriented minivan, ideal for families and commercial fleet use. Seating up to 8 passengers. Powered by 3.6L V6 engine.

## Jeep

### Jeep Cherokee
Compact SUV with confident styling. Available in Latitude, Latitude Lux, Trailhawk, and Limited trims. Available with 2.4L 4-cylinder or 3.2L V6 engine. Jeep Active Drive 4x4 system available.

### Jeep Compass
Subcompact crossover SUV. Available in Sport, Latitude, Latitude Lux, Limited, and Trailhawk trims. Standard 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with 9-speed automatic. Jeep Active Drive 4x4 available.

### Jeep Grand Cherokee
Mid-size 2-row SUV. Available in Laredo, Altitude, Limited, Overland, Summit, and Summit Reserve trims. Engine options include 3.6L V6, 5.7L V8, and 6.4L V8 (SRT). Quadra-Trac 4x4 systems available. Seats 5 passengers.

### Jeep Grand Cherokee L
Mid-size 3-row SUV. Same trims as Grand Cherokee with third-row seating for up to 7 passengers. Extended wheelbase design.

### Jeep Grand Wagoneer
Full-size luxury SUV. Available in Series I, Series II, Series III, and Obsidian trims. Powered by 6.4L V8 engine. Premium interior with up to 75 inches of screen space. Seats up to 8 passengers.

### Jeep Grand Wagoneer L
Extended length version of Grand Wagoneer with additional cargo space and third-row legroom.

### Jeep Wrangler
Iconic off-road SUV. Available in Sport, Sport S, Willys, Sahara, High Altitude, Rubicon, and Rubicon 392 trims. Engine options include 2.0L turbo 4-cylinder, 3.6L V6, 6.4L V8 (Rubicon 392), and 3.0L EcoDiesel V6. Available in 2-door and 4-door (Unlimited) configurations.

### Jeep Gladiator
Mid-size pickup truck based on Wrangler platform. Available in Sport, Sport S, Willy, Overland, High Altitude, Rubicon, and Mojave trims. Powered by 3.6L V6 engine. Maximum towing capacity of 7,650 lbs. Removable doors and roof.

## Dodge

### Dodge Charger
Full-size sedan (muscle car). Available in GT, R/T, Scat Pack, and SRT Hellcat trims. Engine options include 3.6L V6, 5.7L V8, 6.4L V8, and 6.2L supercharged V8 (Hellcat). Available in RWD and AWD (V6 models).

### Dodge Durango
Mid-size 3-row SUV. Available in SXT, GT, R/T, Citadel, SRT 392, and SRT Hellcat trims. Engine options include 3.6L V6, 5.7L V8, 6.4L V8, and 6.2L supercharged V8 (Hellcat). Towing capacity up to 8,700 lbs. Seats up to 7 passengers.

## RAM

### RAM 1500
Full-size light-duty pickup truck. Available in Tradesman, HFE, Big Horn, Lone Star, Laramie, Rebel, Longhorn, Limited, and TRX trims. Engine options include 3.6L V6 eTorque, 5.7L V8 eTorque, and 6.2L supercharged V8 (TRX). Maximum towing up to 12,750 lbs.

### RAM 2500 / 3500
Heavy-duty pickup trucks. Available in Tradesman, Big Horn, Laramie, Power Wagon (2500), Longhorn, and Limited trims. Engine options include 6.4L V8 and 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6. Maximum towing up to 37,090 lbs.

### RAM 4500 / 5500 Chassis Cab
Commercial-grade chassis cab trucks for upfitting. Available in Tradesman and Laramie trims. Powered by 6.4L V8 or 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6. Designed for commercial and fleet applications.

### RAM ProMaster City / ProMaster
Full-size cargo vans. Available in 1500, 2500, and 3500 roof and wheelbase configurations, plus Window Van variants. Powered by 3.6L V6 engine with 9-speed automatic. Designed for commercial cargo and passenger transport.
