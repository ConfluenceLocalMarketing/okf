# Service Center - West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM

The West Hills CJDR Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 900 W Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA, serving Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM vehicles.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work, A/C service
- **Brand-Specific Service Centers** - Dedicated service for Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, and RAM
- **Recall Repairs** - Mopar-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services
- **Battery Service** - Testing and replacement for all CJDR vehicles
