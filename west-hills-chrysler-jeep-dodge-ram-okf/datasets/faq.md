# Frequently Asked Questions - West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM

## Inventory

**Q: What new vehicle brands are available at West Hills CJDR?**
A: Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM — including the Pacifica, Wrangler, Grand Cherokee, Charger, Durango, RAM 1500 trucks, and ProMaster vans.

**Q: What brands are available in the used vehicle inventory?**
A: The pre-owned inventory spans multiple makes including Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, RAM, Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Hyundai, Kia, and more.

**Q: Does West Hills CJDR offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are available with factory-backed inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance.

**Q: Do you have vehicles under $12,000?**
A: Yes, a Bargain Inventory category features vehicles priced under $12,000, and Economy Vehicles are available for budget-conscious shoppers.

## Financing

**Q: What financing options are available?**
A: Traditional loans, leases, and special financing programs. The finance team works with various lenders to find the best terms. Online credit application is available.

**Q: What's better, buying or leasing?**
A: Leasing typically offers lower monthly payments but has mileage and customization restrictions. Financing builds equity with higher payments but lower lifetime cost.

**Q: How long is a typical car loan?**
A: Most loan terms fall between 48-72 months, with longer terms resulting in lower payments.

**Q: Can I get financing with bad credit or no credit?**
A: Yes, the finance team works with customers across all credit profiles. No credit, no problem — they can secure financing options.

**Q: Can I finance a vehicle without a down payment?**
A: In most cases, financing with no down payment is available. Taxes, registration, and fees can often be rolled into the financing deal.

## Service

**Q: Does the service center work on all CJDR brands?**
A: Yes, there are dedicated service centers for Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, and RAM vehicles, all staffed by certified Mopar technicians.

**Q: What service hours are available?**
A: Service is open Monday-Friday 7:30 AM - 7:00 PM, Saturday 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM. Closed Sunday.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through the dealership website.

**Q: Do you offer parts and service financing?**
A: Yes, Sunbit Service & Parts Financing is available for buy now, pay later options.

## Dealership

**Q: Where is West Hills CJDR located?**
A: 900 W Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA 98312-6300, serving Kitsap County, Port Orchard, Silverdale, Tacoma, and Seattle.

**Q: What is the Haselwood Auto Group?**
A: West Hills CJDR is part of the Haselwood Auto Group, a family of dealerships serving Kitsap County with a strong focus on community giving.

**Q: What is the No Dealer Mark-up Program?**
A: Transparent pricing on most new vehicles where the listed price reflects MSRP plus dealer-installed accessories. Excludes Grand Wagoneer Series II.

**Q: Does the dealership offer military appreciation programs?**
A: Yes, special programs are available for military personnel.

**Q: What charitable causes does the dealership support?**
A: Haselwood Family YMCA, Kitsap Humane Society, Fred Hutch Cancer Center, Marine Toys for Tots, Bloodworks Northwest, and the Haselwood Family Foundation.
