# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM

West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton, WA area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

- **Multi-make selection** spanning Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, RAM, Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Hyundai, Kia, GMC, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Subaru, Volkswagen, and more
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, coupes, and convertibles
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $12,000 to premium vehicles (Vehicles Under 12k and Economy Vehicles categories available)
- **Bargain Inventory** - Vehicles priced under $12,000
- **Economy Vehicles** - Fuel-efficient and budget-friendly options
- **Certified Pre-Owned** - Factory-backed vehicles with warranty

Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:

- 24/7 roadside assistance

- CARFAX vehicle history reports
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
