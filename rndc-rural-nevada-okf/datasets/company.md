# Rural Nevada Development Corporation (RNDC)

Rural Nevada Development Corporation (RNDC) is a non-profit Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI) founded in 1992 to support business finance and community housing development across rural Nevada. The organization provides small business financing, down payment assistance, home rehabilitation, weatherization, and multi-family housing to low- and moderate-income families, with special focus on rural communities and Native American Tribes.

## Services

### Business Services
- **Small Business Lending**: Loans for rural entrepreneurs to grow their businesses
- **I-80 Fund**: Low-interest loan program for small businesses along the I-80 corridor, established in partnership with Nevada Gold Mines

### Housing Services
- **Down Payment Assistance**: Help for low-income, first-time home buyers with purchasing residential properties meeting HUD Housing Quality Standards
- **Homeowner Rehabilitation**: Repairs addressing accessibility needs, energy conservation, and home safety
- **Weatherization**: Energy conservation measures to reduce utility bills

### Multi-Family Projects
- **John Marvel Senior Village**: Senior housing development
- **Wells Golden Village**: Multi-family housing project

## Features

- Founded in 1992
- Federally designated CDFI
- Partners with federal, state, and local entities
- Non-profit 501(c)(3) organization
- Equal Opportunity Lender
