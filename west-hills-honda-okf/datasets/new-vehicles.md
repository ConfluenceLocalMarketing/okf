# New Honda Models - West Hills Honda

West Hills Honda carries a full lineup of new Honda vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## Cars

### Honda Civic
Compact sedan and hatchback, available in LX, Sport, EX, Touring, Si, and Type R trims. Features the 2.0L 4-cylinder or 1.5L turbo engine with CVT or 6-speed manual. Available in FWD. Seats 5 passengers. Honda Sensing safety suite standard.

### Honda Civic Hybrid
Hybrid compact sedan and hatchback combining efficiency with Civic sportiness. Available in Sport and Sport Touring trims.

### Honda Accord
Mid-size sedan available in LX, EX, EX-L, and Sport trims. Features the 1.5L turbo 4-cylinder or 2.0L turbo engine with CVT. Available in FWD. Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard.

### Honda Accord Hybrid
Hybrid mid-size sedan with exceptional fuel economy. Available in EX-L, Sport, and Touring trims.

### Honda Civic Si
Performance-oriented compact sedan with 1.5L turbo engine, sport-tuned suspension, and limited-slip differential.

### Honda Civic Type R
High-performance hatchback with 2.0L turbo engine, track-tuned chassis, and aggressive aerodynamics.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Honda CR-V
Compact SUV, Honda's best-selling SUV. Available in LX, EX, EX-L, and Sport Touring trims. Features the 1.5L turbo engine with CVT. Available in FWD and AWD. Seats 5 passengers.

### Honda CR-V Hybrid
Hybrid compact SUV with exceptional fuel economy. Available in Sport and Sport Touring trims.

### Honda HR-V
Subcompact crossover SUV available in LX, Sport, and EX-L trims. Features the 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with CVT. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Honda Pilot
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in LX, EX-L, Sport, TrailSport, Touring, and Elite trims. Powered by a 3.5L V6 engine with 10-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and AWD. Towing capacity up to 5,000 lbs.

### Honda Passport
Mid-size 2-row SUV available in EX-L, Sport, TrailSport, and Black Edition trims. Powered by a 3.5L V6 engine with 10-speed automatic transmission. Available in AWD. Off-road capable TrailSport trim.

### Honda Prologue
All-electric crossover SUV available in EX, Touring, and Elite trims. Powered by a 85 kWh battery with up to 296 miles estimated range. Available in FWD and AWD (dual motor). The first all-electric SUV from Honda.

## Minivan

### Honda Odyssey
Minivan seating up to 8 passengers. Available in EX, EX-L, Sport, Touring, and Elite trims. Powered by a 3.5L V6 engine with 10-speed automatic transmission. Magic Slide second-row seats and Honda CabinWatch.

## Trucks

### Honda Ridgeline
Mid-size pickup truck with unibody construction. Available in RT, RTL, and Black Edition trims. Powered by a 3.5L V6 engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in AWD. In-bed trunk and dual-action tailgate.

## Hybrid & Electric Vehicles

- **Civic Hybrid** - Compact hybrid sedan and hatchback
