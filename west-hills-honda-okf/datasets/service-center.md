# Service Center - West Hills Honda

The West Hills Honda Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 520 W Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Honda Express Service** - Quick in-and-out maintenance without an appointment
- **Honda Recalls** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services including tire wear evaluation
- **Battery Service** - Battery testing and replacement
- **Brake Service** - Pad replacement, rotor replacement, brake system inspection
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Honda's recommended maintenance schedule
