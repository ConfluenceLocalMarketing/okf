# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - West Hills Honda

West Hills Honda maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (945+ vehicles in database)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Honda, Toyota, Ford, Chevrolet, Nissan, BMW, Lexus, Tesla, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover
- **Vehicles Under $20k** - Dedicated selection of affordable pre-owned vehicles

- **HondaTrue Certified Pre-Owned** - Factory-backed inspection with 7-year/100,000-mile powertrain warranty
