# New Vehicles — Basil Family Dealerships

## Overview

While Basil Resale specializes in pre-owned vehicles, the Basil Family Dealerships maintain an inventory of over 1,700 new vehicles across 10 locations in Western New York. Basil Resale can connect customers to new vehicle inventory through the broader family network.

## Available Brands

The Basil Family Dealerships represent multiple franchises including:

- Mitsubishi (Basil Mitsubishi)
- Ford (Basil Ford)
- BraunAbility mobility conversions (Basil Mobility)
- Additional franchise partners

1. Browse new inventory online at [basilcars.com](https://www.basilcars.com/new-vehicles/)
