# New Vehicles — Basil Family Dealerships

## Overview

While Basil Resale specializes in pre-owned vehicles, the Basil Family Dealerships maintain a substantial new vehicle inventory across 10 locations in Western New York. Basil Resale can connect customers to new vehicle inventory through the broader family network.

## Available Brands

The Basil Family Dealerships represent multiple franchises including:

- Mitsubishi (Basil Mitsubishi)
- Ford (Basil Ford, Basil Ford of Niagara Falls)
- Chevrolet (Joe Basil Chevrolet)
- Buick, GMC, Cadillac (Robert Basil Buick GMC Cadillac)
- Volkswagen (Basil Volkswagen)
- Toyota (Basil Toyota)
- BraunAbility mobility conversions (Basil Mobility)

## How It Works

1. Browse new inventory online at [basilcars.com](https://www.basilcars.com/new-vehicles/)
2. Contact Basil Resale for assistance locating a specific new vehicle
3. Leverage the Basil Family network for competitive pricing across locations
4. Commercial trucks also available through network

## Financing

- Multiple lender options through the Basil Family finance centers
- Online credit application available
- Competitive rates across new and used vehicles
