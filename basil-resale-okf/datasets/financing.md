# Financing & Leasing

The Basil Resale Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for pre-owned and new vehicles with multiple lender options regardless of credit history.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home with the online credit application and pre-approval
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets
- **Credit History**: Options available regardless of credit history

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Get an instant online valuation through the Kelley Blue Book Instant Cash Offer tool. In-person appraisal also available at the dealership. Basil Resale offers the highest trade-in allowance guarantee.

## Payment Calculators

Estimate monthly payments using the payment calculator before visiting the dealership.
