# Frequently Asked Questions - Champs Auto Service

## What services does Champs Auto Service offer?

We offer oil changes, brake repair, transmission service, engine diagnostics, tune-ups, exhaust repair, power steering service, fleet services, pre-purchase inspections, tire services, and suspension work. See [services.md](services.md) for details.

## Are you ASE Certified?

Yes. Our technicians are ASE Master Certified and we are a NAPA AutoCare Center. See [company.md](company.md) for all certifications.

## Do you service all makes and models?

Yes. We work on all makes and models including foreign cars and diesel engines.

## How do I schedule an appointment?

Call us at (773) 625-5000. We also offer priority scheduling for fleet customers.

## Do you offer any specials or discounts?

Yes. We offer 10% off brake service, oil change coupons, and seasonal promotions. Ask about our current specials when you call.

## Do you provide fleet services?

Yes. We offer preventive maintenance, repairs, and priority scheduling for business fleets. See [services.md](services.md) for fleet program details.

## Do you perform pre-purchase inspections?

Absolutely. We provide thorough vehicle condition evaluations for used car buyers.

## Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes. As a NAPA AutoCare Center, our repairs are backed by a nationwide warranty honored at 14,000+ locations.

## How long have you been in business?

We have been serving the Chicago community since 2010.

## Do you work on diesel engines?

Yes. Our ASE Master Certified technicians are experienced with diesel engine diagnostics and repair.

## What type of oil do you use?

We use Mobil 1 oil and offer conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic options.

## Do you perform transmission repairs?

Yes. We offer transmission diagnostics, fluid changes, repairs, and rebuilds.
