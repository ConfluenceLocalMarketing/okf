# Champs Auto Service (Champ Auto Repairs)

Champs Auto Service (doing business as Champ Auto Repairs) is a family-oriented auto repair shop in Chicago, Illinois, serving the community since 2010. The shop employs ASE Master Certified technicians and provides full-service repairs for all makes and models including foreign cars and diesel engines.

## Services

- **Oil Changes** — Conventional, synthetic, and full synthetic options with Mobil 1 oil
- **Brake Repair** — Inspections, pads, rotors, calipers, and full brake system service
- **Transmission Service** — Repairs, diagnostics, fluid changes, and rebuilds
- **Engine Diagnostics and Repair** — Check engine light diagnosis, misfires, emissions issues
- **Tune-Ups** — Comprehensive performance optimization
- **Exhaust System Repair** — Muffler and exhaust system service
- **Power Steering Service** — Steering system maintenance and repair
- **Fleet Services** — Preventive maintenance, repairs, and priority scheduling for businesses
- **Pre-Purchase Inspections** — Thorough vehicle condition evaluation for used car buyers
- **Tire Services** — Winter tire services, TPMS, and tire maintenance
- **Suspension Services** — Suspension system repair and maintenance

## Certifications

- **NAPA AutoCare Center** — Nationwide warranty honored at 14,000+ locations
