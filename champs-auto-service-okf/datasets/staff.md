# Staff Directory - Champs Auto Service

## Mike Kowalski — Owner / Operator

Mike founded Champs Auto Service in 2010 with a mission to provide honest, quality auto repair for Chicago drivers. Over 20 years in the automotive industry.

## Andre Williams — ASE Master Technician

Andre holds ASE Master Technician certification with expertise in engine repair, transmission service, brakes, suspension, and electrical systems. 18 years of experience.

## Jose Hernandez — ASE Certified Technician

Jose specializes in diagnostics, exhaust systems, and diesel engine repair. ASE Certified with 10 years of experience.

## Patricia Nguyen — Service Writer

Patricia manages customer relations, scheduling, and service coordination. She helps customers understand their vehicle needs and repair options.

## Tom Grady — Fleet Services Coordinator

Tom handles commercial fleet accounts, preventive maintenance scheduling, and priority service coordination for business customers.
