# Services - Champs Auto Service

Champs Auto Service (Champ Auto Repairs) provides comprehensive automotive care for all makes and models, including diesel engines. All work performed by ASE Master Certified technicians.

## Oil Changes

Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic oil changes using Mobil 1 oil. Includes filter replacement and fluid level check.

## Brake Repair

Complete brake system service including inspections, pad and rotor replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid exchange.

## Transmission Service

Diagnostics, fluid changes, repairs, and rebuilds for automatic and manual transmissions.

## Engine Diagnostics and Repair

Check engine light diagnosis, misfire troubleshooting, emissions issue resolution, and complete engine repair.

## Tune-Ups

Comprehensive performance optimization including spark plug replacement, fuel system cleaning, and ignition system service.

## Exhaust System Repair

Muffler replacement, exhaust pipe repair, catalytic converter service, and exhaust system diagnostics.

## Power Steering Service

Steering system maintenance and repair including fluid flush, pump replacement, and rack service.

## Fleet Services

Preventive maintenance programs, repairs, and priority scheduling for commercial fleets. Customized maintenance plans to minimize downtime.

## Pre-Purchase Inspections

Thorough vehicle condition evaluation for used car buyers, covering engine, transmission, brakes, suspension, and overall vehicle health.

## Tire Services

Winter tire installation and removal, TPMS service, tire rotation, balancing, and tire pressure maintenance.

## Suspension Services

Suspension system repair and maintenance including shock and strut replacement, alignment, and steering component service.
