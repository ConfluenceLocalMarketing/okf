# Montana Fence

Montana Fence is a locally owned and operated fence contractor serving the state of Montana since 1977. The company provides professional fence installation, repair, and retail services across four locations.

> "Built by Montanans for Montanans"

## History

Founded in 1977, Montana Fence has served Montana homeowners and businesses for over four decades. The company has grown from a single location to four branches covering the major population centers of the state.

## Services

Montana Fence offers a full range of fencing solutions:

- **Commercial fencing** - Security fencing, industrial enclosures, and property perimeter systems for businesses and institutions.
- **Residential fencing** - Custom privacy fencing, yard enclosures, and decorative fencing for homes.
- **Fence repair** - Repair and restoration services for existing fences of all types.
- **Retail fencing** - Direct-to-consumer fencing materials and supplies for DIY projects.
- **Free estimates** - On-site consultations and no-obligation quotes for all projects.
