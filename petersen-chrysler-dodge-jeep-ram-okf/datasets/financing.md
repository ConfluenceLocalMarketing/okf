# Petersen Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram Finance Center

Petersen CDJR provides comprehensive financing solutions including commercial vehicle plans, first-time buyer programs, and lease options.

## Financing Options

### New Vehicle Financing
- Competitive rates on new CDJR models
- Flexible terms
- Seasonal specials and rebates

### Used Vehicle Financing
- Financing on pre-owned inventory
- Wide range of loan programs
- Certified pre-owned financing

### All-Credit Programs
- **Excellent Credit** — Premier rates
- **Good Credit** — Standard financing
- **Fair Credit** — Flexible terms
- **Bad Credit / No Credit** — First-time buyer programs and credit-building options

### Commercial Financing
- Business vehicle loans and leases
- Fleet purchase options
- Work truck and van financing
- Commercial account terms

## Buying vs Leasing

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Full ownership | Return or buyout |
| Mileage Limits | Unlimited | Restricted |
| Customization | Allowed | Limited |
| Trade-In Equity | Yes | At lease end only |

## Online Tools

- Online credit application with pre-approval
- Trade-in appraisal tool
- Payment calculator

## Trade-In Program

- Appraise your vehicle online or in person
- All makes and models accepted
- Trade equity applied toward purchase
