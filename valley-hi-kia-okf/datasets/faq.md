# Valley Hi Kia — FAQ

## General

### What type of dealership is Valley Hi Kia?
It is a premier Kia dealership located at 14644 Valley Center Drive, Victorville, CA, serving the High Desert and Inland Empire with new Kia sales, pre-owned vehicles, and expert service. See [company.md](company.md) for the full profile.

### What area does the dealership serve?
Victorville, Apple Valley, Hesperia, Adelanto, Barstow, and the greater High Desert and Inland Empire communities.

### How can I contact the dealership?
Call (760) 983-7146 or email valleyhikia1@gmail.com.

## Sales

### What new Kia models are available?
The full Kia lineup including K4 and K5 sedans, Soul and Sportage compact SUVs, Sorento midsize SUV, Telluride three-row SUV, and Carnival minivan.

### What is the K4?
The K4 is Kia's newest sedan, featuring a bold design and available in multiple trims including GT-Line Turbo.

### Is the Telluride available?
Yes, the Telluride three-row SUV is available, offering seating for up to eight with family-friendly features and capability.
