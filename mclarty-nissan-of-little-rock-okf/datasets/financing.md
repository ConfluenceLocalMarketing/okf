# McLarty Nissan of Little Rock Finance Center

McLarty Nissan offers competitive financing and online buying options for customers throughout central Arkansas.

## Financing Options

### New Nissan Financing
- Low APR promotions on select Nissan models
- Competitive rates through multiple lenders
- Flexible terms

### Used Vehicle Financing
- Financing for pre-owned and certified vehicles
- Price-beat guarantee (10% extra on identical vehicles)
- Wide range of loan programs

### All-Credit Programs
- Programs for excellent, good, fair, and bad credit
- Online pre-approval
- First-time buyer assistance

## Buying vs Leasing

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Term | 36–84 months | 24–48 months |
| Ownership | Full ownership at end | Return or buyout |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Fixed limit |
| Customization | Allowed | Not permitted |

## Online Buying

- Shop and buy entirely online
- Home delivery options available
- Transparent pricing with price-beat guarantee

## Trade-In Program

- Online trade-in valuation
- Competitive offers
- Trade equity applied to purchase
