# New Chevrolet, Buick & GMC Models - Haselwood Chevrolet

Haselwood Chevrolet carries a full lineup of new Chevrolet, Buick, and GMC vehicles. Below is a summary of available models.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Chevrolet Trax
Subcompact SUV, Chevrolet's most affordable SUV. Redesigned for 2026 with bold styling, more space, and smart tech. Turbocharged engine with automatic transmission. Seats 5 passengers.

### Chevrolet Trailblazer
Subcompact SUV with sporty RS trim available. Compact footprint with available AWD. Perfect for city driving with SUV versatility.

### Chevrolet Equinox
Compact SUV, popular family choice. Available in multiple trims including LT, RS, and Premier. Efficient turbocharged engine. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Chevrolet Equinox EV
All-electric compact SUV. Modern design with impressive range. Available in multiple configurations with FWD or AWD. Advanced Ultium platform.

### Chevrolet Blazer
Mid-size SUV with bold styling. Available in LT, RS, and Premier trims. Multiple engine options including available V6. Sporty handling.

### Chevrolet Blazer EV
All-electric mid-size SUV built on the Ultium platform. Modern design with advanced technology. Available in multiple trims.

### Chevrolet Traverse
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in LS, LT, RS, Z71, and High Country trims. Powerful V6 engine with 9-speed automatic. Available in FWD and AWD.

### Chevrolet Tahoe
Full-size SUV seating up to 9 passengers. Available in LS, LT, RST, Z71, Premier, and High Country trims. Multiple engine options including V8. Maximum towing capacity.

### Chevrolet Suburban
Full-size extended SUV, the longest-running nameplate in automotive history. Seating up to 9 passengers. Available in same trims as Tahoe with additional cargo space.

### Buick Enclave
Premium mid-size 3-row SUV. Luxury interior with quiet tuning. Available in preferred trim levels.

### Buick Envision
Compact luxury SUV. Premium styling and refined ride. Available with AWD.

### Buick Encore GX
Subcompact luxury SUV. Efficient and city-friendly with upscale interior.

## Trucks

### Chevrolet Colorado
Mid-size pickup truck available in WT, LT, Z71, ZR2, and Trail Boss trims. Turbocharged 2.7L 4-cylinder engine (up to 310 hp) with 8-speed automatic. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Maximum towing capacity.

### Chevrolet Silverado 1500
Full-size pickup, Chevrolet's best-selling vehicle. Available in WT, Custom, LT, RST, LTZ, ZR2, and High Country trims. Multiple engine options including turbo 4-cylinder, V8, and Diesel. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Maximum towing capacity up to 13,300 lbs.

### Chevrolet Silverado 2500HD / 3500HD
Heavy-duty pickup trucks for maximum capability. Available in WT, LT, LTZ, and High Country trims. Powerful 6.6L V8 gas or Duramax diesel engine. Maximum towing capacity over 20,000 lbs (3500HD).

### Chevrolet Silverado EV
All-electric full-size pickup. Available in WT and RST trims. Ultium platform with up to 400 miles estimated range. Multi-Flex Midgate for extended cargo. Up to 10,000 lbs towing capacity.

### Chevrolet Express Cargo
Full-size cargo van for commercial use. Available in 2500 and 3500 configurations. Multiple roof and wheelbase options.

### GMC Sierra 1500
Full-size pickup, premium counterpart to Silverado. Available in Pro, SLE, Elevation, SLT, AT4, and Denali trims.

## Cars & Performance

### Chevrolet Malibu
Mid-size sedan, comfortable and fuel-efficient. Available in LS, RS, and Premier trims. Standard 1.5L turbocharged engine with CVT.

### Chevrolet Camaro
Iconic muscle car available in LT, LT1, and SS trims. Powerful V6 or V8 engines. Available as coupe or convertible.

### Chevrolet Corvette Stingray
Mid-engine sports car, America's supercar. 6.2L V8 engine with 490 hp. Available as coupe or convertible with Z51 performance package.

### Chevrolet Corvette E-Ray
Hybrid all-wheel-drive Corvette. Combined 655 hp. Electric front axle for AWD traction and quick acceleration.

### Chevrolet Corvette ZO6
High-performance Corvette with naturally aspirated 5.5L V8 engine producing 670 hp. Track-focused design and aerodynamics.

## Electric Vehicles

### Chevrolet Bolt EV / Bolt EUV
All-electric subcompact hatchback/SUV. Affordable EV option with impressive range. Available in 2027 model year at Haselwood.

### Chevrolet Equinox EV
All-electric compact SUV with Ultium platform.

### Chevrolet Blazer EV
All-electric mid-size SUV with Ultium platform.

### Chevrolet Silverado EV
All-electric full-size pickup with Ultium platform.
