# Pre-Owned and CarBravo Certified Vehicles - Haselwood Chevrolet

Haselwood Chevrolet maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in Bremerton with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (hundreds in stock)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Volkswagen, Jeep, Ram, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles, including an Economy Vehicle Program
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, coupes, and sports cars
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover
- **Carfax One Owner vehicles** available

## Quality Assurance

- **Vehicle Inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **CarBravo Certified** - Chevrolet's certified pre-owned program with factory-backed inspections and warranties
- **Service Contracts** - Available for most vehicles
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request
- **Economy Vehicle Program** - Safety-inspected, affordable cars for first-time buyers or budget-conscious families

## CarBravo Program

CarBravo is Chevrolet's certified pre-owned program offering:
- Factory-backed warranty coverage
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Convenient online shopping at carbravo.haselwood.com
