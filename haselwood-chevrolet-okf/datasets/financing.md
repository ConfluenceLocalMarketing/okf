# Financing & Leasing

The Haselwood Chevrolet Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for new and pre-owned vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions for different ownership goals and budgets
- **Capital One Pre-Qualification**: Check rates without impacting your credit score

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Online valuation tool via KBB Instant Cash Offer and in-person appraisal available at the dealership.

## Payment Calculators

Estimate monthly payments using the online car payment calculator on the dealership website.
