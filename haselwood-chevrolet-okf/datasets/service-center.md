# Service Center - Haselwood Chevrolet

The Haselwood Chevrolet Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 501 West Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work, AC repair
- **Mobile Service** - Service van comes to your home or office
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services including rotation and balancing
- **Battery Service** - Testing and replacement
- **Brake Service** - Inspection, repair, and replacement
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Chevrolet's recommended maintenance schedule
- **EV Charging Station** - On-site charging available for electric vehicles
