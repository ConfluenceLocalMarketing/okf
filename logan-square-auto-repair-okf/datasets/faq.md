# Frequently Asked Questions - Logan Square Auto Repair

## What services does Logan Square Auto Repair offer?

We offer engine repair, brakes, oil changes, suspension, tires, A/C, electrical, emissions, transmission repair, and advanced diagnostics. See [services.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/logan-square-auto-repair-okf/datasets/services.md) for full details.

## How long have you been in business?

Logan Square Auto Repair was established in 1997, serving Chicago's Logan Square and Bucktown neighborhoods for over 25 years.

## Are you ASE Certified?

Yes. Our technicians are ASE Certified. We are also AAA Approved, BBB Accredited, and a NAPA AutoCare Center. See [company.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/logan-square-auto-repair-okf/datasets/company.md) for all certifications.

## Do you service luxury and import vehicles?

Yes. We service a wide range including Acura, Audi, BMW, Jaguar, Land Rover, Lexus, Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, Tesla, Volkswagen, Volvo, and more.

## Do you offer the same ownership as Avondale Auto Repair?

Yes. Logan Square Auto Repair and Avondale Auto Repair share the same family ownership. See [company.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/logan-square-auto-repair-okf/datasets/company.md) for details.

## How do I schedule an appointment?

Call us at (773) 772-1639. Walk-ins welcome.

## What are your hours?

Monday through Friday 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, Saturday 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. Closed Sunday.

## Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. We provide a 24-month / 24,000-mile NAPA Peace of Mind warranty honored nationwide.

## Do AAA members receive a discount?

Yes. AAA members receive a 10% labor discount.

## Do you perform transmission repairs?

Yes. We offer automatic and manual transmission service. Our technicians are ATRA affiliated (Automatic Transmission Rebuilders Association).

## Do you work on Tesla vehicles?

Yes. We service Tesla and other electric vehicles at our facility.

## Do you perform emissions testing and repair?

Yes. We offer emissions diagnostics and repair to help your vehicle pass Illinois emissions testing.
