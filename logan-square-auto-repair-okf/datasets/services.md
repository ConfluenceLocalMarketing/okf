# Services - Logan Square Auto Repair

Logan Square Auto Repair offers comprehensive automotive services for a wide range of vehicles including luxury and import makes. All work is performed by ASE Certified technicians using state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment.

## Engine Repair and Diagnostics

Full engine service including diagnostics, check engine light analysis, and complete engine repair for domestic and foreign vehicles.

## Brake Repair

Complete brake system service including inspection, pad replacement, rotor service, caliper repair, and brake fluid exchange.

## Oil Change

Professional oil and filter service. Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic options.

## Suspension and Steering

Ride quality and handling repairs including shock and strut replacement, alignment, and steering system service.

## Tire Service and Repair

Tire sales, rotation, balancing, mounting, and flat repair.

## A/C and Heating

Climate system diagnostics, A/C recharge, compressor replacement, and heater service.

## Electrical Services

Battery testing, alternator repair, starter service, and electrical system diagnostics.

## Emissions Repair

Emissions testing, diagnostics, and repair for Illinois compliance.

## Transmission Repair

Automatic and manual transmission service, diagnostics, fluid changes, and repairs. ATRA affiliated.

## Diagnostics

State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment for accurate and efficient problem identification across all vehicle systems.
