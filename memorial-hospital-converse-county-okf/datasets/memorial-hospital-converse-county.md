# Memorial Hospital of Converse County

## Overview

Memorial Hospital of Converse County (MHCC) is a 25-bed critical access hospital established in 1969. Headquartered at 111 South 5th Street, Douglas, WY 82633, the non-profit organization employs approximately 180 staff across 16 locations throughout Wyoming.
