# Chaparral Buick GMC

Chaparral Buick GMC is a family-owned and operated dealership serving the Tri-Cities of Johnson City, Bristol, and Kingsport, Tennessee. Known as "The Heart of the Motor Mile," the dealership operates with a "No Bull" philosophy focused on pricing transparency and low-pressure sales.

- **Buick:** Full lineup of SUVs and crossovers
- **GMC:** Sierra trucks, Terrain, Acadia, Yukon
- Large selection of new cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans

## Pre-Owned Vehicles

- Wide variety of used cars, trucks, and SUVs
- Accurate inventory details for out-of-town buyers
- Vehicle history reports
