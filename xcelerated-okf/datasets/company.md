# Xcelerated Data

Xcelerated (Xcelerated Data) is a consumer data platform that delivers fully identified, verified, and enriched consumer records for businesses, with a specialization in automotive data solutions. The company has over 30 years of experience in automotive and consumer data.

## Data Solutions

- **Automotive Consumer Data**: Vehicle-linked consumer records for targeting
- **Data Cleaning and Hygiene**: DNC scrub, deduplication, standardization
- **Data Append and Enrichment**: Household income, demographics, lifestyle
- **Consumer Data Solutions**: Custom audience targeting
- **New Movers and Escrow Data**: Fresh mover audiences
- **Identity Resolution**: Cross-channel identity matching
- **DataVast**: Large-scale data marketplace
