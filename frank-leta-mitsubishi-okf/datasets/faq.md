# Frank Leta Mitsubishi FAQ

## Vehicles

**Q: What new Mitsubishi models do you carry?**
A: We carry the full Mitsubishi lineup: Outlander, Outlander PHEV, Eclipse Cross, Outlander Sport, and Mirage. See [new-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/frank-leta-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/new-vehicles.md) for details.

**Q: Do you offer a test drive?**
A: Yes, test drives are available for all new and used vehicles at our Bridgeton location. Schedule online or walk in.

**Q: What used vehicles do you have?**
A: We carry a wide selection of used cars, trucks, and SUVs across all makes and models. Every vehicle passes a 150-point inspection. See [used-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/frank-leta-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/used-vehicles.md).

**Q: Do you offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Not a formal CPO program — every used vehicle undergoes a thorough 150-point inspection with the Leta-Exclusive Protection warranty.

## Financing

**Q: Can I get financing with bad credit?**
A: Yes. We work with over 40 lenders and offer special programs for bad credit, no credit, and all credit situations. See [financing.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/frank-leta-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/financing.md).

**Q: How much down payment do you need?**
A: Qualified buyers can get into a vehicle for as low as $29 down, with up to $25,000 in available credit.

**Q: Do you offer online pre-approval?**
A: Yes, you can apply for pre-approval on our website. It is fast and does not affect your credit score.

**Q: What is a credit amnesty program?**
A: We offer credit amnesty programs that help buyers get approved despite past credit issues like repossession or bankruptcy.

**Q: Do you offer leases?**
A: Yes, competitive lease terms are available on new Mitsubishi models. See [new-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/frank-leta-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/new-vehicles.md) for current offers.

## Service

**Q: What services does your center provide?**
A: Full-service auto repair, routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations, brake service), tire sales, and diagnostics. See [service-center.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/frank-leta-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/service-center.md).

**Q: Do you service non-Mitsubishi vehicles?**
A: Yes, our certified technicians service all makes and models.

**Q: What warranty does Mitsubishi offer?**
A: New Mitsubishi vehicles come with a 10-year/100,000-mile Powertrain Limited Warranty and 5 years of roadside assistance.

**Q: Do I need an appointment for service?**
A: Appointments are recommended but walk-ins are welcome. Schedule online or call (314) 597-9221.
