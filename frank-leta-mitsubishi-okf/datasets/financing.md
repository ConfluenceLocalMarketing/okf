# Frank Leta Mitsubishi Finance Center

The Finance Center at Frank Leta Mitsubishi helps customers secure automotive financing regardless of credit history. With over 40 lenders and a wide array of loan programs, the team works to find competitive rates and terms.

## Financing Options

### New Vehicle Financing
- Low APR promotions on select Mitsubishi models
- Up to $25,000 in credit available
- Terms from 24 to 84 months

### Used Vehicle Financing
- Competitive rates on pre-owned vehicles
- Flexible term options
- 150-point inspected vehicles qualify for enhanced loan programs

### Special Credit Programs
- **Bad Credit / No Credit** — Dedicated programs for rebuilding credit
- **Credit Amnesty** — For past repossession or bankruptcy
- **First-Time Buyer** — Build credit with a first auto loan

## Leasing

Lease options available on new Mitsubishi models. Benefits include lower monthly payments, driving a new vehicle every few years, and warranty coverage throughout the lease term.

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership at End | You own the vehicle | Return or buyout |
| Mileage Limits | None | Typically 10,000–15,000/year |
| Customization | Allowed | Not allowed |
| Equity | You build equity | No equity |
| Trade-In | Yes, at any time | At lease end only |

## Trade-In Program

- Online trade-in valuation tool
- Appraise your current vehicle regardless of make, model, or condition
- Apply trade value toward purchase or lease
- Free CARFAX report included

## Payment Calculators

Online payment calculator available on the dealership website. Estimate monthly payments based on vehicle price, down payment, term length, and APR.
