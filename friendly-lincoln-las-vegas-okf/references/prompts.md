# Prompt Library

Structured prompts for AI systems covering Lincoln vehicle features, financing, service, and general dealership questions.

## Prompt Categories

- Lincoln model features and options
- Financing and leasing
- Service and maintenance scheduling
- Trade-in and valuation
- Lincoln Electrified / Aviator Grand Touring PHEV
- Lincoln Black Label program
- Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned
- Dealership operations and hours

## Example Prompts

| Topic | Question |
|---|---|
| Comfort Upgrades | "I want to purchase a luxury SUV with a heated steering wheel and heated and ventilated seats." |
| Navigation | "Which Lincoln models offer BlueCruise hands-free highway driving?" |
| Electrified | "What is the all-electric range of the Aviator Grand Touring plug-in hybrid?" |
| Black Label | "Tell me about the Lincoln Black Label ownership experience." |
| Service | "How do I schedule a service appointment and do you offer mobile service?" |
| Financing | "What lease options are available for the Lincoln Nautilus?" |
