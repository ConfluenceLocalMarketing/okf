# Team Lincoln Las Vegas Website

The Friendly Lincoln dealership operates its online presence at https://www.teamlincolnlasvegas.com/. The website provides full dealership capabilities including inventory browsing, service scheduling, credit applications, and trade-in valuation.

## Site Features

- New Lincoln vehicle inventory with photos, specs, and pricing
- Pre-owned inventory search
- Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned listings
- Service center appointment scheduling
- Online credit application
- Value Your Trade tool
- Special offers and incentives
- Lincoln model research and comparison
