# Friendly Lincoln Service Center

Friendly Lincoln's service center is staffed by Lincoln factory-trained technicians offering routine maintenance, complex diagnostics, and repairs for all Lincoln models.

## Service Offerings

- Routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations, fluid checks)
- Complex diagnostics and engine/transmission repair
- Genuine OEM Lincoln parts
- Lincoln Pickup & Delivery — complimentary vehicle pick-up and drop-off
- Lincoln Mobile Service — factory-trained technicians dispatched to your home or office
- Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned warranty repairs
- Online service scheduling

## Lincoln Mobile Service

Select maintenance services performed at your home or office by factory-trained technicians, eliminating the need to visit the dealership.
