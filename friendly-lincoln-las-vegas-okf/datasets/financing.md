# Financing & Leasing

Friendly Lincoln Las Vegas offers purchase financing and luxury leasing programs for new and pre-owned Lincoln vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home via the online credit application
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: The finance team works with multiple lenders to find competitive APRs
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan solutions designed for different ownership goals and budgets
- **Protection Products**: Extended warranties and protection plans available

## Luxury Leasing

Lincoln lease programs offer lower monthly payments with the flexibility to drive a new Lincoln every few years. Lincoln Red Carpet Lease provides:
- Competitive lease rates on all Lincoln models
- Flexibility at lease end (purchase, return, or trade)
- Gap protection available

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Customization | Full freedom | Limited during lease term |

## Trade-In

Get an online valuation through the Value Your Trade tool on the dealership website, or bring your vehicle in for an in-person appraisal.

## Payment Calculator

Estimate monthly payments using the online payment calculator before visiting the dealership.
