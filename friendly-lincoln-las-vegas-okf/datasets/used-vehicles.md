## Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned Program

Every Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicle includes:

- 200-point vehicle inspection
- Comprehensive limited warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Complimentary Lincoln Pickup & Delivery for covered repairs
- Vehicle history report
- Rental car coverage for covered repairs

## Pre-Owned Luxury Inventory

Quality pre-owned vehicles receive a multi-point inspection and are competitively priced. Inventory spans Lincoln, Ford, and other luxury makes.
