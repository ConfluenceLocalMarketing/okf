# Frequently Asked Questions

## Vehicles & Inventory

**Q: What Lincoln models are available at Friendly Lincoln Las Vegas?**
A: The dealership carries the full Lincoln SUV lineup including Corsair, Nautilus, Aviator, Navigator, and Navigator L.

**Q: Is the Aviator available as a plug-in hybrid?**
A: Yes. The Aviator Grand Touring is a plug-in hybrid with an estimated 21 miles of all-electric range.

**Q: Do you have pre-owned vehicles from other manufacturers?**
A: Yes. The pre-owned inventory includes luxury vehicles from multiple brands.

**Q: What is the Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned program?**
A: It includes a 200-point inspection, comprehensive warranty, 24/7 roadside assistance, and complimentary Lincoln Pickup & Delivery.

**Q: What is the Lincoln Black Label program?**
A: Black Label is a premium ownership program with curated trim themes, complimentary maintenance, vehicle exchange privileges, and 24/7 concierge service.

## Financing

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes. Complete the online credit application from the dealership website.

**Q: Do you offer both buying and leasing options?**
A: Yes. The finance center provides both purchase financing and luxury lease programs.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes. Use the Value Your Trade tool on the dealership website for instant online valuation.

**Q: What credit situations do you work with?**
A: The finance team works with multiple lending partners to accommodate various credit profiles.

## Service

**Q: Do you use genuine OEM parts?**
A: Yes. The service department uses only genuine OEM Lincoln parts for all repairs.

**Q: What is Lincoln Pickup & Delivery?**
A: Complimentary vehicle pick-up and drop-off for service appointments within the service area.

**Q: What is Lincoln Mobile Service?**
A: Factory-trained technicians dispatched to your home or office for select maintenance services.

**Q: Can I schedule service online?**
A: Yes. Use the online service scheduling tool on the dealership website.
