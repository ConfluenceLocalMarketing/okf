# Financing & Leasing - Nick Mayer Lincoln Mayfield

The Finance Center at Nick Mayer Lincoln Mayfield helps customers secure competitive financing and leasing options for new Lincoln and pre-owned vehicles.

## Financing Options

- **Online Credit Application** — Secure online application with quick pre-approval decisions
- **Multiple Lending Partners** — Network of banks, credit unions, and financial institutions for competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms** — Customized payment plans from 24 to 84 months
- **New Vehicle Financing** — Special rates and incentives on current Lincoln models
- **Used Vehicle Financing** — Competitive rates on Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned and pre-owned inventory
- **Special Financing Programs** — Options for first-time buyers, recent graduates, and buyers with less-than-perfect credit

## Leasing

Lincoln vehicles are especially popular for leasing, offering luxury features at accessible monthly payments. The dealership offers seasonal Lincoln Red Carpet Lease specials and pull-ahead programs for current Lincoln lessees.

### Benefits of Leasing

- Lower monthly payments compared to financing
- Lower down payment requirements
- Drive a new Lincoln every 2-3 years
- Factory warranty coverage for the lease term
- Lincoln complimentary scheduled maintenance during lease

## Buying vs Leasing

| Factor | Buying (Financing) | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Down Payment | Variable | Typically lower |
| Mileage Limits | Unlimited | Limited (typically 10,000-15,000 mi/year) |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Return at lease end |
| Maintenance Costs | Out of warranty | Covered under factory warranty |
| Customization | Allowed | Restricted |
| End of Term | Vehicle is yours | Option to purchase or return |

## Trade-In Appraisal

The dealership offers online and in-person trade-in valuation services. Customers can use the Value Your Trade tool on the website to receive an instant preliminary offer, then bring the vehicle in for a final appraisal.

## Payment Calculators

Online payment calculators allow customers to estimate monthly payments based on vehicle price, down payment, trade-in value, APR, and loan term.
