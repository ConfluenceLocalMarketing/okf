The service center at Nick Mayer Lincoln Mayfield provides factory-authorized maintenance and repairs using genuine Lincoln parts and certified technicians.

- Online scheduling for convenience
- Certified Lincoln technicians with ongoing factory training
- Genuine Lincoln parts and accessories
- Multi-point vehicle inspection with every service visit

- **Lincoln Valet** — Complimentary pick-up and drop-off service within the service area
