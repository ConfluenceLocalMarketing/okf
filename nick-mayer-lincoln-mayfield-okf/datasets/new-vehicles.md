# Lincoln New Vehicle Lineup

Nick Mayer Lincoln Mayfield offers the full Lincoln lineup of luxury SUVs, each available in multiple trims and optionally through the Lincoln Black Label program for premium ownership experiences.

## SUV Models

### Corsair

The Lincoln Corsair is a compact luxury SUV offering a refined ride, available all-wheel drive, and Lincoln's signature Quiet Flight design philosophy. It seats up to five passengers and features a 2.0L 250-horsepower engine with available plug-in hybrid powertrain.

- Body style: Compact SUV
- Passengers: 5
- Drivetrain: FWD / AWD
- Starting MSRP: $40,000+
- Key feature: Available Lincoln Co-Pilot360 driver assist suite

### Nautilus

The Lincoln Nautilus is a mid-size luxury SUV with available SYNC 4 technology, 8-speed SelectShift automatic transmission, and a 2.0L turbocharged engine. It seats five passengers with available all-wheel drive.

- Body style: Mid-size SUV
- Passengers: 5
- Drivetrain: FWD / AWD
- Starting MSRP: $50,000+
- Key feature: SYNC 4 with available 24-inch panoramic display

### Aviator

The Lincoln Aviator is a mid-size three-row luxury SUV offering seating for up to seven passengers. It features a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 engine and is available as a plug-in hybrid (Grand Touring model). Rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations are available.

- Body style: Mid-size 3-Row SUV
- Passengers: 6-7
- Drivetrain: RWD / AWD
- Starting MSRP: $55,000+
- Key feature: Available plug-in hybrid powertrain

### Navigator

The Lincoln Navigator is a full-size luxury SUV with seating for up to eight passengers. It features a 3.5L twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission and standard 4WD. The hands-free liftgate and Lincoln's Perfect Position front seats are hallmark features.

- Body style: Full-size SUV
- Passengers: 7-8
- Drivetrain: 4WD
- Starting MSRP: $90,000+
- Key feature: 3.5L twin-turbo V6 with 10-speed automatic transmission
