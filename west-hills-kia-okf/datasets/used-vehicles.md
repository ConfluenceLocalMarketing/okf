# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - West Hills Kia

West Hills Kia maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (hundreds in stock)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Kia, Chevrolet, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, coupes, and EVs
- **Vehicles under $20,000** - Dedicated budget-friendly selection
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover

## Quality Assurance

- **Multi-Point Inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **Kia Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance
- **Vehicle Service Contracts** - Available for most vehicles to prevent unexpected expenses
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Kia Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- Kia factory-backed limited warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified Kia technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- Value Your Trade tool (KBB Instant Cash Offer)
- Car payment calculator
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Vehicle hold policy
- Vehicle Upgrade Program

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal available (KBB Instant Cash Offer)
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
- Competitive trade-in values
- Sell your car online tool
