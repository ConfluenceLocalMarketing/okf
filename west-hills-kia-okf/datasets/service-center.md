# Service Center - West Hills Kia

The West Hills Kia Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 515 West Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **EV Maintenance** - Specialized electric vehicle service and battery health checks
- **Hybrid Service** - Hybrid system diagnostics and maintenance
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services including balancing
- **Brake Service** - Pad replacement, rotor replacement, fluid flush
- **Battery Service** - Testing, replacement, and electrical system diagnostics
- **A/C Service** - Air conditioning repair and maintenance
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Kia's recommended maintenance schedule
