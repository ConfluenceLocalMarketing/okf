# New Kia Models - West Hills Kia

West Hills Kia carries a full lineup of new Kia vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## Electric Vehicles

### Kia EV9
Three-row all-electric SUV, Kia's flagship EV. Up to 304 miles estimated range. Ultra-fast 800V DC charging (10-80% in ~24 minutes). Available in Light, Wind, Land, and GT-Line trims. Available in RWD and e-AWD. Seats up to 7 passengers. Towing capacity up to 5,000 lbs.

### Kia EV6
All-electric crossover with sporty styling and performance. Up to 310 miles estimated range. 800V DC fast-charging capable. Available in RWD and dual-motor e-AWD. GT version delivers 576 hp.

### Kia Niro EV
Compact all-electric crossover. Practical range for daily commuting. Available in Wind and Wave trims. Ideal for Kitsap County drivers making the switch to electric.

## Hybrid Vehicles

### Kia Sportage Hybrid
Compact SUV with excellent fuel efficiency. Available in LX, EX, and SX Prestige trims. Combined ~43 MPG estimated. Standard AWD.

### Kia Sorento Hybrid
Three-row SUV with hybrid efficiency. Seats up to 7 passengers. Available in S, EX, and SX Prestige trims. Excellent fuel economy for a three-row SUV.

### Kia Niro Hybrid
Compact crossover designed for outstanding MPG - up to 53 city / 54 highway. Available in LX, EX, and SX Touring trims.

### Kia Carnival Hybrid
Minivan versatility with hybrid efficiency. Seats up to 8 passengers. 33 MPG combined, 242 hp. Perfect for growing families in Kitsap County.

### Kia Telluride Hybrid
Award-winning three-row SUV now available with hybrid powertrain. Upscale interior and advanced technology.

## Plug-In Hybrid Vehicles

### Kia Sportage Plug-In Hybrid
Compact SUV PHEV with ~34 miles all-electric range. Combined 470+ miles total range. Available in X-Line and X-Line Prestige trims.

### Kia Sorento Plug-In Hybrid
Three-row SUV PHEV with ~32 miles all-electric range. Seats up to 6 passengers.

### Kia Niro Plug-In Hybrid
Compact crossover PHEV with ~33 miles all-electric range. Outstanding total range.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Kia Telluride
Award-winning three-row SUV. Available in LX, S, EX, SX, and SX Prestige trims. Powered by 3.8L V6 engine (291 hp). Seats up to 8 passengers. Towing capacity up to 5,000 lbs.

### Kia Sorento
Flexible three-row SUV available with gas, hybrid, and plug-in hybrid powertrains. Available in LX, S, EX, SX, and SX Prestige trims. Seats up to 7 passengers.

### Kia Sportage
Versatile compact SUV available in gas, hybrid, and plug-in hybrid versions. Redesigned interior with dual 12.3-inch displays. Available AWD.

### Kia Seltos
Stylish subcompact SUV. Available in LX, S, EX, and SX trims. Available AWD. Perfect entry-level SUV.

## Sedans

### Kia K4
All-new compact sedan replacing the Forte. Modern design with advanced technology. Available in LX, EX, and GT-Line trims.

### Kia K5
Mid-size sedan with bold design. Available in LX, EX, GT-Line, and GT trims. GT version features 2.5L turbo engine (290 hp).

## Vans

### Kia Carnival
Multi-purpose passenger van. Available in LX, EX, SX, and SX Prestige trims. Powered by 3.5L V6 engine. Seats up to 8 passengers. Sliding doors for easy access.
