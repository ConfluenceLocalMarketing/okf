# West Hills Kia Finance Center

The West Hills Kia Finance Center offers comprehensive financing solutions for new and pre-owned Kia vehicles, serving customers throughout Kitsap County, Silverdale, Port Orchard, Poulsbo, and the greater Seattle area.

See [dealership.md](dealership.md) for full business profile.

## Financing Options

- **Online Credit Application** - Secure pre-approval from home before visiting the dealership
- **Multiple Lending Partners** - Network of banks, credit unions, and financial institutions for competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms** - Customized loan terms to fit budget and credit situation
- **New Vehicle Financing** - Special Kia manufacturer rates and incentives, including 0% APR offers on select models
- **Pre-Owned Financing** - Competitive rates on certified pre-owned and used vehicles
- **First-Time Buyer Programs** - Credit-building opportunities for those with limited history
- **Vehicle Protection Plans** - Extended warranties, GAP coverage, and tire-and-wheel protection

## Leasing

- Low monthly payments with option to purchase at lease end
- Flexible mileage allowances
- Pull-ahead programs for current Kia lessees
- Lease-end options: purchase, trade, or return

## Buying vs Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Dealership owns; you return it |
| Mileage Limits | Unlimited | Annual cap (typically 10,000-15,000 miles) |
| Customization | Fully customizable | Limited modifications |
| End of Term | Paid off; keep or trade | Return, purchase, or lease new |
| Warranty | After factory warranty expires | Typically covered by factory warranty |

## Trade-In

- Instant online trade-in valuation powered by Kelley Blue Book
- Competitive offers based on market value, condition, and mileage
- Trade equity can be applied toward down payment
- Vehicle Upgrade Program for current owners

## Payment Calculators

- Used vehicle payment estimator
