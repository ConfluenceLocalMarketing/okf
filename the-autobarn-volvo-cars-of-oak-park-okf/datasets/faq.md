# Autobarn Volvo Cars of Oak Park — FAQ

## General

### What type of dealership is Autobarn Volvo Cars of Oak Park?
It is a full-service Volvo dealership located at 1140 Garfield Street, Oak Park, IL, serving Chicagoland with new and certified pre-owned Volvo vehicles, factory-trained service, and genuine Volvo parts. See [company.md](the-autobarn-volvo-cars-of-oak-park-okf/datasets/company.md) for the full profile.

### What area does the dealership serve?
Oak Park and the greater Chicagoland area.

### What are the dealership hours?
Contact the dealership at (708) 786-6560 for current sales, service, and parts hours.

## Sales

### What new Volvo models are available?
The full Volvo lineup including S60 and S90 sedans; XC40, XC60, and XC90 SUVs; and the Recharge plug-in hybrid and fully electric models.

### What is the Volvo Recharge lineup?
Volvo Recharge encompasses the plug-in hybrid (PHEV) and fully electric (EV) models in the Volvo lineup, combining Scandinavian design with electrified powertrains.

### Do you offer certified pre-owned Volvos?
Yes. The dealership offers Volvo Certified Pre-Owned vehicles with factory-backed warranties, multi-point inspections, and roadside assistance.

## Service

### What services does the service center provide?
Routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations), major repairs, body work, and diagnostics — all performed by factory-trained Volvo technicians using genuine Volvo parts.

### Are the technicians Volvo-certified?
Yes, all technicians are factory-trained and specialize in Volvo vehicles.

### Do you use genuine Volvo parts?
Yes, only genuine OEM Volvo parts are used in all service and repair work.

## Financing

### What financing options are available?
Competitive financing and lease plans are available with online credit pre-approval and personalized budget solutions.

### Can I get pre-approved online?
Yes, online credit pre-approval is available through the dealership website.

## Parts

### Do you sell Volvo parts?
Yes, genuine Volvo parts are available through the service center.

### Can I order parts online?
Yes, the dealership operates the Volvo Parts Webstore at https://www.volvopartswebstore.com/ for OEM Volvo parts and accessories with nationwide shipping.
