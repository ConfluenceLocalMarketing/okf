# Apex Custom Innovations — Services

## Exterior Coatings

### Apex Armor™ Exterior Coating
Proprietary exterior coating available in custom colors that provides battle-ready finish protection. Ideal for lifted Jeeps, trucks, and off-road builds.

### Apex Signature Finish™ Exterior Coating
Premium exterior coating designed to improve gloss and surface durability. Ceramic coating application for long-term paint preservation.

## Paint Protection Film (PPF)
XPEL-certified installation facility. Services include clear bra PPF on high-impact zones, full-front coverage, and full-body coverage. Self-healing film technology protects against chips, scratches, and road debris. Available for headlights, windshield, and interior screens.

## Vinyl Wraps
Custom wrap solutions for full color changes, commercial branding, and personalized styling. Satin, matte, gloss, and specialty finishes. Seen on builds ranging from a galaxy-themed Acura NSX to a satin black Tesla Cybertruck.

## Window Tint
Premium ceramic window tint that blocks up to 99% of UV rays, reduces interior heat, minimizes glare, and adds privacy. Professional cut-to-fit, bubble-free installation.

## Suspension — Lift & Lower

### Lift Kits
Full suspension lifts for trucks, SUVs, and Jeeps. Premium brands: BDS Suspension, Rough Country, ReadyLIFT. Includes leveling kits, coilover upgrades. Matched to tire and wheel setup for proper geometry.

### Lowering Kits
For street trucks and sport builds seeking a lower stance with improved handling. Reduced body roll, tighter cornering, aggressive appearance.

### Leveling Kits
Eliminates factory rake (nose-down tilt). Cost-effective upgrade allowing slightly larger tires without a full lift.

## Wheels & Tires
Custom wheel and tire combinations tailored for appearance, traction, and performance. Brands seen in builds: Vossen, Fuel Off-Road, Nitto, Yokohama. Wheel offset and tire sizing matched to suspension setup.

## Bolt-On Accessories
- Custom bumpers and exterior accessories (utility, protection, appearance)
- Interior lighting and accent packages (ambient LED, off-road lighting)
- Roof racks, steps, and utility accessories for trucks and SUVs
- Audio and technology enhancements
- Lighting enhancements (LED systems, accent lighting, off-road lighting)

## Custom Build Projects
Full vehicle transformations designed around individual customer preferences. Bodywork, fabrication, interiors, paint, electronics. From street-focused show vehicles to off-road-ready trucks to fully personalized daily drivers.
