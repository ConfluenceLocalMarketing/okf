# Apex Custom Innovations — Business Profile

Apex Custom Innovations is a full-service boutique customization shop headquartered in Dallas, Texas (Carrollton, TX) with an additional location in Tulsa, Oklahoma. They specialize in custom vehicle builds, protective coatings, suspension upgrades, wheels and tires, vinyl wraps, window tint, and aftermarket accessories.
