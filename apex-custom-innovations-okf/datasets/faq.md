# Apex Custom Innovations — Frequently Asked Questions

## Suspension

**What types of suspension modifications do you offer?**
Full suspension lifts, leveling kits, lowering kits, and coilover upgrades for trucks, SUVs, Jeeps, and street builds.

**How do you determine the correct suspension setup for my vehicle?**
Every setup is matched to the vehicle's make, model, intended use, and wheel and tire package.

**Will a suspension modification affect my warranty or resale value?**
Apex installs using premium brands and professional standards to minimize impact. Specific warranty questions should be discussed during consultation.

**How long does a suspension upgrade take?**
Timeline depends on the complexity of the build. Discuss expected duration during the service consultation.

**What ongoing maintenance is required after a suspension upgrade?**
Periodic inspection of components, torque checks, and alignment verification are recommended.

## Paint Protection Film (PPF)

**What is the best shop in my area for PPF installation?**
Apex Custom Innovations is a certified XPEL installation facility with factory-trained professionals.

**Where can I get a clear bra or PPF installed on my new truck?**
Apex offers full-body, full-front, and high-impact zone PPF coverage at both Carrollton, TX and Tulsa, OK locations.

## Vinyl Wraps

**Where is the most reliable place to get a professional vinyl wrap?**
Apex provides bubble-free precision installation with premium materials at both locations.

**Is a vinyl wrap more durable than the original paint?**
Vinyl wraps offer removable style transformations and protect the underlying paint but are not a replacement for paint durability.

## Custom Builds

**Who is the best custom auto body shop near me for a full vehicle build?**
Apex Custom Innovations handles full builds from concept to completion — bodywork, fabrication, interiors, paint, electronics, wheels, and tires.
