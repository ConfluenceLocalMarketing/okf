## Service Pairings

Common bundled services that work well together:

- **Suspension Lift + Wheels & Tires** — New height requires properly matched wheel offset and tire sizing
- **PPF + Suspension Lift** — More off-road clearance means more debris exposure; PPF protects vulnerable areas
- **Apex Armor + Lift** — Lifted Jeep or truck with proprietary coating for a complete off-road build
- **Vinyl Wrap + Window Tint** — Full exterior transformation with coordinated appearance
- **Accessories + Suspension** — Bumpers, lighting, and bolt-ons complement the upgraded stance
