# Ron Marhofer Nissan - Finance Center

## Finance Center

The Finance Center at Ron Marhofer Nissan offers flexible financing and leasing solutions for new and pre-owned vehicles. The finance team works with over 30 lenders including Nissan Motor Acceptance Corporation (NMAC) and Capital One to secure competitive rates for customers across all credit profiles.

## Financing Options

- **New Vehicle Loans** - Competitive APR financing on all new Nissan models through NMAC
- **Used Vehicle Loans** - Rate financing on pre-owned inventory with flexible terms
- **Capital One Pre-Qualification** - Quick pre-qualification without impacting credit score
- **Special Finance Programs** - First-time buyer assistance, college graduate, and military programs
- **Online Credit Application** - Secure pre-approval from home
- **Credit-Challenged Programs** - Options for less-than-perfect credit through multiple lenders

## Leasing

Nissan lease programs offer lower monthly payments and the ability to drive a new Nissan every few years. Lease options include NMAC lease-end options and mileage flexibility.

## Buying vs Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Return at lease end |
| Mileage Limits | None | Annual mileage cap applies |
| Warranty Coverage | After factory warranty | Typically covered by warranty |
| End of Term | No further obligations | Option to buy or return |
| Equity Building | Yes | No |

## Trade-In

Get a value for your trade-in through the online appraisal tool or in-person evaluation. The dealership accepts all makes and models regardless of condition.

## Payment Calculators

Online payment calculator tools allow customers to estimate monthly payments based on vehicle price, down payment, trade-in value, APR, and loan term.
