# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Ron Marhofer Nissan

Ron Marhofer Nissan maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Cuyahoga Falls area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (hundreds in stock)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Nissan, Chevrolet, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Volkswagen, GMC, Jeep, Ram, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Land Rover, Mitsubishi, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover

## Quality Assurance

- **120+ Point Inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **Nissan Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed inspections, warranties, and roadside assistance
- **Vehicle Service Contracts** - Available for most vehicles to prevent unexpected expenses
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request

Nissan Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- Nissan factory-backed warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified Nissan technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments
