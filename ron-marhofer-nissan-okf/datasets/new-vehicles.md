# New Nissan Models - Ron Marhofer Nissan

Ron Marhofer Nissan carries a full lineup of new Nissan vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Nissan Rogue
Compact SUV, Nissan's best-selling model. Available trims include S, SV, Rock Creek, Midnight Edition, Platinum, and Dark Armor. Features the 1.5L VC-Turbo engine (201 hp) with Xtronic CVT. Available in FWD and AWD. Seats 5 passengers. Class-leading cargo space with 74.1 cu ft maximum.

### Nissan Rogue Plug-In Hybrid
Hybrid electric compact SUV combining efficiency with versatility.

### Nissan Pathfinder
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in S, SV, SL, and Platinum trims. Powered by a 3.5L V6 engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and 4WD. Towing capacity up to 6,000 lbs.

### Nissan Armada
Full-size SUV with powerful 5.6L V8 engine. Seating up to 8 passengers. Premium trims and features. Maximum towing capacity.

### Nissan Murano
Mid-size 2-row crossover SUV with distinctive styling. Available in multiple trims with a 3.5L V6 engine and Xtronic CVT.

### Nissan Kicks
Subcompact crossover SUV, value-oriented and fuel-efficient. Affordable entry point into the Nissan SUV lineup.

### Nissan Ariya
All-electric crossover SUV available in multiple configurations. Up to 304 miles estimated range. Available in FWD and e-4ORCE AWD. Up to 389 hp in performance variants.

## Cars

### Nissan Altima
Mid-size sedan available with FWD or AWD. Features the 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with Xtronic CVT. Available trims include S, SV, SR, SL, and Platinum. Nissan Safety Shield 360 standard.

### Nissan Sentra
Compact sedan, stylish and fuel-efficient. Available in S, SV, and SR trims. Standard 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with Xtronic CVT.

### Nissan Maxima
Full-size sedan with sporty design. Available in multiple trims. Powerful V6 engine.

### Nissan Versa
Subcompact sedan, Nissan's most affordable model. Fuel-efficient and city-friendly.

## Trucks

### Nissan Frontier
Mid-size pickup truck available in S, SV, and PRO-4X trims. Powered by a 3.8L V6 engine (310 hp) with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Maximum towing capacity of 7,150 lbs.

### Nissan Titan / Titan XD
Full-size pickup truck with powerful 5.6L V8 engine. Available in multiple configurations including the heavy-duty Titan XD. Designed for maximum capability.

## Electric Vehicles

### Nissan Ariya
All-electric crossover SUV with up to 304 miles range. Advanced e-4ORCE AWD system available.

### Nissan LEAF
All-electric hatchback, one of the best-selling EVs globally. Affordable electric mobility.
