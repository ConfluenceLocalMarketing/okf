# Basil Mobility Service Center

Basil Mobility's service center features BraunAbility-certified technicians trained to service all conversion types, adaptive equipment, and vehicle systems.

## Service Hours

| Day | Hours |
|---|---|
| Mon–Fri | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM |
| Sat | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM |
| Sun | Closed |

**Phone:** (716) 683-6800

## Services Offered

### Routine Maintenance
- Oil changes and filter replacements
- Tire rotation and balancing
- Fluid checks and replacements
- Cabin air filter replacement
- Brake inspections and repairs

### Mobility Equipment Service
- Ramp diagnostics and repair (power fold-out and in-floor)
- Wheelchair lift repair (electrical, mechanical, hydraulic)
- Power door system service
- Securement system checks (tie-downs)
- Adaptive driving equipment maintenance (hand controls, steering aids)
- Electronic mobility control system diagnostics
- Power ramp calibration

### Vehicle Repair
- Powertrain and drivetrain repairs for accessible vans
- Standard auto repair for all makes and models
- Diagnostics and computer scanning
- Paratransit vehicle safety inspections
- Wheelchair lift safety inspections

## Parts Center

- Genuine BraunAbility replacement parts
- OEM and aftermarket components
- Accessibility replacement parts (ramps, lifts, doors)
- Quick ordering for specialty components

## Service Perks

- Two-way courtesy shuttle during service
- Online scheduling
- Vehicle repair status updates
- Certified BraunAbility technicians
- Standard auto repair combined with mobility expertise

## Also Available

- Upfitting standard factory minivans with accessibility equipment
- Installing power lift systems into customer vehicles
- Custom interior modifications for wheelchair accommodation
