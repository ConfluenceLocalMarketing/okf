# Financing & Leasing

The Basil Mobility Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for wheelchair accessible vehicles through BraunAbility Financial Services.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home through BraunAbility Financial Services
- **BraunAbility Financial Services**: APR as low as 2.99% for up to 72 months (well-qualified buyers)
- **Dealer Cash**: Up to $3,000 dealer cash available on select conversions
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Get an instant online valuation through the dealership's trade-in tool. In-person appraisal also available at the dealership.

## Payment Calculators

Estimate monthly payments using the payment calculator before visiting the dealership.
