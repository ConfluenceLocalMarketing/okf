## Shopping Considerations

- **Side-entry vs. rear-entry:** Side-entry vans load at sliding door (best for standard wheelchairs). Rear-entry loads through back doors (best for tilter/transporter chairs).
- **Lowered-floor:** More headroom and easier transfers.
- **Hybrid option:** XT on Toyota Sienna for fuel efficiency.
- **In-floor ramp (XI):** Ramp stows under the vehicle floor, leaving more cabin space.
