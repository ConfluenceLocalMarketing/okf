# Frequently Asked Questions - Haselwood Auto Group

## Inventory

**Q: What brands are available at Haselwood Auto Group?**
A: 14+ brands across 11 member dealerships: Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, Hyundai, Volkswagen, Toyota, Ford, Honda, Kia, Mazda, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and Ram.

**Q: Where are the member dealerships located?**
A: All located at the West Hills Autoplex in Bremerton, WA: 501 West Hills Blvd (Chevrolet, GMC), 5008 Auto Center Blvd (Hyundai, VW), 900-950 West Hills Blvd (Toyota, Chrysler Jeep Dodge Ram, Honda, Kia, Mazda, Autoplex), and 1000-1100 Oyster Bay Ave (Ford, Mazda).

**Q: Does Haselwood Auto Group offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, factory-backed certified pre-owned programs are available for most brands including Chevrolet, GM, Hyundai, Volkswagen, Toyota, Ford, Honda, Kia, and Chrysler/Jeep/Dodge/Ram.

**Q: What is the Economy Vehicle Program?**
A: An exclusive program offering budget-friendly, pre-owned cars that undergo rigorous safety and dependability checks for first-time buyers and value-seekers.

## Financing

**Q: What financing options are available?**
A: Traditional loans, leases, and special financing programs are available through each member dealership's finance department.

**Q: Does the group offer online credit applications?**
A: Yes, online credit application and pre-approval are available through the group website.

**Q: Is trade-in accepted?**
A: Yes, trade-in appraisals are available at any member dealership with competitive values based on third-party market evaluation.

## Service

**Q: Can the service centers work on vehicles not purchased from the group?**
A: Yes, the service centers service all vehicles whether purchased from the group or elsewhere.

**Q: Do you offer loaner vehicles?**
A: Yes, service loaner vehicles are available for extended appointments, and there is an on-site rental car agency.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through the group website for any of the 11 brand-specific service centers.

**Q: What warranty comes with West Hills pre-owned vehicles?**
A: A 3 Month or 3,000 Mile Customer Confidence Warranty on West Hills pre-owned vehicles, with Extended Service Plus plans available at purchase.

## Dealership

**Q: Who founded Haselwood Auto Group?**
A: Chuck Haselwood, who started selling used cars in Bremerton after WWII and opened the first Honda franchise in the United States in 1974.

**Q: What areas does Haselwood Auto Group serve?**
A: Bremerton, Silverdale, Port Orchard, Poulsbo, Gig Harbor, Tacoma, and surrounding communities across the Kitsap Peninsula.

**Q: Does the group offer home delivery?**
A: Yes, Click-N-Drive online purchasing includes home delivery options.

**Q: Is military appreciation offered?**
A: Yes, the group has a dedicated Military Appreciation program with specialized incentives for active-duty members and veterans.
