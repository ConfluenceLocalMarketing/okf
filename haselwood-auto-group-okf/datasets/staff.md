# Staff Directory - Haselwood Auto Group

Staff directory compiled from the group website and customer reviews across member dealerships.

## Executive Leadership

- **Eric Wiler** - President of Haselwood Auto Group; awarded 2019 Kitsap Philanthropic Award and Leaders of Distinction Under 40 Award
- **Chuck Haselwood** - Founder (1923-); established the group starting with a gas station in Bremerton, first Honda franchise in the US in 1974, 1998 Time Magazine Quality Dealer of the Year
