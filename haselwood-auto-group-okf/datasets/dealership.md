# Haselwood Auto Group

Haselwood Auto Group is a multi-franchise automotive dealership group based at the **West Hills Autoplex** in Bremerton, WA, serving the Kitsap Peninsula including Silverdale, Port Orchard, Poulsbo, Gig Harbor, and Tacoma. The group operates 11+ member dealerships representing 14+ automotive brands with new and pre-owned vehicle sales, certified service centers, genuine OEM parts, and flexible financing.

## History

Founded by **Chuck Haselwood** (1923-). After serving in WWII under General Patton, he returned to Bremerton, partnered with Danny Jones to open a gas station, and started selling used cars on the side. He later partnered with Bud Higgs to open Chuck & Buds used cars, offering a pioneering 30-day warranty. Haselwood entered the new car industry with Buick and in 1974 became the first Honda franchise in the country. Over decades he expanded into Toyota, Kia, Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and Ram, creating the West Hills Autoplex.
