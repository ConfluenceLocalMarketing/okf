# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Haselwood Auto Group

Haselwood Auto Group maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory across all member dealerships at the West Hills Autoplex in Bremerton, with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across 11+ member dealership locations
- **Multi-brand selection** spanning Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, Hyundai, Volkswagen, Toyota, Ford, Honda, Kia, Mazda, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, Ram, and more makes
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles, including the Economy Vehicle Program for budget-conscious buyers
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, coupes, and convertibles
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover across locations

## West Hills Pre-Owned Program

The West Hills Pre-Owned collection is a hallmark of the Haselwood Auto Group:

- **3 Month / 3,000 Mile Warranty** - Customer confidence warranty on West Hills pre-owned vehicles
- **West Hills Extended Service Plus** - Service plans available at time of vehicle purchase
- **Free CarFax Report** - Included with every pre-owned vehicle
- **Third-Party Market Evaluation** - Independent pricing verification
- **Live-Market Pricing** - Actively researched competitive pricing
- **Instant Deal Reveal** - Verified pricing transparency online

## Certified Pre-Owned Programs

Factory-backed certified programs available by brand:

- **Chevrolet Certified Pre-Owned** - Rigorous inspection, warranty, and roadside assistance
- **GM Certified Pre-Owned** (Buick, GMC) - Factory-backed with 12-month/12,000-mile bumper-to-bumper warranty
- **Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned** - 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain coverage
- **Volkswagen Certified Pre-Owned** - 2-year/24,000-mile bumper-to-bumper warranty
- **Toyota Certified** - 12-month/12,000-mile comprehensive warranty, 7-year/100,000-mile limited powertrain
- **Ford Blue Advantage** - Gold and Blue certifications with varying warranty coverage
- **Honda Certified** - 12-month/12,000-mile comprehensive warranty, 7-year/100,000-mile powertrain
- **Kia Certified Pre-Owned** - 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain warranty
- **Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, Ram Certified** - Factory-backed inspections and warranties

An exclusive program offering budget-friendly, pre-owned cars that undergo rigorous safety and dependability checks. High-quality, low-cost alternatives for first-time buyers and value-seekers.
