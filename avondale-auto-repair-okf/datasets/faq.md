# Frequently Asked Questions - Avondale Auto Repair

## What services does Avondale Auto Repair offer?

We offer engine repair, routine maintenance, brake repair, oil changes, suspension and steering service, tire service, A/C and heating repair, electrical diagnostics, and emissions repair. See [services.md](services.md) for full details.

## Are you ASE Certified?

Yes, our technicians are ASE Certified. We are also AAA Approved, a NAPA AutoCare Center, and a CARFAX Top-Rated Service Center. See [company.md](company.md) for all certifications.

## Do you work on both domestic and foreign vehicles?

Yes. We service all makes and models including Chevy, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Lexus, and more.

## How do I schedule an appointment?

Call us at (773) 267-5440. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

## What are your hours of operation?

Monday through Friday 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, Saturday 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. Closed Sunday.

## Do you offer any warranty on repairs?

Yes. As a NAPA AutoCare Center, we offer a 24-month / 24,000-mile nationwide warranty honored at 14,000+ locations.

## Do you provide financing options?

Yes. We accept the Synchrony Car Care Credit Card and Snap Finance. See [company.md](company.md) for details.

## How much does an oil change cost?

Pricing varies by vehicle and oil type. Contact us at (773) 267-5440 for a quote.

## What if my check engine light is on?

We provide full engine diagnostics to identify the issue. Bring your vehicle in and our ASE Certified technicians will diagnose and repair it.

## Do you perform emissions testing and repair?

Yes. We offer emissions diagnostics and repair services to help your vehicle pass Illinois emissions testing.

## Can you repair my brakes?

Yes. We provide complete brake inspection, pad replacement, and rotor service for all makes and models.

## Do you sell tires?

Yes. We offer tire sales, rotation, balancing, and flat repair services.
