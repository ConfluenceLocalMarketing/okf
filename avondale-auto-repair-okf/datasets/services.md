# Services - Avondale Auto Repair

Avondale Auto Repair provides comprehensive automotive services for domestic and foreign vehicles. All work is performed by ASE Certified technicians using quality parts and equipment.

## Engine Repair and Diagnostics

Full engine service including diagnostics, check engine light analysis, misfire diagnosis, and complete engine repair for all makes and models.

## Maintenance

Routine preventive maintenance including oil changes, fluid checks, belt replacement, and multi-point inspections to keep your vehicle running reliably.

## Brake Repair

Complete brake system service including inspection, pad replacement, rotor resurfacing and replacement, caliper service, and brake fluid exchange.

## Oil Change

Professional oil and filter service using quality oils. Conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic options available.

## Suspension and Steering

Ride quality and handling repairs including shock and strut replacement, alignment, steering system service, and suspension component repair.

## Tire Service and Repair

Tire sales, rotation, balancing, mounting, and flat repair. Proper tire maintenance to extend tread life and improve fuel economy.

## A/C and Heating

Climate system diagnostics and repair including A/C recharge, compressor replacement, heater core service, and ventilation system repair.

## Electrical Services

Battery testing and replacement, alternator repair, starter service, and electrical system diagnostics.

## Emissions Repair

Emissions testing, diagnostics, and repair to ensure compliance with Illinois environmental standards.
