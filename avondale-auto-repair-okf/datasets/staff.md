# Staff Directory - Avondale Auto Repair

## Tony Martino — Owner / General Manager

Tony has been in the auto repair industry for over 25 years. He owns both Avondale Auto Repair and Logan Square Auto Repair, bringing decades of experience serving Chicago drivers.

## Carlos Reyes — Master Technician (ASE Certified)

Carlos holds ASE certifications in engine repair, brakes, suspension, and electrical systems. Over 15 years of experience with domestic and foreign vehicles.

## David Kim — Senior Technician (ASE Certified)

David specializes in diagnostics, engine performance, and emissions repair. ASE Certified with 12 years of experience.

## Maria Sanchez — Service Writer / Customer Relations

Maria handles appointments, customer inquiries, and service coordination. Bilingual in English and Spanish.

## James O'Brien — Technician

James performs general maintenance, oil changes, tire services, and brake repairs. Committed to quality work and customer satisfaction.
