# Texan Title — FAQ

## What services do you offer?

Texan Title provides a range of title services including residential and commercial title insurance, escrow services, and thorough title searches. The dedicated team ensures a seamless transaction process from start to finish.

## How do you ensure client satisfaction?

Texan Title focuses on personalized service, ensuring clear communication and support throughout every transaction. The team is committed to addressing client needs promptly and efficiently to build lasting relationships.

## What sets Texan Title apart from other title companies?

Texan Title stands out through strong community focus and deep local knowledge. The company values building trust and understanding clients' unique needs. With 27 offices across Texas and 30+ years of experience, Texan Title combines statewide reach with local expertise.

## Do you provide assistance for first-time buyers?

Yes. Texan Title understands that first-time buyers have many questions and concerns. The team guides buyers through the process, ensuring they feel informed and secure in their decisions.

## How much does owner's title insurance typically cost in Texas?

Title insurance premiums in Texas are regulated by the Texas Department of Insurance. Texan Title provides a Premium Rate Sheet (TTIC 2026) available through their Realtor tools page.

## Where can I find a Texas closing cost calculator?

Texan Title offers the SouthLandAgent app — Real Estate's #1 Net Sheet and Closing Cost platform — available free at https://southlandagentapp.com for buyer estimates, seller net sheets, and quick title quotes.

## How do I safely deposit earnest money?

Texan Title handles earnest money deposits as part of their escrow services at all 27 office locations. Contact the nearest office for deposit instructions.

## Does Texan Title handle 1031 exchanges?

Yes. Texan Title 1031 Exchange provides Qualified Intermediary services with Exchange Manager Pro℠ software, backed by a $50M fidelity bond and CES-certified specialists. See the 1031 Exchange page for details.
