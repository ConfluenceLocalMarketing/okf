# Texan Title — Underwriter Partners

Texan Title represents the largest title insurance underwriters in the nation, plus their own underwriter entity.

| Underwriter | Type | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------|
| Texan Title Insurance Company (TTIC) | In-house | Established 2019, own underwriter entity |
| Old Republic Title | National | One of the largest title insurers in the US |
| Fidelity National Title | National | Part of Fidelity National Financial |
| First National Title Insurance Company | National | Major national underwriter |
| Alamo Title Insurance | Regional/National | Texas-based national underwriter |
| Stewart Title Insurance Company | National | One of the largest title insurance companies |
| Title Resources Group | National | Stewart-affiliated |
| Chicago Title Insurance Company | National | Part of Fidelity National Financial |
| First American Title Insurance Company | National | One of the largest title insurers in the US |
| Essent Title Insurance | National | Mortgage insurance and title services |
