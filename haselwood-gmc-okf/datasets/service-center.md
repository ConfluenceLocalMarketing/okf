# Service Center - Haselwood GMC

The Haselwood GMC Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 501 W Hills Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Mobile Service** - Let the service team come to you for select maintenance
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services
- **Battery Services** - Battery testing, replacement, and maintenance
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following GMC's recommended maintenance schedule

## Service Team

- **Bill Baker** - Service Manager

- **Online scheduling** available through the dealership website
