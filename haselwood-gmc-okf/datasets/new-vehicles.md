# New GMC Models - Haselwood GMC

Haselwood GMC carries a full lineup of new GMC vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs

### GMC Acadia
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in multiple trims. Powered by a turbocharged engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and AWD. Modern design with spacious interior and advanced safety features.

### GMC Terrain
Compact SUV, value-oriented and fuel-efficient. Available in multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Compact size with impressive cargo versatility and available AWD.

### GMC Yukon
Full-size SUV with powerful V8 engine options. Seating up to 8 passengers. Available in SLE, SLT, AT4, and Denali trims. Maximum towing capacity. Premium interior materials and advanced technology.

### GMC Yukon XL
Extended-length full-size SUV offering maximum cargo space behind the third row. Seating up to 8 passengers with generous legroom throughout. Available in Denali and AT4 trims with V8 power.

### GMC HUMMER EV SUV
All-electric supertruck SUV. Available in 3X and Edition 1 configurations. Up to 830 hp in 3X trim with CrabWalk and Extract Mode. Approx 300+ miles estimated range. UltraVision underbody cameras.

## Trucks

### GMC Canyon
Mid-size pickup truck available in Elevation, AT4, and Denali trims. Powered by a turbocharged engine. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Off-road capability with AT4 trim featuring Terrain Mode and off-road suspension.

### GMC Sierra 1500
Full-size light-duty pickup truck. Available in Pro, SLE, Elevation, SLT, AT4, and Denali trims. Multiple engine options including turbocharged 4-cylinder, V8, and Duramax diesel. Available in 2WD and 4WD. MultiPro Tailgate available on select trims.

### GMC Sierra 2500 HD
Heavy-duty pickup truck with maximum towing capability. Available in Pro, SLE, SLT, AT4, and Denali trims. Powerful gas and diesel engine options. Designed for serious work and towing.

### GMC Sierra 3500 HD
Heavy-duty pickup truck with maximum payload capacity. Available in Pro, SLE, SLT, AT4, and Denali trims. Dual rear wheel configuration available. Class-leading towing and payload specs.

### GMC HUMMER EV Pickup
All-electric supertruck pickup. Available in 2X and 3X trims. Up to 830 hp with Watts to Freedom mode. CrabWalk diagonal driving capability. Approx 300+ miles estimated range. Available with adaptive air suspension.

### GMC Sierra EV
All-electric full-size pickup truck. Available in Denali trim. Up to 440 miles estimated range. MultiPro Tailgate and MidGate for flexible cargo configurations. Available with Super Cruise hands-free driving.
