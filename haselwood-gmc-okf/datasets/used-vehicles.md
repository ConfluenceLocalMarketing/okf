# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Haselwood GMC

Haselwood GMC maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned
- **Multi-make selection** spanning GMC, Chevrolet, Buick, Cadillac, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Jeep, Ram, Audi, Subaru, Nissan, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range from under $10,000 to premium vehicles
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover
- **CarBravo** - GM's certified used vehicle marketplace with nationwide inventory search

## Quality Assurance

- **Thorough Inspection** - All pre-owned vehicles inspected by certified technicians
- **GM Certified Pre-Owned** - Factory-backed inspections and warranties
- **Vehicle Service Contracts** - Available through GMC Protection
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

GM Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous inspection and include:
- GM factory-backed warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Multi-point inspection by certified technicians
- Competitive pricing with transparent value
- Integration with financing and service departments

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- Value Your Trade tool
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Vehicle hold policy
- CarBravo nationwide inventory search

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal available
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
- Competitive trade-in values
- KBB Instant Cash Offer available

## CarBravo

CarBravo is GM's certified used vehicle program that offers:
- Nationwide inventory of GM and non-GM vehicles
- Multi-point inspection
- 90-day/4,000-mile limited warranty
- Flexible return policy
- Online buying and at-home delivery options
