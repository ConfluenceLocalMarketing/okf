# Service Center at Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg

The Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg service center provides factory-trained maintenance and repair for GMC vehicles.

## Service Department

Contact: **931-246-6354**

Factory-trained technicians perform oil changes, tire rotations, brake service, engine diagnostics, transmission service, air conditioning service, and scheduled maintenance per the GM maintenance schedule.

## Parts Department

Contact: **931-903-2374**

The parts department stocks genuine OEM GMC parts, accessories, and fluids. Parts can be ordered online for pickup.

- Oil changes and fluid flushes
- Tire rotation and balancing
- Brake inspection and replacement
- Engine and transmission diagnostics
- Air conditioning service
- Battery testing and replacement
- Scheduled maintenance (GM maintenance schedule)
- Warranty repairs
- GM accessories installation
