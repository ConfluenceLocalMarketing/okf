# New GMC Vehicles at Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg

Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg offers the complete GMC model lineup, from light-duty pickups and heavy-duty work trucks to luxury SUVs and the all-electric Hummer EV.

## GMC Model Lineup

### Sierra 1500
Full-size light-duty pickup truck. Available in multiple trims (Pro, SLE, Elevation, SLT, AT4, Denali). Offers a choice of engines including the turbo-diesel Duramax 3.0L. Towing capacity up to 13,000 lbs. Bed and cab configurations include Regular Cab, Double Cab, and Crew Cab.

### Sierra HD (2500 / 3500)
Heavy-duty pickup truck engineered for maximum capability. Towing capacity up to 36,000 lbs when properly equipped. Available with the 6.6L V8 gas or 6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel engine. Trims include WT, SLE, SLT, AT4, and Denali.

### Yukon
Full-size SUV seating 7-9 passengers. Available with 5.3L V8, 6.2L V8, or 3.0L Duramax diesel. Trims range from SLE to Denali Ultimate. Features luxury interior, advanced trailering technology, and available 4WD.

### Yukon XL
Extended-length version of the Yukon offering maximum cargo capacity behind the third row. Same engine and trim lineup as the standard Yukon. Seating for up to 9 passengers with generous storage.

### Acadia
Mid-size SUV seating up to 7 passengers. Available with a 2.5L 4-cylinder or 3.6L V6. Front-wheel drive standard, all-wheel drive available. Trims include AT4 and Denali.

### Terrain
Compact SUV seating 5 passengers. Standard FWD, available AWD. Powered by a 1.5L turbo 4-cylinder. Known for efficient daily driving and a well-equipped interior.

### Canyon
Mid-size pickup truck seating 5 passengers. Available in Elevation, AT4, and Denali trims. RWD standard, 4WD available. Known for off-road capability and refined on-road manners.

### Hummer EV
All-electric supertruck. Up to 1,000 hp from three-motor e4WD drive system. Features include CrabWalk diagonal driving, Extract Mode air suspension, and Ultium battery platform. Available as SUV or SUT (pickup) body styles.
