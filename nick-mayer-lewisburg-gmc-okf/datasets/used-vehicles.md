# Pre-Owned Vehicles at Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg

Nick Mayer GMC Lewisburg carries a diverse selection of pre-owned vehicles spanning multiple makes and models.

## CarBravo Certified Used Vehicles

The dealership participates in **CarBravo**, GM's certified pre-owned vehicle program. CarBravo offers:

- Rigorous 172-point inspection
- Vehicle history report
- Limited warranty coverage
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Flexible return policy
- GM-backed financing options

## Trade-In Appraisal

Customers can value their current vehicle online through the dealership website and receive a trade-in offer.

## Inventory

The pre-owned lot typically includes late-model GMC trucks and SUVs, along with competitive makes from Ford, RAM, Toyota, Honda, and Chevrolet. Inventory is updated daily online.
