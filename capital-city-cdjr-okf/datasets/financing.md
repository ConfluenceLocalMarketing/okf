# Finance Center - Capital City CDJR

Capital City CDJR offers flexible financing solutions to help you drive home in your next CDJR vehicle. The finance center works with multiple lending partners to secure competitive rates.

## Financing Options

- **Online Credit Application** — Apply from home for fast pre-approval
- **Competitive Rates** — Through a network of lending partners
- **Flexible Lease Options** — Low-mileage and standard lease terms

## Leasing

Chrysler Capital offers competitive lease programs on CDJR vehicles.

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Return at lease end |
| Mileage Limits | None | Typical 10,000–15,000 mi/year |
| End of Term | Trade or keep | Return or purchase |
