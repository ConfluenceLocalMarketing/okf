# Canyon Crest Event Center Prompts

## Wedding Venue

- **Canyon Crest Event Center Wedding Venue** — Premier wedding venue on the Snake River Canyon in Twin Falls, Idaho. Indoor and outdoor ceremony and reception spaces for up to 350 guests, with full catering, staff-assisted setup and teardown, state-of-the-art audiovisual system, and breathtaking canyon views. The Pointe outdoor ceremony site overlooks the Perrine Bridge and Snake River Canyon.

## Corporate Events & Conferences

- **Canyon Crest Event Center Corporate Events** — Versatile convention facility with 21,000 square feet divisible into five meeting rooms. Advanced telecommunications connectivity, A/V support, private boardroom with balcony for groups up to 20, trail-side room with canyon views, and on-site Redhawk Gastropub. Ideal for meetings, conferences, trade shows, and breakout sessions.

## Catering Services

- **Canyon Crest Event Center Catering** — Full-service banquet and off-site catering in Twin Falls, Idaho. Customizable menus ranging from elegantly plated meals to buffet-style service. Experienced team for menu customization, theme coordination, and event planning. On-site and off-site event catering available.
