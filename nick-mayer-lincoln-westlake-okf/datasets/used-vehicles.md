# Pre-Owned Vehicle Inventory

Nick Mayer Lincoln Westlake offers a wide selection of pre-owned vehicles including Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicles and multi-make used cars, trucks, and SUVs. As the #1 volume Lincoln dealer in the Great Lakes Market area, the pre-owned inventory is consistently refreshed.

## Inventory Features

- Multi-make pre-owned inventory including Lincoln, Ford, and other brands
- Searchable online inventory with photos and vehicle history reports
- CarFax vehicle history reports available on most vehicles

## Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned

The Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned program provides:

- Comprehensive 172-point inspection by certified Lincoln technicians
- Limited warranty coverage
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Complimentary Lincoln Pickup & Delivery for covered repairs
- Rental car reimbursement
- Vehicle history report

## Trade-In Program

- Online Value Your Trade tool for instant estimates
- Appraisal appointments available
- Trade-in equity can be applied to new or pre-owned purchases
