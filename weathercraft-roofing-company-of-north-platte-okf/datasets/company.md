# Weathercraft Roofing Company of North Platte

Weathercraft Roofing Company of North Platte is a full-service roofing, siding, gutter, window, and overhead door contractor serving the Midwest. Founded in 1976 in North Platte, Nebraska, the company has grown to 15 locations across 5 states. Weathercraft is an Elevate Master Contractor and offers both commercial and residential services.

## History

Founded in 1976 by Alan Erickson and Bill Livengood, branching out from Weathercraft of Lincoln, NE. In 1983, Alan became sole owner. In 2006, Scott Erickson and Joe Staroska purchased the company and remain owners today.
