# Weathercraft Garden

Weathercraft Garden is a roofing and exterior solutions contractor based in Garden City, Kansas, operating as a branch of the Weathercraft Companies network. With over 40 years of service, the company provides commercial, industrial, and residential roofing alongside siding, gutters, windows, and overhead door installation.

## Services

### Commercial Roofing

Installation, repair, and maintenance of roofing systems on commercial buildings. Weathercraft is an Elevate Master Contractor offering TPO, EPDM, and Hot Tar (Modified Bitumen) systems. Services include preventative maintenance plans, non-destructive inspections, and comprehensive warranties.

### Industrial Roofing

Specialized industrial roofing solutions for large-scale facilities. Offers Hot Tar, TPO, and EPDM systems tailored to manufacturing and industrial environments. Addresses challenges unique to industrial roofs such as chemical exposure, heavy equipment, and extreme weather. Also provides repair services for standing seam, fiberglass, and metal roofs.

### Flat Roofing

Certified Elevate Master Contractor for flat and low-slope roofing. Options include Traditional Hot Tar, TPO (energy-efficient, UV-resistant), and EPDM (flexible, long-lasting). Serves both commercial and residential properties.

### Roofing Coatings

Silicone and elastomeric roof coating services that extend existing roof lifespan. Coatings form a seamless, fluid-applied membrane with UV reflection, leak protection, and energy efficiency benefits. Applicable to metal, BUR, TPO, and other surfaces.

### Shingle Roofing

Asphalt shingle installation and repair for residential properties. Offers products from Tamko, Malarkey, and DaVinci (synthetic slate). Covers traditional asphalt, architectural, and luxury shingle options.

### Metal Roofing

Corrugated metal roofing and wall paneling installation for residential, agricultural, and commercial buildings. Also provides metal building repair for leaks, wind damage, rust, and corrosion.
