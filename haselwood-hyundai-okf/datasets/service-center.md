# Service Center - Haselwood Hyundai

The Haselwood Hyundai Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 5008 Auto Center Blvd, Bremerton, WA.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Hyundai Complimentary Maintenance** - Factory-scheduled maintenance at no cost for 3 years / 36,000 miles
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Tire Replacement and Alignment** - Full tire services
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Hyundai's recommended maintenance schedule
- **EV Diagnostics and Service** - Specialized electric vehicle maintenance
