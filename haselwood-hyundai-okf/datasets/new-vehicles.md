# New Hyundai Models - Haselwood Hyundai

Haselwood Hyundai carries a full lineup of new Hyundai vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## SUVs & Crossovers

### Hyundai Venue
Subcompact SUV, Hyundai's most affordable model. Fuel-efficient and city-friendly with a starting MSRP around $20,550.

### Hyundai Kona
Subcompact crossover SUV available in multiple trims. Available as a conventional gasoline model or the all-electric **Kona Electric** (starting around $32,975).

### Hyundai Tucson
Compact SUV, Hyundai's best-selling model. Available in gasoline, **Tucson Hybrid**, and **Tucson Plug-In Hybrid** variants. Features bold design, available AWD, and advanced safety technology. Starting MSRP around $29,450.

### Hyundai Santa Fe
Mid-size SUV with modern, boxy design. Available in gasoline and **Santa Fe Hybrid** variants. Seats up to 5 passengers. Starting MSRP around $35,050.

### Hyundai Palisade
Full-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in gasoline and **Palisade Hybrid** variants. Premium features and trims. Starting MSRP around $39,435.

### Hyundai IONIQ 5
All-electric compact crossover with up to 303 miles estimated range. Available in RWD and e-AWD configurations. Features 800V ultra-fast charging capability. Starting MSRP around $35,000.

### Hyundai IONIQ 5 N
High-performance all-electric crossover. Up to 601 hp with N Grin Boost. Track-capable with Drift Optimizer and N e-shift.

### Hyundai IONIQ 9
All-electric 3-row flagship SUV. Premium electric SUV with spacious interior and advanced technology. Starting MSRP around $58,955.

### Hyundai Santa Cruz
Compact pickup truck (Sport Adventure Vehicle) combining SUV comfort with truck utility. Available in multiple trims. Starting MSRP around $29,750.

## Cars

### Hyundai Elantra
Compact sedan, stylish and fuel-efficient. Available in gasoline, **Elantra Hybrid**, and high-performance **Elantra N** variants. Starting MSRP around $22,625.

### Hyundai Sonata
Mid-size sedan with sleek design. Available in gasoline and **Sonata Hybrid** variants. Advanced safety and convenience features. Starting MSRP around $27,450.

Most new Hyundai models include:
- **Hyundai SmartSense** - Advanced driver assistance suite

- **Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist**
- **Lane Keeping Assist**
- **Safe Exit Assist**
