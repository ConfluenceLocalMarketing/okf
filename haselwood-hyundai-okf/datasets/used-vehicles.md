# Pre-Owned and Certified Pre-Owned Vehicles - Haselwood Hyundai

Haselwood Hyundai maintains a substantial pre-owned vehicle inventory in the Bremerton area with a wide selection spanning multiple makes and models.

## Inventory Scope

- **Total vehicles:** Extensive inventory across new and pre-owned (140+ new, hundreds pre-owned)
- **Multi-make selection** spanning Hyundai, Volkswagen, Chevrolet, GMC, Ford, Toyota, Honda, Kia, and more
- **Price range:** Broad range including the Economy Vehicle Program for budget-conscious buyers
- **Vehicle types:** Sedans, SUVs, trucks, vans, and coupes
- **Daily fresh inventory** with regular turnover

## Quality Assurance

- **173-Point Inspection** - Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned rigorous inspection
- **Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)** - Factory-backed inspections, remainder of 5yr/60k mi new vehicle warranty, 10yr/100k mi CPO powertrain warranty
- **Vehicle Service Contracts** - Available for most vehicles
- **Carfax History Reports** - Available on request

## Certified Pre-Owned Program

Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned vehicles undergo rigorous 173-point inspection and include:
- Remainder of 5-Year / 60,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty
- 10-Year / 100,000-Mile CPO Powertrain Warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Comprehensive vehicle history report
- Multi-point inspection by certified Hyundai technicians

## Economy Vehicle Program

Special program offering safety-checked, affordable pre-owned options for first-time buyers and budget-conscious customers seeking high-value second vehicles.

## Online Buying Tools

- Online inventory browsing and filtering
- Value Your Trade tool (KBB Instant Cash Offer)
- Car payment calculator
- Online credit application and pre-approval
- Deal Reveal - instant pricing transparency
- Remote vehicle purchasing

## Trade-In

- Trade-in appraisal via KBB Instant Cash Offer
- Cash offers for trade-ins (even without vehicle purchase)
- Competitive trade-in values
- Vehicle Upgrade Program
