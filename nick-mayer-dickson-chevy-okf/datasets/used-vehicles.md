# Used and Pre-Owned Vehicles

## Pre-Owned Inventory

Nick Mayer Chevrolet of Dickson carries a wide selection of pre-owned vehicles across all makes and models. Every pre-owned vehicle undergoes a multi-point inspection before listing.

## CarBravo Certified Used

The dealership is part of the CarBravo certified pre-owned program, which provides:

- Comprehensive 172-point inspection
- Vehicle history report
- Warranty coverage
- 24/7 roadside assistance
- Flexible return policy

## Trade-In

The dealership offers online trade-in valuation. Customers can receive an instant cash offer or apply trade value toward a new or pre-owned purchase.

- Browse inventory online with photos and CarFax history
- Filter by make, model, price range, and features
- Schedule test drives online
- Secure credit application
- Value your trade from home
