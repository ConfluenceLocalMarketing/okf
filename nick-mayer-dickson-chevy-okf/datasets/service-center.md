# Service Center

The service center at Nick Mayer Chevrolet of Dickson is equipped to handle all maintenance and repair needs for Chevrolet and other makes.

## Services Offered

- Routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations, fluid checks)
- Brake service and repair
- Engine diagnostics and repair
- Transmission service
- Air conditioning service
- Tire sales and installation
- Wheel alignment
- Battery testing and replacement
- Factory-scheduled maintenance
- Warranty repairs
- Recall service

## Factory-Trained Technicians

All service work is performed by certified technicians who receive ongoing training on the latest Chevrolet vehicle technologies and diagnostic equipment.

## Genuine OEM Parts

The service center uses genuine OEM parts to ensure proper fit, function, and durability. The attached parts department stocks a full inventory of Chevrolet components.
