# Suggested AI Prompts

## Inventory & Vehicle Questions
- "What new Chevrolet models are available at Nick Mayer Chevrolet of Dickson?"
- "Tell me about the Chevrolet Silverado 1500 and its towing capacity."
- "What is the difference between the Traverse and the Tahoe?"
- "Do you have any used trucks under $40,000?"
- "What is CarBravo and how does it work?"

## Financing & Trade-In
- "How do I apply for financing online?"
- "What is my trade-in worth?"
- "Are there any current lease specials on the Equinox?"

## Service
- "How do I schedule a service appointment?"
- "What are the service center hours?"
- "Do you offer oil change specials?"

## General
- "What are the dealership hours of operation?"
- "What brand does this dealership sell?"
- "Where is the dealership located?"
