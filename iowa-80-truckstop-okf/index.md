# Iowa 80 Truckstop

World's largest truckstop located in Walcott, Iowa since 1964 — featuring 900 truck parking spaces, 7 restaurant options, 300-seat full-service restaurant, truck service center, CAT Scale, private showers, movie theater, barber shop, chiropractor, dentist, workout room, laundry, Truckomat truck wash, Dogomat pet wash, gift store, Super Truck Showroom, Iowa 80 Trucking Museum, and home of the annual Walcott Truckers Jamboree.
