# Iowa 80 Truckstop

## Overview

Iowa 80 Truckstop is the world's largest truckstop, located in Walcott, Iowa at I-80 Exit 284. Established in 1964 by Bill Moon, it has never closed — operating 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. The facility spans 220 acres and serves over 5,000 customers daily. It is the flagship property of the Iowa 80 Group.

## Location

- **Address:** 755 W. Iowa 80 Road, I-80 Exit 284, Walcott, IA 52773
- **Phone:** (563) 284-6961
- **Hours:** 24 hours a day, 365 days a year (never closed since 1964)

## History

Founded in 1964 by Bill Moon at the intersection of Interstate 80 and Highway 6 in Walcott, Iowa. What began as a modest fuel stop has grown over six decades into the world's largest truckstop, expanding continuously to meet the needs of America's truck drivers and travelers. The Iowa 80 Trucking Museum preserves the history of trucking and complements the truckstop experience.

## Annual Events

### Walcott Truckers Jamboree

A three-day event held annually in July celebrating America's truckers. Established in 1979, the Jamboree features:

- Super Trucks Beauty Contest
- Antique truck displays
- Exhibitor booths
- Live entertainment
- Food vendors
- Family-friendly activities

The 2026 Walcott Truckers Jamboree is scheduled for July 9–11, 2026.

## Iowa 80 Trucking Museum

Located on the property, the Iowa 80 Trucking Museum showcases the history of trucking with a collection of antique trucks, memorabilia, and interactive exhibits.
