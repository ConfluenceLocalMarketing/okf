# Full Circle Strategic Marketing

Full Circle Strategic Marketing is a full-service marketing company based in Farmington Hills, MI, that helps clients leverage advanced technology, artificial intelligence (AI), and innovative marketing to fuel growth. ## Data-Driven Strategy

The team collects, interprets, and humanizes data to drive transparent and intelligent strategy across three phases: Understand (identify prospects), Find (locate based on channels/preferences), Activate (deliver optimal message timing and channel).
