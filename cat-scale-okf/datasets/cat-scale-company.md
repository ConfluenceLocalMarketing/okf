# CAT Scale Company

CAT Scale Company is the world's leading truck scale network, providing guaranteed accurate certified weights at over 2,275 locations across the United States and Canada. Founded in 1977 by truckstop entrepreneur Bill Moon, the company is headquartered in Walcott, Iowa, and operates as part of the Iowa 80 Group.

## History

Bill Moon installed the first CAT Scale in 1977 in South Holland, Illinois, revolutionizing the trucking industry with the first fully automated, full-length platform scale. By 1988 there were 28 CAT Scales in operation; the company began rapid expansion thereafter. The 1,500th scale opened in 2014 at a Love's Country Store in Springville, Utah. The network has since grown to over 2,275 locations.

## Products

- **CAT Scale Weighing Service** — Certified, accurate weighing of trucks and trailers at automated locations nationwide.
- **Weigh My Truck App** — Mobile application allowing drivers to weigh and pay from their smartphone, with emailed PDF scale tickets.
- **CAT Scale Locator App** — Mobile app to find nearby CAT Scale locations, plan routes, and save favorites.

## Guarantee

CAT Scale offers an unconditional guarantee: if a driver weighs legal on a CAT Scale and receives an overweight fine, CAT Scale will either pay the fine or appear in court with the driver as an expert witness to get the fine dismissed.

## Features

- Totally automated full-length platform scales
- 24/7 availability at all locations
- Certified accurate weights
- Network in 47 US states and 8 Canadian provinces

## Parent Company

CAT Scale Company is part of the Iowa 80 Group, the same family that owns Iowa 80 Truckstop, the world's largest truck stop.
