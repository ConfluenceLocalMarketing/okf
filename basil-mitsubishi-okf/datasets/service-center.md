# Basil Mitsubishi Service Center

Basil Mitsubishi's service center is a state-of-the-art facility featuring factory-trained technicians, genuine Mitsubishi parts, and the latest diagnostic equipment. The department services all makes and models — not just Mitsubishi vehicles.

## Service Hours

| Day | Hours |
|---|---|
| Mon–Fri | 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM |
| Sat | 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM |
| Sun | Closed |

**Phone:** (716) 666-4574

## Services Offered

### Routine Maintenance
- Oil changes (full synthetic)
- Tire rotation and balancing
- Battery replacement
- Fluid replacements and top-offs
- Engine air filter replacement
- Wiper blade installation
- State inspections

### Diagnostics & Repairs
- Check engine light diagnostics
- Brake assembly repairs (genuine pads)
- A/C and climate control repair
- Suspension adjustments
- Timing chain replacement
- Transmission service
- Major engine repairs

### Seasonal Services
- Winter tire installation
- Cooling system service

## Collision Center

The on-site collision center handles exterior body repairs:

- Dings, dents, scrapes, and scuffs
- Undercarriage inspection for structural integrity
- Post-accident restoration
