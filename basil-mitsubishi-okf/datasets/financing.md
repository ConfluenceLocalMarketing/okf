# Basil Mitsubishi Finance Center

The Basil Mitsubishi Finance Center offers comprehensive financing solutions for new and pre-owned Mitsubishi vehicles, serving customers throughout the greater Buffalo area including Williamsville, Amherst, Clarence, Lockport, Tonawanda, Depew, Cheektowaga, Kenmore, Lackawanna, and West Seneca.

See [dealership.md](basil-mitsubishi-okf/datasets/dealership.md) for full business profile.

## Financing Options

- **Online Credit Application** - Secure pre-approval from home before visiting the dealership
- **Multiple Lending Partners** - Network of banks, credit unions, and financial institutions including Santander Consumer USA for competitive rates
- **Flexible Terms** - Customized loan terms to fit budget and credit situation
- **New Vehicle Financing** - Special Mitsubishi manufacturer rates and incentives, including 0% APR for 72 months on select models
- **Pre-Owned Financing** - Competitive rates on certified pre-owned and used vehicles
- **First-Time Buyer Programs** - Credit-building opportunities for those with limited history
- **Vehicle Protection Plans** - Extended warranties, GAP coverage

## Leasing

- Low monthly payments with option to purchase at lease end
- Flexible mileage allowances
- Lease-end options: purchase, trade, or return
- Big rebates and low lease options available across the lineup

## Buying vs Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | You own the vehicle | Dealership owns; you return it |
| Mileage Limits | Unlimited | Annual cap (typically 10,000-15,000 miles) |
| Customization | Fully customizable | Limited modifications |
| End of Term | Paid off; keep or trade | Return, purchase, or lease new |
| Warranty | After factory warranty expires | Typically covered by factory warranty |

## Trade-In

- Instant online trade-in valuation powered by Kelley Blue Book
- Competitive offers based on market value, condition, and mileage
- **$2,500 more for your trade** based on Kelley Blue Book fair market value (in-stock inventory only, excludes vehicles with over 80,000 miles)
- Trade equity can be applied toward down payment
- Highest trade-in allowance guaranteed
