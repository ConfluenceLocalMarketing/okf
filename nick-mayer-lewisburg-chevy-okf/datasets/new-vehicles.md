# New Chevrolet Vehicles at Nick Mayer Chevrolet Lewisburg

Nick Mayer Chevrolet Lewisburg offers the complete Chevrolet model lineup, from trucks and SUVs to sedans, sports cars, and electric vehicles.

## Chevrolet Model Lineup

### Silverado 1500
Full-size light-duty pickup truck. Available in multiple trims (WT, Custom, LT, RST, LTZ, High Country, ZR2). Offers a choice of engines including the turbo-diesel Duramax 3.0L. Towing capacity up to 13,000 lbs. Configurations include Regular Cab, Double Cab, and Crew Cab.

### Silverado HD (2500 / 3500)
Heavy-duty pickup truck engineered for maximum capability. Towing capacity up to 36,000 lbs when properly equipped. Available with 6.6L V8 gas or 6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel engine.

### Equinox
Compact SUV seating 5 passengers. Standard FWD, available AWD. Known for fuel efficiency, modern technology, and a spacious interior. Available in LS, LT, RS, and Premier trims.

### Traverse
Three-row mid-size SUV seating 7-8 passengers. Available in LS, LT, RS, Z71, High Country trims. FWD standard, AWD available. Powered by a 2.5L turbo 4-cylinder. Class-leading cargo space.

### Tahoe
Full-size SUV seating 7-9 passengers. Available with 5.3L V8, 6.2L V8, or 3.0L Duramax diesel. Trims range from LS to High Country. Legendary nameplate with premium interior and advanced trailering tech.

### Suburban
Extended-length full-size SUV seating 7-9 passengers. Same engine and trim lineup as Tahoe but with additional cargo capacity behind the third row. The longest-running automotive nameplate in continuous production.

### Trax
Subcompact SUV seating 5 passengers. All-new design for 2024. Standard FWD. Known for affordability, modern styling, and standard Chevy Safety Assist suite. LS, 1LT, 2LT, and ACTIV trims.

### Blazer
Mid-size SUV seating 5 passengers. Sporty styling with available RS trim. FWD standard, AWD available. Engine options include a 2.0L turbo 4-cylinder and 3.6L V6.

### Malibu
Mid-size sedan seating 5 passengers. FWD. Efficient daily driver with available 2.0L turbo engine in the RS trim. Modern infotainment with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

### Colorado
Mid-size pickup seating 5 passengers. RWD standard, 4WD available. Known for off-road capability with the Trail Boss trim. Available in WT, LT, Trail Boss, Z71, and ZR2 trims.

### Corvette
Iconic American sports car seating 2 passengers. Mid-engine 6.2L V8 producing 490+ hp (495 hp with performance exhaust). RWD. Available as coupe or convertible (Stingray, Z06, E-Ray).

### Bolt EV / Bolt EUV
All-electric vehicles. FWD with 259 miles of estimated range per charge. Bolt EUV offers slightly larger dimensions and available Super Cruise hands-free driving. Affordable entry into EV ownership.
