# Staff at Nick Mayer Chevrolet Lewisburg

Nick Mayer Chevrolet Lewisburg employs a team of sales consultants, service advisors, finance managers, and parts specialists dedicated to serving the Lewisburg, Columbia, Franklin, and Fayetteville communities.

## Sales Team

The sales team assists customers with new Chevrolet purchases, pre-owned vehicle selection, test drives, and online financing applications.

## Service Advisors

Service advisors coordinate maintenance and repair appointments through the Chevrolet factory-trained service center.

## Finance Department

Finance managers guide customers through financing, leasing, warranty, and protection product options.
