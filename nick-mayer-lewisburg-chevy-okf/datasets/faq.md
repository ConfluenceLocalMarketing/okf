# Frequently Asked Questions — Nick Mayer Chevrolet Lewisburg

## General

**What are the dealership hours?**
Monday–Friday 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Sunday Closed.

**What areas do you serve?**
Lewisburg, Columbia, Franklin, Fayetteville, and surrounding Middle Tennessee communities.

## Chevrolet Inventory

**What Chevrolet models are available?**
Silverado 1500, Silverado HD (2500/3500), Equinox, Traverse, Tahoe, Suburban, Trax, Blazer, Malibu, Colorado, Corvette, and Bolt EV/EUV.

**Can I test drive a vehicle?**
Yes. Contact the sales department at 931-246-4218 to schedule a test drive.

## Pre-Owned and CarBravo

**What is CarBravo?**
CarBravo is GM's certified pre-owned vehicle program with a 172-point inspection, warranty coverage, and roadside assistance.

**Do you take trade-ins?**
Yes. Online trade-in appraisal is available on the dealership website.

## Financing

**How do I apply for financing?**
A secure online credit application is available on the dealership website.

**Do you offer leases?**
Yes. Lease deals and special offers are available on select Chevrolet models.

## Service

**Do I need an appointment for service?**
Appointments are recommended but walk-ins are welcome. Call 931-246-4351.

**Do you use genuine OEM parts?**
Yes. The parts department stocks genuine GM and ACDelco parts. Call 931-300-2764.
