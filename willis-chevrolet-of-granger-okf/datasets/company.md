# Willis Chevrolet Of Granger

Willis Chevrolet Of Granger is a full-service Chevrolet dealership in Granger, Iowa, proudly serving Clive, Des Moines, Urbandale, Waukee, and West Des Moines. The dealership traces its roots through Willis Automotive (1947) and the former Beneventi Chevrolet.

## New Chevrolet Sales

Full lineup of new Chevrolet vehicles including:

- **Trucks:** Silverado 1500 and HD series, Silverado EV, Colorado
- **SUVs:** Equinox, Blazer, Trax, Suburban, Tahoe, Traverse
- **Cars:** Malibu, Corvette
- **Electric:** Silverado EV, Blazer EV, Equinox EV
