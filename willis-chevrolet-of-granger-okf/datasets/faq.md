# Willis Chevrolet Of Granger — FAQ

## General

### What type of dealership is Willis Chevrolet Of Granger?
It is a full-service Chevrolet dealership in Granger, IA, serving the Des Moines metro area with new Chevy sales, certified pre-owned vehicles, and expert GM-certified service. See [company.md](company.md) for the full profile.

### What area does the dealership serve?
Granger, Clive, Des Moines, Urbandale, Waukee, West Des Moines, and the greater Des Moines metro area.

### How long has Willis Automotive been in business?
Willis Automotive traces its roots to 1947, providing automotive sales and service in Iowa for over 75 years.

## Sales

### What new Chevrolet models are available?
Full Chevrolet lineup including Silverado 1500 and HD series, Silverado EV, Colorado, Equinox, Blazer, Trax, Suburban, Tahoe, Traverse, Malibu, and Corvette.

### Do you offer the Silverado EV?
Yes, the Silverado EV is available alongside the Blazer EV and Equinox EV.

### Can I pre-qualify for financing online?
Yes, online pre-qualification is available from the comfort of home through the dealership website.

## Service

### What service does the service center offer?
Routine maintenance, major repairs, genuine OEM parts, and online service scheduling.

### Are the technicians GM-certified?
Yes, all technicians are GM-certified with specialized training on Chevrolet vehicles.

### Can I schedule service online?
Yes, online service scheduling is available through the dealership website.

## Financing

### What financing options are available?
Lease and purchase options, Chevrolet incentive programs, and special programs for teachers, first responders, and military personnel.

### Is online pre-qualification available?
Yes, customers can pre-qualify online from home before visiting the dealership.

## Pre-Owned

### What pre-owned vehicles are available?
Quality used cars and trucks with multi-point inspections, Chevrolet Certified Pre-Owned program vehicles, and free CARFAX reports on all pre-owned inventory.

### Do you provide vehicle history reports?
Yes, free CARFAX reports are available on all pre-owned vehicles.
