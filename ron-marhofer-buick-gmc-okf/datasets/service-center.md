# Service Center - Ron Marhofer Buick GMC

The Ron Marhofer Buick GMC Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 5617 Whipple Avenue Northwest, North Canton, OH.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes, tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, engine repair, suspension work
- **Tire Services** - Tire rotation, replacement, and alignment
- **Brake Service** - Brake pad replacement, rotor resurfacing
- **A/C Service** - Air conditioning repair and recharge
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following Buick and GMC recommended maintenance schedules
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work

## Service Experience

- **Online scheduling** available through the dealership website
- **Certified Technicians** - Factory-trained using genuine Buick and GMC OEM parts
- **Genuine OEM Parts** - Manufacturer's parts best suited for each repair
- **Multi-point inspections** with every service visit
