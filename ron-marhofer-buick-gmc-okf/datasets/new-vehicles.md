# New Buick and GMC Models - Ron Marhofer Buick GMC

Ron Marhofer Buick GMC carries a full lineup of new Buick and GMC vehicles. Below is a summary of available models and trims.

## GMC Trucks

### GMC Sierra 1500
Full-size pickup truck, available in multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Multiple engine options including 2.7L Turbo, 5.3L V8, 6.2L V8, and 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel. Available in 2WD and 4WD configurations. Maximum towing capacity up to 13,200 lbs. Advanced trailering technology and GMC ProGrade suite.

### GMC Sierra 2500 HD
Heavy-duty pickup truck for serious towing and hauling. Powered by 6.6L V8 gas or 6.6L Duramax Turbo-Diesel engine. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Maximum towing capacity up to 22,500 lbs.

### GMC Sierra 3500 HD
Heavy-duty pickup truck with maximum capability. Available in single rear wheel and dual rear wheel configurations. Powerful 6.6L Duramax Turbo-Diesel option. Maximum towing capacity up to 36,000 lbs.

### GMC Sierra 3500 HD Chassis Cab
Commercial-grade chassis cab configuration for upfitting. Available in multiple wheelbase options with 6.6L Duramax Turbo-Diesel engine.

### GMC Sierra EV
All-electric full-size pickup truck with GM's Ultium platform. Offers up to 440 miles of estimated range. Available in multiple trims including Denali Edition 1. Features e4WD, adaptive air suspension, and MultiPro Midgate.

### GMC Canyon
Mid-size pickup truck, available in AT4 and Denali trims. Powered by 2.7L Turbo engine. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Off-road capable with Terrain Mode and available skid plates.

### GMC HUMMER EV Pickup
All-electric supertruck with revolutionary capability. Features CrabWalk diagonal driving, Extract Mode adaptive air suspension, and Watts to Freedom launch mode. Up to 1,000 hp with three-motor e4WD drive system.

## GMC SUVs

### GMC Yukon
Full-size SUV available in multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Seating for up to 9 passengers. Standard 5.3L V8 engine with available 6.2L V8 or 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel. Available in 2WD and 4WD. Maximum towing capacity up to 8,400 lbs.

### GMC Acadia
Mid-size 3-row SUV seating up to 8 passengers. Available in multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Powered by 2.5L 4-cylinder engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and AWD.

### GMC Terrain
Compact SUV seating 5 passengers. Available in multiple trims including AT4 and Denali. Efficient 1.5L Turbo engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available in FWD and AWD.

## Buick SUVs

### Buick Encore GX
Subcompact luxury crossover SUV with distinctive styling. Available in Preferred, Sport Touring, and Avenir trims. Efficient engine options and available AWD. Compact size with premium interior appointments.

### Buick Envision
Compact luxury crossover SUV available in Preferred, Sport Touring, and Avenir trims. Turbocharged engine with available AWD. Premium interior with Quiet Tuning technology. Advanced safety and driver assistance features.

### Buick Enclave
Mid-size 3-row luxury SUV seating up to 7 passengers. Available in Preferred, Sport Touring, and Avenir trims. Powerful V6 engine with 9-speed automatic transmission. Available FWD and AWD. Premium interior appointments with Buick Quiet Tuning.
