# Acura of Springfield

Acura of Springfield is a full-service Acura dealership located at 3130 S Campbell Ave in Springfield, Missouri, serving Ozark, Nixa, Branson, and surrounding communities. Part of the Frank Leta family since 1965, the dealership provides a lifetime powertrain warranty with every new Acura.

## New Acura Sales

- **Sedans:** Integra A-Spec, TLX
- **SUVs:** RDX AWD, MDX Premium/Technology, ADX A-Spec
- Precision engineering, advanced technology, and safety-focused design

## Certified Pre-Owned

- Acura Precision Certified Pre-Owned program
- 182-point inspections
- 12-month/12,000-mile limited warranty
- 24/7 roadside assistance

## Service Center

- Certified technicians using genuine Acura parts
- Acura Accelerated Service (30-min or less quick options)
- Loaner vehicles available
- Online scheduling

## Leta for Life Benefits

- Lifetime powertrain warranty on new vehicles
- Complimentary car washes
- Loaner vehicles during service
