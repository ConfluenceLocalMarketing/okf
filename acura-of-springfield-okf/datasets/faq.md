# Frequently Asked Questions - Acura of Springfield

## Vehicles

### What new Acura models does Acura of Springfield offer?
Acura of Springfield offers the Integra A-Spec sedan, TLX sedan, RDX AWD SUV, MDX Premium/Technology SUV, and ADX A-Spec SUV. See [new-vehicles.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/acura-of-springfield-okf/datasets/new-vehicles.md) for the full lineup.

### Does Acura of Springfield have certified pre-owned vehicles?
Yes, through the Acura Precision Certified Pre-Owned program. Every CPO vehicle undergoes a 182-point inspection and includes a 12-month/12,000-mile limited warranty with 24/7 roadside assistance.

### Can I schedule a test drive?
Yes, contact the sales team at (417) 427-7671 or visit 3130 S Campbell Ave, Springfield, MO.

## Financing

### What financing options are available?
Acura of Springfield offers financing through multiple lending partners, lease options, and flexible terms. See [financing.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/acura-of-springfield-okf/datasets/financing.md) for full details.

### What is the Leta for Life program?
Leta for Life includes a lifetime powertrain warranty on new vehicles, complimentary car washes, and loaner vehicles during service appointments.

### Can I apply for financing online?
Yes, an online credit application is available.

## Service

### What services does the Acura service center provide?
Certified technicians perform routine maintenance, major repairs, and Acura Accelerated Service (30-minute or less quick options). See [service-center.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/acura-of-springfield-okf/datasets/service-center.md) for details.

### Do you offer loaner vehicles during service?
Yes, loaner vehicles are available for service customers.

### How do I schedule service?
Online scheduling is available.

## Dealership Operations

### Where is Acura of Springfield located?
3130 S Campbell Ave, Springfield, MO, serving Ozark, Nixa, Branson, and surrounding communities.

### Is Acura of Springfield part of a larger group?
Yes, it has been part of the Frank Leta family since 1965.

### Does Acura of Springfield offer a lifetime warranty?
Yes, every new Acura includes a lifetime powertrain warranty as part of the Leta for Life program.
