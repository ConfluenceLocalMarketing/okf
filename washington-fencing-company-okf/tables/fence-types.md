# Fence Types

| Material | Durability | Maintenance | Best Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cedar | Moderate | Moderate; requires staining or sealing | Residential privacy, aesthetics |
| Vinyl / PVC | High | Low; occasional cleaning | Residential privacy, low-maintenance |
| Metal (Ornamental Iron) | Very High | Low; occasional rust touch-up | Commercial, decorative, security |
| Post-on-Pipe | High | Low | Agricultural, rural, cost-effective |
| Hog Wire | Moderate | Low | Pet containment, rustic aesthetic |
| Dog / Pet | Moderate | Low | Pet containment, yard boundaries |
