# Staff Directory - BMW of Cincinnati North

BMW of Cincinnati North operates as part of the Jake Sweeney Company, a 4th generation family-owned auto group since 1917. The dealership is a **BMW M Certified Dealer** with factory-trained technicians and BMW product experts. Specific staff names were not available from the site or PromptGraph API data, but typical roles include:

## Sales Department

- **Sales Consultants** - BMW product experts specializing in new BMW sedans, SUVs, coupes, convertibles, electric iPerformance models, and M performance vehicles
- **BMW Genius** - Product specialists providing in-depth technology demonstrations and vehicle walkarounds
- **Internet Sales Team** - Handling online inquiries, remote purchasing, and out-of-town sales
- **Client Advisor** - Dedicated point of contact throughout the purchasing process

## Sales Management

- **Sales Manager** - Oversees sales operations and customer experience
- **Finance Manager** - Specializing in BMW Financial Services products, lease structuring, loan approval, and protection packages

## Service Department

- **BMW Certified Technicians** - Factory-trained for BMW maintenance and repair, including M and iPerformance vehicles
- **Service Advisors** - Customer-facing service consultants managing appointments, estimates, and communication
- **M Certified Technicians** - Specialized training for M performance vehicle service

## Parts Department

- **Parts Specialists** - Genuine BMW parts and accessories expertise
- **Parts Counter Staff** - Customer-facing parts sales

## Management

- **General Manager** - Overall dealership operations
- **Service Manager** - Service department oversight
- **Parts Manager** - Parts department operations
- **Marketing Manager** - Dealership marketing and digital presence
