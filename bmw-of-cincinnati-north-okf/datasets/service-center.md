# BMW Service Center - BMW of Cincinnati North

The BMW of Cincinnati North Service Center provides certified maintenance and repair services at 105 West Kemper Road, Cincinnati, OH. The center is a **BMW M Certified** service facility.

## Service Capabilities

- **Routine Maintenance** - Oil changes (BMW TwinPower Turbo oil), tire rotations, alignments, brake inspections, cabin air filters
- **BMW Value Service** - Competitive pricing on select maintenance services for older BMW models
- **Diagnostics** - Check engine light diagnosis, computerized system analysis, BMW-specific diagnostics
- **Major Repairs** - Transmission service, alternator replacement, suspension work, cooling system
- **Original BMW Batteries** - Factory-spec batteries with BMW battery management system registration
- **Recall Repairs** - OEM-specified recall and campaign work
- **Scheduled Maintenance** - Following BMW's Condition Based Servicing (CBS) system
- **M Performance Service** - Specialized service for M vehicles including differential service, brake fluid flushes, and performance inspections
- **iPerformance Service** - High-voltage battery diagnostics, electric drivetrain service

## Service Experience

- **Text message updates** during service visits
- **Realistic time estimates** communicated upfront
- **Clean waiting area** with amenities
- **Follow-up calls** after service to ensure satisfaction

## BMW Value Service

BMW Value Service provides competitively priced maintenance for select BMW models outside of the included maintenance period. Services include oil service, microfilter replacement, brake service, and vehicle check.
