- **Price range:** Broad range from affordable to premium luxury

## BMW Certified Pre-Owned Program

The BMW Certified Pre-Owned program offers exceptional value with comprehensive coverage:

- **Rigorous Multi-Point Inspection** - 1,000+ point inspection by BMW-certified technicians
- **Extended Warranty** - Remaining new vehicle warranty plus 1-year/ unlimited-mileage extended warranty
- **24/7 Roadside Assistance** - Included with every CPO vehicle
- **BMW Financial Services APR Offers**:
  - 1.99% APR up to 72 months on 2022-2024 Certified iX models
  - 2.99% APR up to 72 months on 2022-2024 Certified i4, i5, i7 models
  - 4.99% APR up to 36 months on 2021-2024 Certified models
- **BMW Certified Loyalty Credit** - $500 for current or former BMW customers
- **Military and First Responder Credit** - $500 purchase credit for eligible customers
- **College Grad Offer** - $1,000 towards lease or finance of any CPO 2021-2024 model
- **No Payments for 3 Months** - Available on all 2021-2024 CPO models
