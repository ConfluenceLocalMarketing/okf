# Frequently Asked Questions - BMW of Cincinnati North

## Inventory

**Q: What new BMW models are available?**
A: The full BMW lineup including sedans (3 Series, 5 Series, 7 Series), SUVs (X1-X7, XM, iX), coupes (2 Series, 4 Series, 8 Series), convertibles (Z4, 4 Series, 8 Series), Gran Coupes, electric iPerformance (i4, i5, i7, iX), and M performance variants (M2, M3, M4, M5, M8, X4 M/X5 M/X6 M, XM). ALPINA models also available.

**Q: What electric BMW models are offered?**
A: The i4 hatchback, i5 sedan, i7 sedan, and iX SUV comprise the electric iPerformance lineup. Plug-in hybrid options include the 550e, 750e, and X5 xDrive50e.

**Q: Does BMW of Cincinnati North offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, BMW Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are available with rigorous multi-point inspection, extended warranty, 24/7 roadside assistance, and special APR financing.

**Q: Is the dealership a BMW M Certified Dealer?**
A: Yes, BMW of Cincinnati North is designated as a BMW M Certified Dealer center, authorized for M performance vehicle sales, service, and parts.

## Financing

**Q: What financing programs are available?**
A: BMW Financial Services offers traditional loans and leases. Special programs include BMW Loyalty Credit ($500), College Grad Offer ($1,000), Military and First Responder Credit ($500), and No Payments for 3 months on select CPO models.

**Q: What APR offers are available on Certified Pre-Owned models?**
A: As low as 1.99% APR for 2022-2024 Certified iX, 2.99% APR for 2022-2024 Certified i4/i5/i7, and 4.99% APR for 2021-2024 other Certified models.

**Q: Does the dealership offer BMW lease specials?**
A: Yes, current lease offers include 2026 X3 ($822/mo), X5 ($1,208/mo), 330i ($720/mo), 530i ($1,014/mo), and X1 ($735/mo) with loyalty discounts available.

## Service

**Q: Can the service center work on M performance vehicles?**
A: Yes, as a BMW M Certified Dealer, the service center has specialized M-Certified technicians for M performance vehicle service and repair.

**Q: Does the center service electric BMW vehicles?**
A: Yes, the service center is equipped for iPerformance electric vehicle service including high-voltage battery diagnostics and electric drivetrain repair.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through the dealership website. Text message updates are provided during service visits.

## Dealership

**Q: How long has BMW of Cincinnati North been in business?**
A: The dealership is part of the Jake Sweeney Company, family-owned and operated for over 100 years since 1917, now in its 4th generation.

**Q: What is the BMW Value Service program?**
A: BMW Value Service offers competitively priced maintenance for select out-of-warranty BMW models including oil service, brake service, microfilter replacement, and vehicle checks.

**Q: Does the dealership have genuine BMW parts?**
A: Yes, the parts department carries genuine Original BMW Parts, Original BMW Batteries, M Performance Parts, and BMW accessories.
