# New BMW Models - BMW of Cincinnati North

BMW of Cincinnati North carries a full lineup of new BMW vehicles, including the latest iPerformance electric models, M performance variants, and ALPINA models.

## Sedans

### 2 Series Gran Coupe
Entry-level BMW sedan. Available as **228i Gran Coupe** ($39,600) and **M235i Gran Coupe** ($50,000). Front-wheel drive based with xDrive available. Turbocharged 4-cylinder engines.

### 3 Series Sedan
The iconic BMW sports sedan. Available as **330i** ($47,500) and **M340i** ($61,700). Turbocharged 4-cylinder (330i) and 6-cylinder (M340i) with xDrive all-wheel drive available.

### 5 Series Sedan
Mid-size luxury sedan. Available as **530i** ($58,700), **540i** ($65,800), and **550e xDrive** plug-in hybrid ($75,500). Combines luxury, technology, and performance.

### 7 Series Sedan
Full-size flagship luxury sedan. Available as **740i** ($99,300), **750e xDrive** plug-in hybrid ($110,000), and **760i xDrive** V8 ($124,700). The pinnacle of BMW luxury.

### i5 Sedan
All-electric mid-size luxury sedan. Starting MSRP $67,100. Part of BMW's electric iPerformance lineup.

### i7 Sedan
All-electric full-size flagship sedan. Starting MSRP $105,700. Electric luxury at its finest.

### M3 Sedan
High-performance sports sedan. Starting MSRP $79,300. Turbocharged 6-cylinder with rear-wheel drive or M xDrive.

### M5 Sedan
Ultimate high-performance luxury sedan. Starting MSRP $119,500. V8 engine with M xDrive. Available as M5 Touring ($125,300).

Ultra-luxury high-performance full-size SUV. Starting MSRP $156,000. Hand-assembled by ALPINA with bespoke interior and enhanced V8.
