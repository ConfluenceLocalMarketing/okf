# Financing & Leasing

The BMW of Cincinnati North Finance Center offers purchase financing and lease programs for new and pre-owned BMW vehicles through BMW Financial Services.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start the financing process from home with the online credit application and pre-approval
- **BMW Financial Services**: Lease and finance options through BMW Financial Services NA, LLC
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions designed around different ownership goals and budgets
- **Special Programs**: BMW Loyalty Credit ($500 for returning customers on Certified Pre-Owned), BMW College Grad Program ($1,000 towards lease or finance of Certified Pre-Owned), Military and First Responder Credit ($500 purchase credit)

## Leasing

Leasing is available for customers who prefer lower monthly payments and the option to drive a new BMW every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own the vehicle at end of term | Return vehicle at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited by contract |
| Sales Tax | Paid upfront or rolled into loan | Included in monthly payment |

## Trade-In

Get an instant online valuation through the dealership's trade-in tool. In-person appraisal also available at the dealership.

Estimate monthly payments using the payment calculator before visiting the dealership.
