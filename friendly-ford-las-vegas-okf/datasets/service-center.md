# Service Center

The Friendly Ford Las Vegas Service Department provides factory-trained maintenance and repair services for Ford vehicles.

## Services Offered

- Routine maintenance: oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, preventative maintenance
- Brake service and repair
- Advanced diagnostics and repair: engine diagnostics, electrical service, transmission repairs
- Tire sales and service
- Genuine Ford OEM parts and accessories
- Mobile Service - on-site repairs at your home or office
- Ford Pickup & Delivery - vehicle pickup and return service
- FordPass Rewards integration
