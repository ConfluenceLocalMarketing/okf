# Frequently Asked Questions

## Vehicles & Inventory

**Q: What Ford models are available at Friendly Ford Las Vegas?**
A: The dealership carries the full Ford lineup including F-150, F-150 Lightning, Maverick, Ranger, Super Duty, Bronco, Bronco Sport, Explorer, Expedition, Expedition MAX, Mustang, Mustang Mach-E, E-Transit, Transit-250, and Transit-350.

**Q: Do you have used vehicles from other manufacturers?**
A: Yes. The pre-owned inventory spans multiple makes and models, not just Ford.

**Q: Can I see window stickers for used vehicles online?**
A: Yes. Transparent original window stickers are displayed on digital inventory pages.

**Q: How many vehicles do you have in inventory?**
A: Friendly Ford Las Vegas carries over 470 new Ford vehicles.

## Financing

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes. Complete the online credit application from the Friendly Ford website.

**Q: What credit situations do you work with?**
A: The finance team works with multiple lending partners to accommodate various credit profiles, including an ITIN program for qualified buyers.

**Q: Do you offer both buying and leasing options?**
A: Yes. The finance center provides both purchase financing and lease programs.

**Q: Can I value my trade-in online?**
A: Yes. Use the online trade-in valuation tool for instant online assessment.

**Q: Do you offer commercial credit?**
A: Yes. Commercial fleet and business credit options are available.

## Service

**Q: Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts?**
A: The service department uses only genuine Ford OEM parts for all repairs.

**Q: Do you offer mobile service?**
A: Yes. Mobile Service brings repairs to your home or office.

**Q: Can I schedule service online?**
A: Yes. Use the online service appointment form on the Friendly Ford website.

**Q: Do you offer Ford Pickup & Delivery?**
A: Yes. The dealership provides vehicle pickup and delivery service for service appointments.

**Q: Do you have a collision center?**
A: Yes. Friendly Ford operates a full collision center at 1650 N Decatur Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89107 for body repair and paint services.
