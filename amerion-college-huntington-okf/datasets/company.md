# Amerion College — Huntington

Amerion College Huntington is a career training school located in Huntington, West Virginia, offering focused diploma programs in healthcare fields. The campus provides hands-on training designed to prepare students for entry-level positions in the medical and dental fields.

## Location

- **Address**: 517 9th St, Huntington, WV 25701
- **Phone**: (304) 522-3244
- **Website**: https://amerion.edu/campuses/huntington-wv/

## Programs Offered

### Dental Assistant (DA)
- 12-week program
- Hands-on training in dental procedures, radiography, infection control
- Preparation for DANB and COA certifications

### Medical Assistant (MA)
- Classroom and practical training
- Clinical and administrative skills
- Externship placement

### Phlebotomy Technician (PT)
- Blood draw techniques and specimen handling
- Patient interaction and safety protocols

### EKG Technician
- Cardiac monitoring and basic interpretation
- ECG equipment operation

### Patient Care Technician (PCT)
- Patient care skills including vital signs, mobility assistance

### Clinical Medical Assistant (CMA)
- Expanded clinical skills beyond basic medical assisting

## Features

- Accelerated diploma programs (as short as 12 weeks)
- Hands-on lab training
- Externship opportunities
- Small class sizes
- Financial aid available for those who qualify
- Job placement assistance

## Admissions

- High school diploma or equivalent required
- Rolling enrollment throughout the year
- Campus tours available by appointment
