# Financing & Leasing

The Jake Sweeney Used Car Superstore Finance Center offers purchase financing, lease programs, and buy-here-pay-here options.

## Financing Options

- **Online Application**: Start from home
- **Multiple Lending Partners**: Competitive rates for all credit profiles
- **Buy Here Pay Here**: In-house financing with flexible terms
- **Flexible Terms**: Loan and lease solutions

## Leasing

Lower monthly payments, drive a newer vehicle every few years.

## Buying vs. Leasing Table

| Factor | Buying | Leasing |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Own at end of term | Return at end of term |
| Mileage | Unlimited | Limited |
| Sales Tax | Upfront or in loan | Included in payment |
