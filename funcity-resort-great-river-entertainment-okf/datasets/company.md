# Great River Casino Resort and FunCity

**Address:** 3001 Winegard Dr, Burlington, IA 52601
**Phone:** (319) 753-2946
**Website:** https://www.funcityresort.com/

Great River Casino Resort and FunCity is a premier entertainment destination in Burlington, Iowa. The property is part of the Elite Casino Resorts family, which includes Catfish Bend Casino, FunCity Resort Hotel, Great River Hotel, and Hampton Inn.

## Overview

The resort offers a comprehensive entertainment experience combining casino gaming, multiple hotel properties, a family fun center, outdoor water park, golf course, spa, and event center — making it a destination for both adult gaming and family recreation.

## Casino

The casino floor features a wide selection of gaming options open daily from 10 AM to 2 AM.

- **Slots:** Hundreds of slot machines ranging from classic reels to modern video slots
- **Table Games:** Blackjack, roulette, craps, and more
- **Poker:** Dedicated poker room with regular tournaments and cash games
- **Sportsbook:** Full-service sports betting

## Hotels

Three hotel properties on campus:

- **Hampton Inn** — Branded hotel accommodation
