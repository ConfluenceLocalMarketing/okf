# Gibson Truck World

Gibson Truck World is the largest used truck dealership in Florida, located on a 14-acre site in Sanford, FL. Founded in 1995 by Yves Belanger, the dealership has grown from 3 employees to over 65 team members and 150 trucks in inventory, selling approximately 150 vehicles per month.
