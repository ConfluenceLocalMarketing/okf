# Gibson Truck World Service Center

Gibson Truck World operates a full-service maintenance and repair center complemented by GTW Customs, a custom truck modification shop.

## Maintenance & Repair

- Oil changes for gas and diesel engines
- Brake service including heavy-duty truck brakes
- Tire rotations, balancing, and replacement
- Engine diagnostics and repair
- Transmission service
- HVAC system service
- Suspension and steering repair
- Electrical diagnostics

## GTW Customs — Lift Kit Installation

- Custom 4x4 lift kit installation
- Aftermarket wheel and tire packages
- Suspension upgrades (leveling kits, coilovers)
- Power step and running board installation
- Custom bumpers and lighting
- Bed liners and bed covers

## Parts & Accessories

- Aftermarket wheels and tires
- Lift kit components
- Truck accessories (tonneau covers, nerf bars)
- Performance parts
- Special order capabilities
