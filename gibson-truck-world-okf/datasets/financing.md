# Gibson Truck World Finance Center

Gibson Truck World provides financing solutions for truck buyers across the country. The finance team works with multiple lenders to serve all credit situations.

## Financing Options

### Truck Loans
- Competitive rates on pre-owned trucks, SUVs, and cars
- Flexible terms to fit budgets
- Nationwide financing available

### All-Credit Programs
- **Excellent Credit** — Best rates and terms
- **Good Credit** — Competitive standard financing
- **Fair Credit** — Flexible approval with reasonable terms
- **Bad Credit / No Credit** — Special programs to help get approved

### Commercial Financing
- Business vehicle loans for work trucks
- Fleet purchase options
- Commercial account terms available

## Buying vs Leasing

| Aspect | Buying | Leasing |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Monthly Payment | Higher | Lower |
| Ownership | Full ownership | Return at lease end |
| Mileage Limits | None | Restricted |
| Customization | Allowed | Limited |
| Trade-In | Any time | At lease end only |

## Trade-In Program

- Trade any vehicle toward your purchase
- Online trade-in valuation tool
- We buy trucks (2018+, under 80k miles) even without a purchase

Online payment calculator on the Gibson Truck World website for estimating monthly payments.
