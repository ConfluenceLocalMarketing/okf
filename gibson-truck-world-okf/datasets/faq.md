# Gibson Truck World FAQ

## Vehicles

**Q: What types of trucks do you sell?**
A: We carry pre-owned trucks across Ford, RAM, Chevrolet, GMC, Nissan, Toyota, and more — including lifted and custom builds. See [used-vehicles.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/used-vehicles.md).

**Q: Do you sell cars and SUVs?**
A: Yes, alongside our massive truck inventory we offer a multi-make selection of pre-owned cars and SUVs.

**Q: What is GTW Customs?**
A: GTW Customs is our in-house custom shop specializing in lifted trucks, aftermarket wheels and tires, suspension upgrades, power steps, and accessories.

**Q: What warranty comes with a vehicle?**
A: Every vehicle includes a one-year bumper-to-bumper warranty. See [used-vehicles.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/used-vehicles.md).

**Q: Do you offer a test drive?**
A: Yes, test drives are available on all vehicles at our Sanford location.

## Financing

**Q: Can I get financing with bad credit?**
A: Yes, we offer nationwide financing options for all credit situations. See [financing.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/financing.md).

**Q: Do you offer online pre-approval?**
A: Yes, apply online through our website for fast pre-approval.

**Q: Can I finance a lifted truck?**
A: Yes, GTW Customs builds and lifted trucks qualify for financing just like standard vehicles.

## Service

**Q: What services does your center provide?**
A: Full maintenance and repair, custom lift kit installation, aftermarket parts, and accessories. See [service-center.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/service-center.md).

**Q: Do you service non-Ford trucks?**
A: Yes, our certified technicians service all makes and models.

**Q: Can I have a lift kit installed on my existing truck?**
A: Yes, GTW Customs can install lift kits and accessories on your current truck.

## General

**Q: Where is Gibson Truck World?**
A: 3455 S Orlando Dr, Sanford, FL 32773. See [company.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/company.md).

**Q: What are your hours?**
A: Mon–Fri 9AM–7PM, Sat 9AM–6PM, Sun 10AM–5PM.

**Q: Do you buy used trucks?**
A: Yes, we buy trucks from 2018 and newer with under 80,000 miles. See [company.md](gibson-truck-world-okf/datasets/company.md).

**Q: Can you deliver a truck nationwide?**
A: Yes, we offer nationwide delivery on purchased vehicles.
