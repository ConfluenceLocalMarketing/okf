# Gibson Truck World — OKF Bundle

This bundle documents Gibson Truck World, the largest used truck dealership in Florida.

- [faq.md](datasets/faq.md) — Frequently asked questions about trucks, financing, service, and dealership operations.

- [staff.md](datasets/staff.md) — Truck sales, GTW Customs, service, and management in Sanford, FL.
