# Frequently Asked Questions - West Hills Auto Plex

## Group

**Q: What is the West Hills Auto Plex?**
A: A multi-franchise automotive group in Bremerton, WA operating five brand-specific dealerships under the Haselwood Auto Group: West Hills Honda, West Hills Chrysler Jeep Dodge RAM, West Hills Ford, West Hills Kia, and West Hills Mazda.

**Q: Where is the West Hills Auto Plex located?**
A: Dealerships are located along West Hills Boulevard and Oyster Bay Avenue South in Bremerton, WA 98312, just off Route 3.

**Q: Who owns the West Hills Auto Plex?**
A: The group is owned and operated by Rick Wiler and the Haselwood Auto Group, continuing the legacy founded by Chuck Haselwood.

**Q: What is the Haselwood Auto Group?**
A: The parent organization that operates the West Hills-branded dealerships plus Haselwood Chevrolet GMC, Haselwood Hyundai, Haselwood Volkswagen of Bremerton, and Heartland Toyota.

## Inventory

**Q: What new vehicle brands are available?**
A: Honda, Ford, Kia, Mazda, Chrysler, Jeep, Dodge, and RAM across five branded dealerships.

**Q: Do you sell used vehicles?**
A: Yes, all member dealerships carry pre-owned and certified pre-owned inventory spanning multiple makes and models.

**Q: What is the West Hills Pre-Owned warranty?**
A: A 3-Month or 3,000-Mile Customer Confidence Warranty on West Hills pre-owned vehicles.

**Q: Do you offer certified pre-owned vehicles?**
A: Yes, each franchise offers manufacturer-certified pre-owned programs with factory-backed inspections and warranties.

## Financing

**Q: What financing options are available?**
A: Traditional loans, leases, and special financing programs through a network of nationally recognized lenders.

**Q: Does the group work with all credit profiles?**
A: Yes, the finance teams work with multiple lenders to accommodate various credit situations and budgets.

**Q: Can I apply for financing online?**
A: Yes, online credit applications are available for all member dealerships.

**Q: How do I value my trade-in?**
A: KBB (Kelley Blue Book) trade-in valuation is available online through all member dealership websites.

## Service

**Q: Can I service a vehicle at any West Hills dealership?**
A: Service centers are brand-specific. Take your Honda to West Hills Honda, your Ford to West Hills Ford, etc. Most service centers can also work on other makes.

**Q: How do I schedule service?**
A: Online scheduling is available through each dealership's website.

**Q: Do you offer home delivery for service?**
A: Yes, maintenance pick-up and delivery is available in the local area.

**Q: Do you have service coupons?**
A: Yes, each dealership offers service specials and coupons on their websites.

## Dealership Amenities

**Q: Do you offer online purchasing?**
A: Yes, Click-N-Drive online purchasing with home delivery is available across the group.

**Q: Is there a rental car available during service?**
A: Yes, some locations offer on-site rental car agencies and loaner vehicles.

**Q: Do you serve military customers?**
A: Yes, the group has dedicated military appreciation programs given its proximity to Naval Base Kitsap.
