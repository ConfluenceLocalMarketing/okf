# Tuscaloosa Chevrolet — FAQ

## General

### What type of dealership is Tuscaloosa Chevrolet?
It is the largest Chevrolet dealer in Central Alabama, located at 6500 Interstate Pkwy, Cottondale, AL, offering new and pre-owned Chevy vehicles, certified service, and commercial fleet sales. See [company.md](https://github.com/ConfluenceLocalMarketing/okf/blob/main/tuscaloosa-chevrolet-okf/datasets/company.md) for the full profile.

### What area does the dealership serve?
Cottondale, Tuscaloosa, Hoover, Bessemer, Birmingham, AL and Columbus, MS.

### What are the dealership hours?
Sales: Mon–Sat 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM (Sat until 6:00 PM), closed Sunday. Service and Parts: Mon–Fri 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM, closed weekends.

### Is the dealership BBB accredited?
Yes, the dealership has been BBB accredited since 1985 with an A+ rating.

## Sales

### What new Chevrolet models are available?
The full Chevrolet lineup including Silverado 1500 and HD trucks, Colorado, Tahoe, Suburban, Traverse, Equinox, Blazer, Trax, Corvette, and EV models including Silverado EV, Equinox EV, Blazer EV, and Bolt EV.

### Do you offer the Chevrolet Silverado EV?
Yes, the Silverado EV is available alongside the full Silverado lineup including 2500/3500 HD and chassis cab models.

### Do you sell commercial vehicles?
Yes, the dealership offers commercial fleet sales including BrightDrop, Express vans, Silverado Chassis Cab (4500/5500/6500 HD), and Low Cab Forward.

### Do you offer the Corvette?
Yes, including Corvette, Corvette E-Ray, Corvette ZR1, and Corvette Z06.

## Service

### What makes your service center unique?
Tuscaloosa Chevrolet is the only Certified Service Elite GM repair facility in Alabama, staffed by factory-trained technicians.

### What services are offered?
Routine maintenance, major repairs, OEM parts, electric vehicle charging stations, and GM-certified diagnostics.

### Are the technicians GM-certified?
Yes, all service center technicians are factory-trained and GM-certified.

## Financing

### What financing options are available?
Loan and lease options, online credit application, trade-in appraisal, and commercial fleet financing.

### Can I value my trade-in online?
Yes, an online trade-in value tool is available on the dealership website.

## Parts

### Do you sell OEM parts?
Yes, genuine GM and ACDelco OEM parts are available through the parts department.

### What are the parts department hours?
Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, closed weekends.
