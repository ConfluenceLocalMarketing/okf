# BDC Door Company

BDC Door Company is a manufacturer and supplier of commercial and industrial door systems, providing rolling steel doors, fire-rated doors, sectional overhead doors, dock equipment, and custom-engineered door solutions. The company serves contractors, architects, and facility managers across the United States.

## Overview

BDC Door Company specializes in the design, fabrication, and installation of commercial and industrial door products. Their product line covers a wide range of applications from warehouse loading docks to fire-rated openings in commercial buildings.

## Products

### Rolling Steel Doors
- Standard rolling steel service doors
- Insulated rolling steel doors
- Rolling steel fire doors (UL-labeled)
- Rolling steel grilles and counter shutters
- Heavy-duty industrial rolling doors

### Sectional Overhead Doors
- Standard commercial sectional doors
- Insulated sectional doors
- Heavy-duty industrial doors

### Fire-Rated Doors
- UL-labeled rolling steel fire doors
- Fire-rated counter shutters
- Fire doors for commercial openings

### Dock Equipment
- Dock levelers (hydraulic and mechanical)
- Dock bumpers and seals
- Vehicle restraints
- Dock lights and accessories

### Custom Solutions
- Custom-size rolling doors
- Special finish and coating options
- Motorized and manual operation
- Security grilles for retail and commercial applications

## Services

- **Design and Engineering**: Custom door system design for specific project requirements
- **Installation**: Professional installation by factory-trained technicians
- **Maintenance and Repair**: Ongoing service and emergency repair
- **Code Compliance**: Assistance with fire code and building code requirements
